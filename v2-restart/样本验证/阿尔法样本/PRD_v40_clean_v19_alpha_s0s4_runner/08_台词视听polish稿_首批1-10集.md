## EPISODE 1

**INT. HALE MANOR - EAST WING SALON - NIGHT**

Chandeliers. Silk. A line of polished girls holding numbered cards.

HELENA HALE stands in front of them like a judge.

HELENA  
Tonight ends this.  
You choose a wife, the trust gets its heir, and the board shuts up.

JULIAN HALE stands half in shadow, collar open, hand shaking once before he buries it in a fist.

JULIAN  
Send them out.

HELENA  
Not tonight.

In the service corridor, MIA BROOK hurries with a sealed document pouch against her chest. Academy blazer. Cheap shoes. Entirely in the wrong wing.

A FOOTMAN passes her.

FOOTMAN  
Last door. Drop it and go.

Mia opens the wrong door.

**INT. JULIAN'S PRIVATE SUITE - CONTINUOUS**

Dark. One lamp. Torn medicine packs. An overturned glass.

Julian turns.

JULIAN  
Late.

MIA  
I'm sorry, I think I--

He reaches her before the sentence lands. The pouch drops. Papers scatter.

MIA  
Please. I don't belong here.

JULIAN  
Neither do any of them.

He pins her against the door. Her shoulder hits wood.

MIA  
Let me go--

JULIAN  
Stay.

Hands. Breath. The lamp swings. Black.

**EXT. HALE MANOR - FRONT DRIVE - LATER**

Mia stumbles into the freezing night, blazer torn, both hands clutching it closed.

On the balcony above, a WOMAN watches.

Mia runs to a waiting town car.

BALCONY WOMAN  
(low)  
Wrong girl.

The car disappears into the dark.

## EPISODE 2

**INT. ST. DORIAN ACADEMY CLINIC - DAY**

A result sheet slides across the desk.

SCHOOL DOCTOR  
You're pregnant.

Mia stares, then presses a hand over her stomach.

SCHOOL DOCTOR  
If you don't keep it, you need money fast.

MIA  
How much?

SCHOOL DOCTOR  
Ten thousand. Minimum.

Mia folds the page with shaking hands.

**EXT. ST. DORIAN ACADEMY GATE - DAY**

A black car door opens. Julian steps out in trustee black.

Students stop to watch.

JULIAN  
I've been looking for you.

MIA  
Then give me ten thousand dollars and leave me alone.

His face shuts at once.

JULIAN  
So that's the price.

MIA  
No. That's survival.

JULIAN  
You put a number on one night.

Mia slaps him.

MIA  
You bastard.

She runs.

Julian stands still for one beat, then grabs his phone.

JULIAN  
Mother. That girl. Was she on your list?

HELENA (V.O.)  
What girl?

His eyes sharpen.

## EPISODE 3

**EXT. ALLEY BESIDE ST. DORIAN - LATE AFTERNOON**

KARINA LORDE yanks Mia backward by the hair.

KARINA  
You slap him, then hide?

GIRL #1  
Strip her. Film it.

MIA  
I did nothing to you--

Karina slaps her. Then again.

A hand catches Karina's wrist.

Julian.

He sees the red mark on Mia's cheek and nothing else.

JULIAN  
Who touched her?

KARINA  
We were joking--

He takes Mia's hand and puts her in front of Karina.

JULIAN  
Hit her.

MIA  
What?

JULIAN  
Hit her back.

Mia freezes. Julian covers her hand with his and drives it across Karina's face.

CRACK.

JULIAN  
Touch her again, I bury your family.

He turns to Mia.

JULIAN  
Stay here.

He heads for the corner pharmacy.

Mia watches him go, shaken.

REG BROOK steps from behind a van.

REG  
Found you.

MIA  
Dad--

His hand drops on the back of her neck.

She crumples. Her backpack hits the bench.

Julian returns seconds later with a paper bag.

The bench is empty.

## EPISODE 4

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - PRIVATE DEBT CLUB - NIGHT**

Mia wakes tied to a velvet gaming table.

Her school clothes are gone. A thin satin costume has been strapped onto her body.

Reg counts cash in both hands.

VAUGHN studies her slowly.

MIA  
Dad.

REG  
Your debt's cleared. Mine too.

MIA  
You sold me?

REG  
Don't make it ugly.

Vaughn climbs the steps to the table.

VAUGHN  
Behave and this goes easy.

Mia twists against the restraints.

MIA  
Please. Please don't.

**EXT. CITY STREETS - NIGHT**

Julian's car tears through a red light.

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - CONTINUOUS**

The men close in.

Vaughn lifts a riding crop.

## EPISODE 5

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - CONTINUOUS**

The crop cracks across Mia's stomach.

She folds around the pain.

MIA  
I'm pregnant!

A beat. Then laughter.

MAN  
Then don't hit the face.

The next strike lands lower. Blood runs down her leg.

MIA  
My baby--

Hands close around her. The room closes in.

**EXT. ALLEY / MOVING - NIGHT**

Julian kneels by the bench, fingers on Mia's dropped backpack. He finds the clinic result.

His head snaps up.

Then he runs.

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - NIGHT**

Vaughn reaches for Mia's chin.

The front door EXPLODES inward.

Julian stands in the doorway, backlit, lethal.

## EPISODE 6

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - CONTINUOUS**

Julian smashes Vaughn face-first into the table edge.

Blood. Teeth. Screaming.

Another man lunges. Julian catches him by the throat and hurls him into the mirrored wall.

Glass rains.

JULIAN  
Touch her again.

No one moves.

Mia is crying so hard she can barely breathe.

MIA  
Julian...

He sees the blood beneath her. The color leaves his face.

He tears the restraints free, wraps her in his coat, and lifts her into his arms.

VAUGHN  
Please--

Julian kicks him down without looking.

JULIAN  
Stay awake.  
Stay with me.

Mia curls into him, one hand pressed weakly to her stomach.

**INT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - EMERGENCY HALL - LATER**

Orderlies rush Mia through double doors.

Julian stops only when the doors slam shut between them.

## EPISODE 7

**INT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - PRIVATE ROOM - NIGHT**

Monitors hum.

Mia lies pale in bed. Julian stands beside her, motionless.

DR. KELLER checks the chart.

DR. KELLER  
The baby survived.

Julian closes his eyes once.

Helena enters fast, then softens when she sees Mia.

HELENA  
Good. Then she lives too.

Mia wakes with a gasp, hand flying to her stomach.

MIA  
The baby--

JULIAN  
Still here.

Tears spill from her eyes.

Keller watches them and understands too much.

DR. KELLER  
She needs observation. No stress. No mistakes.

Helena takes Mia's hand.

HELENA  
You are under Hale protection now.

Keller turns away, face smooth.

DR. KELLER  
I'll prepare the transfer papers.

His eyes harden once he's out of their sight.

## EPISODE 8

**INT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - LATER**

Keller returns with forms already signed.

DR. KELLER  
Her abdominal trauma is too unstable for this unit.  
We move her to the maternal center. Tonight.

Julian looks at Mia. She's half-awake, barely tracking the room.

HELENA  
If it keeps the baby breathing, we move her.

Julian nods, hating it.

The bed unlocks and begins to roll.

Keller hangs his coat over the side rail.

Under the cuff, a dark crest flashes at his wrist.

Julian sees it. Not enough to name. Enough to feel wrong.

Mia's hand slips from the blanket edge as the bed rolls away.

## EPISODE 9

**INT. HOSPITAL SERVICE CORRIDOR - NIGHT**

The bed wheels rattle over tile.

Keller pushes. No orderly now. Just him.

Mia drifts in and out, fingers twitching once over her stomach.

At the ambulance bay doors, rain spits across the concrete.

Keller leans near her ear.

DR. KELLER  
You and that baby were never meant to make it this far.

He straightens before anyone can hear.

Behind him, Julian stands in the corridor, staring at the crest in his memory.

He has seen it before. Somewhere hot. Somewhere bloody.

The doors close between them.

Julian's face changes.

## EPISODE 10

**EXT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - AMBULANCE BAY - NIGHT**

The ambulance doors slam.

Inside, Mia lies strapped to the gurney, barely conscious, one hand hanging over the side.

Keller climbs in.

**INT. HOSPITAL CORRIDOR - SAME**

The crest clicks into place in Julian's mind.

JULIAN  
Keller.

He runs.

**EXT. AMBULANCE BAY - CONTINUOUS**

The ambulance pulls out hard into the rain.

Julian bursts through the doors just in time to see the taillights swing into the street.

His eyes burn red.

JULIAN  
Mia.

He takes one step after the van, then another, then breaks into a dead sprint after the disappearing lights.
