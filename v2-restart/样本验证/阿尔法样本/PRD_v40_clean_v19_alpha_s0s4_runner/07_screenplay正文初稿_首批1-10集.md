## EPISODE 1

**INT. HALE MANOR - EAST WING SALON - NIGHT**

Chandeliers. Silk. A line of polished girls holding numbered cards.

HELENA HALE stands in front of them like a judge.

HELENA  
Tonight ends this circus.  
Julian chooses a wife. The trust gets its heir. The board stops circling.

At the far end of the room, JULIAN HALE stands half in shadow, collar open, jaw tight, hand shaking once before he crushes it into a fist.

JULIAN  
Send them home.

HELENA  
Not tonight.

In the service corridor outside, MIA BROOK hurries with a sealed document pouch tucked to her chest. Academy blazer. Cheap shoes. Nineteen steps too far into the wrong wing.

A FOOTMAN sweeps by.

FOOTMAN  
Last door. Leave it and go.

Mia opens the wrong door.

**INT. JULIAN'S PRIVATE SUITE - CONTINUOUS**

Dark. One lamp. A glass overturned. Medicine blister packs torn open.

Julian turns.

JULIAN  
You're late.

MIA  
I'm sorry, I think I made a--

He reaches her before the sentence lands. The pouch drops. Papers fan across the floor.

MIA  
Please. I don't belong here.

JULIAN  
Neither do any of them.

He backs her into the door. Her shoulder hits wood. She shoves him hard. It barely moves him.

Hands. Breath. The lamp swings. Her fingers claw at polished fabric, then air.

MIA  
Let me go--

JULIAN  
Stay.

The room goes black.

**EXT. HALE MANOR - FRONT DRIVE - LATER**

Mia stumbles out into the freezing night, blazer torn, eyes wet, clutching the front closed with both hands.

High above, a WOMAN stands on the balcony, watching.

Mia runs to a waiting town car and slams the door.

BALCONY WOMAN  
(low)  
Wrong girl.

The car pulls away into the dark.

## EPISODE 2

**INT. ST. DORIAN ACADEMY CLINIC - DAY**

A result sheet slides across the desk.

The SCHOOL DOCTOR keeps her voice low.

SCHOOL DOCTOR  
You're pregnant.

Mia stares, then presses a hand over her stomach like she can push the word back in.

SCHOOL DOCTOR  
If you don't intend to keep it, you need money. Fast.

MIA  
How much?

SCHOOL DOCTOR  
Ten thousand, minimum.

Mia folds the paper with shaking hands.

**EXT. ST. DORIAN ACADEMY GATE - DAY**

A black car door opens. Julian steps out in trustee black.

Students turn. Whisper.

Mia freezes.

JULIAN  
I've been looking for you.

MIA  
Then give me ten thousand dollars and leave me alone.

His face shuts at once.

JULIAN  
So that's the price.

MIA  
No. That's survival.

JULIAN  
You took one night and put a number on it.

Mia slaps him.

The courtyard goes silent.

MIA  
You bastard.

She runs.

Julian stands there with her handprint rising on his face. Then he grabs his phone.

JULIAN  
Mother. That girl. Was she on your list?

HELENA (V.O.)  
What girl?

His eyes sharpen.

## EPISODE 3

**EXT. ALLEY BESIDE ST. DORIAN - LATE AFTERNOON**

KARINA LORDE yanks Mia backward by the hair.

KARINA  
You slap him, then hide?

Two girls laugh behind her.

GIRL #1  
Strip her. Film it.

MIA  
I did nothing to you--

Karina slaps her. Then again.

KARINA  
Liar.

A hand catches Karina's wrist.

Julian.

He looks at the red mark on Mia's cheek before he looks at anyone else.

JULIAN  
Who touched her?

KARINA  
We were joking--

He takes Mia's hand and places her in front of Karina.

JULIAN  
Hit her back.

MIA  
What?

JULIAN  
Hit her.

Mia doesn't move. Julian closes his hand over hers and drives it across Karina's face.

CRACK.

KARINA  
Julian--

JULIAN  
Anyone touches her again, I bury your family.

Karina steps back, stunned.

Julian turns to Mia.

JULIAN  
Stay here.

He heads toward the corner pharmacy.

Mia watches him go, confused, shaken.

REG BROOK steps from behind a van.

REG  
Found you.

MIA  
Dad--

His hand comes down on the back of her neck.

She drops. Her backpack hits the bench.

Seconds later, Julian returns with a paper pharmacy bag.

The bench is empty.

## EPISODE 4

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - PRIVATE DEBT CLUB - NIGHT**

Mia wakes tied to a velvet gaming table under low amber light.

Her school clothes are gone. A thin satin costume has been strapped onto her body.

REG counts cash in both hands.

VAUGHN, heavy and damp with appetite, looks her over slowly.

MIA  
Dad.

Reg doesn't meet her eyes.

REG  
Your debt's cleared. Mine too.

MIA  
You sold me?

REG  
Don't make it ugly.

Vaughn climbs the steps to the table.

VAUGHN  
Pretty thing. Behave and this goes easy.

Mia twists against the restraints until the rope cuts skin.

MIA  
Please. Please don't.

Vaughn lifts a riding crop and lets it tap once against her knee.

**EXT. CITY STREETS - NIGHT**

Julian's car blows through a red light.

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - CONTINUOUS**

The men gather closer.

Vaughn smiles and raises the crop.

## EPISODE 5

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - CONTINUOUS**

The crop cracks across Mia's stomach.

She folds around the pain with a scream.

MIA  
I'm pregnant!

A beat. Then a man laughs.

MAN  
Then don't hit the face.

The next strike lands lower. Blood runs down Mia's leg.

Her hands fly to her belly.

MIA  
My baby--

The room closes around her. Hands. Breath. Laughter.

Cut to:

**EXT. ALLEY / MOVING - NIGHT**

Julian kneels by the bench, fingers on Mia's dropped backpack. He finds the clinic result sheet tucked inside.

His head snaps up.

Then he runs.

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - NIGHT**

Vaughn reaches for Mia's chin.

The front door EXPLODES inward.

Everyone freezes.

Julian stands in the doorway, lit from behind, murder in his face.

## EPISODE 6

**INT. THE STAG ROOM - CONTINUOUS**

Julian crosses the room in three strides and smashes Vaughn face-first into the table edge.

Blood, teeth, screaming.

Another man grabs for him. Julian catches him by the throat and hurls him into the mirrored wall.

Glass rains everywhere.

JULIAN  
Touch her again.

No one moves.

Mia is crying too hard to breathe.

MIA  
Julian...

He sees the blood beneath her. The color leaves his face.

He rips the restraints free, shrugs off his coat, wraps it around her, and lifts her into his arms.

VAUGHN  
Please--

Julian kicks him back down without looking.

JULIAN  
Stay awake.  
Stay with me.

Mia curls against his chest, one hand pressed weakly to her stomach.

**INT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - EMERGENCY HALL - LATER**

Orderlies rush Mia through double doors.

Julian stops only when the doors slam shut between them.

## EPISODE 7

**INT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - PRIVATE ROOM - NIGHT**

Monitors hum.

Mia lies pale in bed. Julian stands beside her like moving would break something.

DR. KELLER checks the chart.

DR. KELLER  
The baby survived.

Julian closes his eyes once.

HELENA enters fast, all control and cold metal, then softens when she sees Mia.

HELENA  
Good. Then she stays alive too.

Mia wakes with a gasp, hand flying to her stomach.

MIA  
The baby--

JULIAN  
Still here.

Mia starts to cry.

Keller looks from her to Julian and understands too much.

DR. KELLER  
She needs observation. No stress. No mistakes.

Helena takes Mia's hand.

HELENA  
You are under Hale protection now.

Keller turns away, face smooth.

DR. KELLER  
I'll prepare the transfer papers.

His eyes harden just out of their sight.

## EPISODE 8

**INT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - LATER**

Keller returns with forms already signed.

DR. KELLER  
Her abdominal trauma is too unstable for this unit.  
We move her to the maternal center. Tonight.

Julian looks at Mia. She's half-awake, exhausted, trying to understand through pain.

HELENA  
If it keeps the baby breathing, we move her.

Julian nods once. He hates it.

Orderlies unlock the bed and begin to wheel Mia out.

Keller removes his coat and hangs it over the side rail.

Under the cuff, a dark crest flashes at his wrist.

Julian sees it. Not enough to name it. Enough to feel wrong.

Mia's hand slips from the blanket edge as the bed rolls away.

## EPISODE 9

**INT. HOSPITAL SERVICE CORRIDOR - NIGHT**

The bed wheels rattle over tile.

Keller pushes. No orderly now. Just him.

Mia drifts in and out, lips dry, fingers twitching once over her stomach.

At the ambulance bay doors, rain spits across the concrete.

Keller leans close to her ear.

DR. KELLER  
You and that baby were never meant to make it this far.

He straightens before anyone can hear.

Behind him, Julian stands in the corridor, staring at the crest in his memory.

He has seen it before. Somewhere hot. Somewhere bloody.

The doors slide shut between them.

Julian's face changes.

## EPISODE 10

**EXT. SAINT AURELIA PRIVATE HOSPITAL - AMBULANCE BAY - NIGHT**

The ambulance doors slam.

Inside, Mia lies strapped to the gurney, barely conscious, one hand hanging over the side.

Keller climbs in.

**INT. HOSPITAL CORRIDOR - SAME**

Julian sees it clearly now in his mind: the same crest on the man who stood behind the men waiting to gut the Hale bloodline.

JULIAN  
Keller.

He runs.

**EXT. AMBULANCE BAY - CONTINUOUS**

The ambulance pulls out hard into the rain.

Julian bursts through the doors just in time to watch the taillights swing into the street.

His eyes burn red.

JULIAN  
Mia.

He takes one step after the van, then another, then breaks into a dead sprint after the disappearing lights.
