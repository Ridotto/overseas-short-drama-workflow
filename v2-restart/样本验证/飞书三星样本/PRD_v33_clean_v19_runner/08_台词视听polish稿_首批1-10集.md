## EPISODE 1

**INT. ASHFORD ESTATE KITCHEN - NIGHT**

SERENA VALE slides an encrypted notebook into a hidden slot behind the spice rack. In her blazer pocket: a folded sonogram. Two heartbeats.

She sets Adrian's dinner on the island.

The doors burst open.

ADRIAN ASHFORD walks in with CHLOE MERCER under his arm, his lawyer's envelope landing flat on the marble.

ADRIAN  
Stop cooking.

SERENA  
You're home early.

ADRIAN  
Sign. Chloe's carrying my heir.

Serena looks at Chloe's hand on Adrian's chest.

SERENA  
So this is how you tell me.

CHLOE lets her heel slip and clutches Adrian.

CHLOE  
Adrian --

Adrian turns and SLAPS Serena.

ADRIAN  
Don't go near her.

Blood shines at Serena's lip. She pulls the sonogram from her pocket, crushes it in her fist, shoves it into her mouth, and swallows it down with water.

CHLOE  
What did you just do?

SERENA  
Buried something you don't deserve.

She signs the divorce papers.

SERENA  
Train your heir not to choke on stolen things.

She walks out with one suitcase.

UPSTAIRS BALCONY - Chloe watches her go, smile gone.

CHLOE  
(low)  
You won't reach the airport.

## EPISODE 2

**EXT. COASTAL HIGHWAY - NIGHT**

Serena rides alone in the back of a town car, passport and boarding pass in her lap.

A truck blasts through the side mirror and slams the car broadside. The sedan flips, hits the barrier, and lands hard.

Gasoline drips. Smoke curls.

SERENA  
Help! Open the door!

Across the divider, Chloe's SUV has also crashed.

Adrian's convoy skids in. Security races toward Serena's car.

SECURITY CHIEF  
Mr. Ashford! Ms. Vale is trapped!

ADRIAN  
Get Chloe out.

He smashes the SUV window and drags Chloe out first.

Serena pounds the glass.

SERENA  
Adrian! I'm carrying your twins!

Adrian looks at her through smoke and broken light.

ADRIAN  
You wanted her scared. Stay and watch what that buys you.

CHLOE  
Please take me away.

ADRIAN  
Move!

The convoy leaves.

Serena watches his taillights vanish. Fire catches the fuel line.

SERENA  
(to herself)  
You chose.

The car explodes.

## EPISODE 3

**INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - NIGHT**

Serena wakes hard. Bandages. Burn wraps. Her hand flies to her stomach.

DOCTOR LEWIS is already wearing the answer.

DOCTOR LEWIS  
You made it. The babies didn't.

A television murmurs in the room.

ON TV - Adrian raises a glass beside Chloe under a gold sign: *ASHFORD HEIR FOUNDATION WING*.

ADRIAN  
Tonight, we celebrate the future of the Ashford family.

The door opens. VICTOR SOKOLOV walks in with an old file box.

VICTOR  
If you break now, they get to kill you twice.

He opens the file across Serena's bed: route maps, acquisition trails, Raven signatures.

VICTOR  
You were never a wife. You were a weapon.

SERENA  
That woman burned in the car.

VICTOR  
Then come back colder.

He places a silver pen in her hand.

SERENA  
No.  
Worse.

FIVE YEARS LATER - Auction hall doors open. Heels hit marble.

## EPISODE 4

**INT. REDWATER MARITIME ASSET AUCTION - NIGHT**

The auctioneer points to a scale model of REDWATER PORT.

AUCTIONEER  
Ninety-five million. Going once --

ADRIAN lifts his paddle.

ADRIAN  
Ninety-eight.

A woman's voice slices in from the back.

SERENA (O.S.)  
One hundred and twenty.

The room turns.

Serena steps into the light in black, flanked by quiet security. Chloe rises too fast.

CHLOE  
You came back to beg?

Serena crosses the floor and SLAPS her flat into a chair.

SERENA  
I came back to buy your oxygen.

Adrian starts to signal security.

Serena throws him a leather folder.

SERENA  
Read first.

Inside: revoked access, frozen berth rights, rerouted cold-chain lanes, insurance locks. Ashford's emergency freight now runs through Raven-controlled entities.

SERENA  
Your winter freight, your bonded storage, your priority berths -- all mine.  
Raise the paddle again and explain to your board why your empire stopped breathing on camera.

Adrian looks up at her, rattled.

ADRIAN  
Serena.

SERENA  
Now you remember.

The hammer drops.

AUCTIONEER  
Sold. Raven Capital.

## EPISODE 5

**INT. SERENA'S PENTHOUSE SUITE - NIGHT**

The doors burst open.

Chloe storms in with guards behind her.

CHLOE  
You froze Adrian's ports. You touched Ashford property.

Serena sits in silence, wine untouched.

SERENA  
You're standing in mine.

Chloe charges.

A hidden panel slides open. Serena's security team floods the room and folds Chloe's men to the floor in seconds.

A wall screen lights up:

ASHFORD WEST COAST HOLD: SUSPENDED.  
EUROPE COLD-CHAIN: TERMINATED.  
MARKET DROP: 11.4%

Chloe's face empties.

CHLOE  
Adrian will end you.

Serena walks over and pins Chloe's hand to the floor with her heel.

CHLOE SCREAMS.

SERENA  
You took my children.  
This is the first invoice.

She presses harder.

SERENA  
Tell Adrian he isn't bleeding because I hate him.  
He's bleeding because he once thought I was harmless.

She steps off.

SERENA  
Stay alive. I want you front row when he kneels.

## EPISODE 6

**INT. ASHFORD TOWER PARKING GARAGE - NIGHT**

Rows of lights snap on. Serena's car is boxed in by Adrian's convoy.

Adrian reaches her door before anyone else can.

ADRIAN  
Where were you for five years?

Serena steps out, calm.

SERENA  
Out of your reach.

ADRIAN  
You took my routes, my port, half my board. Why?

She peels back her sleeve.

The burn scar runs from wrist to elbow.

SERENA  
This burned that night.  
So did the child inside me.

Adrian goes still.

ADRIAN  
What did you say?

SERENA  
You didn't leave one woman in that car.  
You left a mother and her child there.

She gets back in.

SERENA  
Dig up whatever lets you sleep, Adrian.

Her car glides out.

Adrian braces a hand on the lane divider, shaking.

ADRIAN  
I want every file from that crash. Every one.

## EPISODE 7

**INT. ASHFORD FOUNDATION WINTER GALA - NIGHT**

A donor nursery display glows under crystal light. Cameras flash as Chloe reaches for the ribbon.

CHLOE  
The future heir of Ashford --

The applause dies halfway through.

Serena enters with DR. KELLER and MARTIN HALE, the chauffeur who fed timing to the truck driver.

She places an old recorder and two cashier's checks on the champagne tower.

SERENA  
Let's tell the whole story.

ADRIAN  
Not here.

SERENA  
It started in public. It ends in public.

She nods to Keller.

DR. KELLER  
I falsified Ms. Mercer's prenatal file. She wasn't pregnant when she paid me.

Martin swallows.

MARTIN  
She paid me to track Ms. Vale's route. I passed the window to the truck.

CHLOE  
Lies!

Serena clicks on the recorder.

CHLOE'S VOICE (RECORDER)  
No bruises on me. No police. And if Serena burns with the car, all the better.

The room recoils.

Adrian signals security on instinct.

SERENA  
Five years ago you protected the lie.  
Go ahead. Do it again.

His hand stops in the air.

## EPISODE 8

**INT. ASHFORD FOUNDATION WINTER GALA - CONTINUOUS**

Serena drops two DNA envelopes and a child's heir bracelet at Adrian's feet.

SERENA  
Open them.

Chloe dives for the papers. Serena traps Chloe's gown under her heel.

Adrian tears the first envelope open.

SERENA  
That's your non-match.

He opens the second.

SERENA  
That's the boy and his father.

A short security clip flashes on the wall: Chloe kissing JULIAN CROSS in a hotel elevator, timestamp glowing.

Phones lift. Donors whisper. Some laugh.

Adrian grabs Chloe's arm.

ADRIAN  
Tell me this is fake.

CHLOE  
You want the truth? Fine!  
The lie worked because you wanted it!  
You left Serena in that fire while she was carrying your twins!

The room goes thin around Adrian.

SERENA  
You didn't lose me to her lies.  
You lost me when you walked away.

He lets Chloe go like she's venom.

The envelopes shake in his hand.

## EPISODE 9

**INT. OLD STORAGE LOFT - NIGHT**

Adrian smashes open a rusted wall safe.

Inside: a sonogram stamped *TWO FETAL HEARTBEATS*, a half-knitted baby sweater, a velvet ring box, and a cream card with two names half-written in Serena's hand.

He touches the sweater and breaks.

FLASH - Serena on a sofa, smiling, holding up the unfinished sweater.

SERENA (FLASH)  
Do you think they'll have your eyes?

FLASH ENDS.

Adrian sinks to his knees.

ADRIAN  
No...

He grabs the sonogram and stumbles outside.

**EXT. RAIN-SOAKED STREET - CONTINUOUS**

Rain slams the pavement.

Adrian drops into the mud and hits himself again and again.

ADRIAN  
I killed them!  
I killed my sons!

He crushes the soaked sonogram to his forehead and sobs Serena's name into the storm.

## EPISODE 10

**INT. SKYLINE REVOLVING RESTAURANT - NIGHT**

New York burns below in gold.

Victor lifts a glass across from Serena. Shipping financiers and old Ashford allies pretend not to stare.

VICTOR  
Half his board. Half his freight.  
You took the air out of him.

SERENA  
This is breathing. Not victory.

The restaurant doors slam inward.

Adrian storms in soaked, muddy, half ruined.

He shoves Victor aside and drops to his knees before Serena.

Gasps ripple through the room.

He sets down the Ashford signet seal, controlling proxies, trust deeds, emergency signatures -- one by one.

ADRIAN  
Everything's signed.  
The ports. The holding company. The voting block.  
Take it all.

He looks up, stripped raw.

ADRIAN  
I can't give back our children. I can't unmake the fire.  
But I can spend what's left of my life under your hand.  
Use me. Hate me. Keep me alive and punish me.  
Just don't cast me out of what I destroyed.

Victor watches, rigid.

Serena rises. A compact silver pistol appears in her hand.

She presses it to Adrian's forehead.

SERENA  
Two sons.  
One life.  
Show me the math.

Adrian closes his eyes.

SERENA  
You want forgiveness?  
Bring me an answer first.

She pulls the trigger.

BANG.

BLACK.
