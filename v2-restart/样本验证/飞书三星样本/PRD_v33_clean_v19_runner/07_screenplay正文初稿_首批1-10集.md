## EPISODE 1

**INT. ASHFORD ESTATE KITCHEN - NIGHT**

SERENA VALE closes a slim encrypted notebook and slides it into a hidden slot behind the spice rack. She checks the small sonogram folded inside her blazer pocket. Two tiny circles. Two heartbeats.

She plates ADRIAN ASHFORD'S favorite dinner.

The kitchen doors swing open.

ADRIAN enters with CHLOE MERCER wrapped around his arm, one hand resting on her stomach. A lawyer's envelope lands on the marble island.

ADRIAN  
Stop cooking.

SERENA  
You're early.

CHLOE smiles like she owns the room already.

ADRIAN  
Sign the divorce papers. Chloe is carrying my heir.

Serena stares at him. Then at Chloe's hand on Adrian's chest.

SERENA  
Three years, Adrian. You bring her into my kitchen?

ADRIAN  
Your kitchen? This house has been running on my name since the day I married you.

Chloe takes one step forward, lets her heel slide, and gasps.

CHLOE  
Adrian!

Adrian spins and SLAPS Serena across the face before Serena even moves.

ADRIAN  
Don't touch her.

Blood beads at Serena's lip. She slowly reaches into her pocket, pulls out the folded sonogram, and looks at it for one last second.

Then she crushes it in her fist, shoves it into her mouth, grabs a glass of water, and swallows.

CHLOE  
What was that?

SERENA  
Something you don't get to steal.

She signs the divorce papers with a steady hand.

SERENA  
Send your heir to my kitchen when he learns how to chew.

Serena walks out with one suitcase.

UPSTAIRS BALCONY - Chloe leans over the rail, all sweetness gone.

CHLOE  
(low)  
You won't make it to the airport.

## EPISODE 2

**EXT. COASTAL HIGHWAY - NIGHT**

Serena sits alone in the back of a town car, passport and one boarding pass in her lap.

Headlights flash in the side mirror.

A TRUCK rams the car broadside. Metal screams. The sedan flips, slams into the barrier, and lands hard on its side.

Gasoline drips.

Serena tries the door. Jammed.

SERENA  
Help! Somebody help me!

Across the divider, a black SUV has also hit the rail. Chloe's scream cuts through the night.

A convoy of Ashford security vehicles skids to a stop. Adrian jumps out first.

SECURITY CHIEF  
Mr. Ashford! Ms. Vale is trapped!

ADRIAN  
Get Chloe out!

He smashes the SUV glass with a tire iron and pulls Chloe into his arms.

Serena pounds the window, smoke crawling around her ankles.

SERENA  
Adrian! Adrian, please! I'm carrying your twins!

Adrian looks at her through the broken glare of the headlights.

ADRIAN  
You tried to scare Chloe off the road. If you want pity, find it in hell.

CHLOE clings to his neck, trembling perfectly.

CHLOE  
Please take me away from here.

ADRIAN  
Move out!

The convoy leaves.

Serena watches the red taillights disappear. Fire races along the leaking fuel line.

SERENA  
(whispers)  
You chose.

The car erupts.

## EPISODE 3

**INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - NIGHT**

Serena wakes under harsh white light. Bandages. Burn wraps. Her hand flies to her stomach.

DOCTOR LEWIS stands beside the bed, already grieving for her.

DOCTOR LEWIS  
You survived the blast. The babies didn't.

The room goes silent inside Serena's head.

A television on the wall is playing a society broadcast.

ON TV - Adrian raises a champagne glass beside Chloe.

ADRIAN  
Tonight, we celebrate the future of the Ashford family.

Chloe laughs against him. Behind them: a gold sign reading *ASHFORD HEIR FOUNDATION WING*.

The door opens. VICTOR SOKOLOV walks in carrying an old file box and a newspaper.

VICTOR  
If you cry now, they win twice.

Serena says nothing.

Victor drops the file on the bed. Shipping maps. Acquisition trails. Three names circled in red.

VICTOR  
You were never made for a dead marriage and a warm kitchen.  
Raven should have buried Ashford years ago.

Serena turns to him.

SERENA  
Raven is dead too.

VICTOR  
Then become something worse.

He places a silver pen in her hand. Serena grips it until her knuckles whiten.

SERENA  
No.  
Something colder.

Victor smiles.

FIVE YEARS LATER - AUCTION HALL DOORS open to a wall of flashbulbs.

## EPISODE 4

**INT. REDWATER MARITIME ASSET AUCTION - NIGHT**

Rows of billionaires. Shipping brokers. Family trustees.

Onstage, the auctioneer points to a model of REDWATER PORT.

AUCTIONEER  
Ninety million. Going once --

ADRIAN lifts his paddle.

ADRIAN  
Ninety-five.

A woman's voice cuts through the room from the back.

SERENA (O.S.)  
One hundred and twenty.

Heads turn.

Serena steps into the light in a black coat, flanked by quiet security. Not a trace of the old housewife remains.

Chloe goes pale, then angry.

CHLOE  
Look who crawled back. Couldn't survive without Ashford money?

Serena crosses the floor, stops in front of her, and SLAPS her hard enough to send her into a chair.

SERENA  
You still mistake noise for power.

Adrian rises, jaw locked.

ADRIAN  
Security.

Serena tosses a leather folder into his chest.

SERENA  
Read before you bark.

He opens it. Insurance locks. Port access revocations. Cold-chain priority seized. Fuel hedges reassigned. Every emergency route Ashford needs now runs through Raven-controlled entities.

SERENA  
Your West Coast berths, your bonded warehouses, your winter freight insurance -- all routed through me.  
Bid again if you like. Then explain to your board why your empire suffocated on live television.

Adrian looks up slowly.

ADRIAN  
...Serena?

SERENA  
Too late for that name to save you.

The hammer falls.

AUCTIONEER  
Sold. To Raven Capital.

## EPISODE 5

**INT. SERENA'S PENTHOUSE SUITE - NIGHT**

The front doors crash open.

Chloe storms in with six private guards.

CHLOE  
You froze Adrian's port traffic. You touched what isn't yours.

Serena sits on a sofa, one leg crossed over the other, wine untouched in her hand.

SERENA  
You're standing in what is.

Chloe lunges.

A hidden panel slides open. Serena's security team floods the room and has Chloe's men face-down in seconds.

A wall screen lights up behind Serena.

ASHFORD CARGO HOLD: SUSPENDED.  
EUROPEAN COLD-CHAIN LICENSES: TERMINATED.  
MARKET DROP: 11.4%

Chloe's bravado falters.

CHLOE  
Adrian will destroy you.

Serena stands, walks over, and pins Chloe's hand to the floor with her stiletto heel.

Chloe SCREAMS.

SERENA  
Five years ago you stole my house, my children, and my name.  
This is interest.

She presses harder.

SERENA  
Tell Adrian he isn't bleeding because I hate him.  
He's bleeding because he once believed I couldn't do this.

Serena lifts her heel. Chloe pulls her hand back, shaking.

SERENA  
Run. I want you alive long enough to watch him kneel.

## EPISODE 6

**INT. ASHFORD TOWER PARKING GARAGE - NIGHT**

Overhead lights SNAP ON in a row.

Serena's car stops. Adrian's convoy blocks the exit.

Adrian walks toward her before the driver can open the door.

ADRIAN  
Where did you go for five years?

Serena steps out, closing the door behind her.

SERENA  
Somewhere your name couldn't reach.

ADRIAN  
You took my routes, my port, half my board's confidence. Why?

SERENA  
You want the short answer?

She peels back her sleeve.

The burn scar snakes from wrist to elbow.

Adrian's breath catches.

SERENA  
This burned that night.  
So did the child inside me.

Adrian freezes.

ADRIAN  
What did you say?

SERENA  
I said you didn't leave one woman in a car.  
You left a mother and her child there.

She gets back into the car.

SERENA  
Now chase records. Chase ghosts. Chase whatever lets you sleep.

The car pulls away.

Adrian stands in the lane, stunned.

ADRIAN  
Get me every file from that crash. The clinic, the driver, the tow yard. Everything.

## EPISODE 7

**INT. ASHFORD FOUNDATION WINTER GALA - NIGHT**

A donor-sized nursery display sits under crystal chandeliers. Cameras flash as Chloe places a hand on the ribbon.

CHLOE  
The future heir of Ashford will grow up knowing this city loved him first --

Applause starts.

Then stops.

Serena enters with DR. KELLER, gray-haired and sweating, and MARTIN HALE, the chauffeur who drove Chloe the night of the crash.

Serena sets an old voice recorder and two cashier's checks on the champagne tower.

SERENA  
Let's finish the origin story.

Chloe's smile cracks.

ADRIAN  
Not here.

SERENA  
It happened in public. It dies in public.

She nods to Dr. Keller.

DR. KELLER  
I falsified Ms. Mercer's prenatal file. She wasn't pregnant when she paid me.

The room ripples.

Martin swallows hard.

MARTIN  
She paid me to shadow Ms. Vale's car route. I passed the timing to the truck driver.

CHLOE  
They're lying!

Serena flicks on the recorder.

CHLOE'S VOICE (RECORDER)  
No bruises on me. No police. And if Serena burns with the car, all the better.

Adrian looks at Chloe. Chloe looks at the exits.

ADRIAN  
Shut this down.

SERENA  
Five years ago you protected the lie.  
Do it again. Let every donor here watch you choose her twice.

Nobody moves.

## EPISODE 8

**INT. ASHFORD FOUNDATION WINTER GALA - CONTINUOUS**

Serena drops two sealed DNA envelopes and a child's heir bracelet at Adrian's feet.

SERENA  
Open them.

Chloe lunges for the envelopes. Serena catches the train of Chloe's gown under her heel and stops her cold.

CHLOE  
Don't touch that!

ADRIAN tears the first envelope open.

His face drains.

SERENA  
That's your non-match.

He opens the second.

SERENA  
That's the boy and his real father.

The wall screen flashes one short security clip: Chloe kissing JULIAN CROSS in a hotel elevator, timestamped six months before her "heir" announcement.

Gasps. Phones rise. Whispers turn into laughter.

Adrian grabs Chloe by the arm.

ADRIAN  
Tell me this is fake.

CHLOE  
You want truth? Fine!  
The lie worked because you wanted it to work.  
You left Serena in that fire. You left her there while she was carrying your twins!

The room drops away from Adrian.

SERENA  
You didn't lose me to her lies, Adrian.  
You lost me when you walked away.

Adrian releases Chloe like she's poison.

The DNA papers shake in his hand.

## EPISODE 9

**INT. OLD STORAGE LOFT - NIGHT**

Adrian smashes open a rusted wall safe with a crowbar.

Dust rains down.

Inside: a faded sonogram stamped *TWO FETAL HEARTBEATS*, a half-knitted baby sweater, a velvet ring box, and a cream card with two names half-written in Serena's hand.

Adrian touches the sweater like it might cut him.

FLASH - Serena on a sofa, younger, smiling, holding the unfinished sweater.

SERENA (FLASH)  
Do you think they'll look more like you?

FLASH ENDS.

Adrian sinks to his knees.

ADRIAN  
No...

He clutches the sonogram to his chest and stumbles outside.

**EXT. RAIN-SOAKED STREET - CONTINUOUS**

Rain hammers the pavement.

Adrian drops into the mud, slapping himself again and again.

ADRIAN  
I killed them!  
I killed my sons!

He presses the soaked sonogram to his forehead and breaks apart in the street, crying Serena's name like it is the only language he has left.

## EPISODE 10

**INT. SKYLINE REVOLVING RESTAURANT - NIGHT**

New York glitters below.

Victor raises a glass across from Serena. Around them sit shipping financiers, old Ashford allies, and two reporters pretending not to listen.

VICTOR  
Half his routes. Half his board.  
You finally took the air out of him.

SERENA  
This is breathing, not victory.

The restaurant doors explode inward.

Adrian strides in soaked, muddy, half wild.

He shoves Victor aside and drops to his knees in front of Serena.

Gasps ripple through the room.

Adrian lays one item after another on the table: the Ashford signet seal, controlling proxies, trust deeds, emergency signatures.

ADRIAN  
Everything is signed.  
The ports. The holding company. The voting block.  
Take all of it.

He looks up, wrecked.

ADRIAN  
I can't return our children. I can't undo the fire.  
But I can spend what's left of my life under your hand.  
Use me. Hate me. Keep me alive and punish me.  
Just don't send me away from what I destroyed.

Victor watches, jaw tight.

Serena rises. Pulls a compact silver pistol from the back of her dress.

She presses it to Adrian's forehead.

SERENA  
Two sons.  
One life.  
Show me the math.

Adrian closes his eyes.

SERENA  
You want forgiveness? Earn an answer first.

She pulls the trigger.

BANG.

BLACK.
