# PRD v16-v6workflow 豪门商战版首批 1-10 集

Title: The Ex-Wife Holds The Live Button

Genre: overseas short drama / family empire / business revenge / groveling ex-husband

Main characters:

- Serena Hale / Aria North: Adrian's ex-wife. Once erased by Blackwell, now the controlling force behind North Capital.
- Adrian Blackwell: CEO and heir of Blackwell Global. Proud, image-bound, and late to the truth.
- Chloe Whitmore: Adrian's old love and Blackwell Foundation chair. Her child and rescue story are both lies.
- Marcus Arden: Serena's capital partner and protector.
- Bennett Cole: Blackwell CFO, Chloe's secret lover and Noah's real father.
- Lydia Price: former south gate supervisor from the night Serena was taken away.
- Noah: Chloe and Bennett's son.

---

## Episode 1: The Brooch On Her Chest

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - LAUNCH BACKSTAGE - NIGHT

A launch countdown glows above the curtain.

ON SCREEN: 00:10:00.

Serena Hale shuts a silver rescue case. Her palm leaves a green scan on the glass top.

Inside the case: emergency funding papers, a Blackwell seal, and a final launch authorization.

SERENA
Tell the banks the case is alive. Blackwell survives tonight.

She ends the call.

From her purse, Serena takes out an ultrasound. Two pale dots sit inside the dark oval.

She slips it into an anniversary card and rests the card on the case.

SERENA
Your father gets his company and his babies on the same night.

The backstage door opens.

Adrian Blackwell enters with Chloe Whitmore on his arm. Chloe wears Serena's Blackwell spouse brooch. Lawyers and board members follow them.

Serena rises.

SERENA
Why is my brooch on her chest?

Adrian drops a divorce packet onto the rescue case.

ADRIAN
Sign before the curtain goes up.

Chloe touches her stomach.

CHLOE
I did not want it this way, Serena. But the heir cannot grow up in scandal.

Serena looks at Adrian.

SERENA
The heir?

ADRIAN
My child.

The room looks away from Serena.

Serena pushes the rescue case toward Chloe.

SERENA
Then let the mother of your heir open the case that saved your company.

Chloe does not touch it.

CHLOE
She wants to make me fail in front of them.

SERENA
One palm. That is all.

Adrian grabs Serena's wrist.

ADRIAN
This is why you are leaving.

The anniversary card falls. The ultrasound slides across the floor.

Serena reaches down.

Chloe steps first. Her heel grinds the corner of the ultrasound. The two white dots smear under her shoe.

Serena freezes.

CHLOE
Oh. I did not see it.

Adrian tears the brooch from Serena's dress.

The pin rips skin below her collarbone. Blood beads along the torn fabric.

ADRIAN
You are no longer Blackwell.

He puts a pen in Serena's hand.

ADRIAN
Sign.

Serena picks up the ultrasound. Her thumb only spreads the dirt.

She signs the divorce page. She leaves the liability page blank.

SERENA
I will not sign your lie.

Adrian nods to security.

Two guards take Serena by the arms.

At the door, Serena looks at the brooch on Chloe's chest.

SERENA
Keep it warm.

The guards push her out.

The room exhales.

Chloe opens a lawyer's briefcase and takes out a red folder.

The folder tab reads: SERENA HALE.

She slides it to a compliance investigator waiting by the service entrance.

CHLOE
Before she runs.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 2: The Door He Closed

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - MAIN LAUNCH HALL - NIGHT

Reporters fill the hall. The launch screen stays black.

Federal investigators step onto the stage.

INVESTIGATOR
Serena Hale is wanted for questioning in the failed rescue filing.

The side doors open.

Serena is dragged in by guards. Blood has dried under her torn collarbone.

Cameras swing to her.

SERENA
Adrian, do not sign that folder. Chloe cannot open the case.

Chloe folds against Adrian.

CHLOE
She is doing it again.

Serena pulls the ruined ultrasound from her fist.

SERENA
I am pregnant. Twins. Yours.

The hall falls quiet.

Adrian sees the shoe print over the two dots.

For one breath, his face cracks.

Chloe gasps and clutches her stomach.

CHLOE
Adrian. Our baby.

The board chair thrusts the red folder at Adrian.

BOARD CHAIR
Sign it. If she remains tied to Blackwell tonight, the banks walk.

Serena steps toward him.

SERENA
Look at me. Just once.

Adrian signs.

Serena's face empties.

The investigators cuff her hands.

Above the stage, a rig snaps. Sparks rain down. A curtain catches fire.

The hall erupts.

People rush for the exits.

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - GLASS SERVICE CORRIDOR - CONTINUOUS

Serena is shoved through a glass service corridor. Her cuff chain catches on a steel rail.

Behind her, smoke rolls in.

On the other side of the hall, Chloe is pinned under a fallen lighting frame.

CHLOE
Adrian!

Serena pounds the glass.

SERENA
Adrian! Open the door!

A guard throws Adrian one override cutter.

GUARD
One cut, sir. The fire doors are dropping.

Adrian looks from Serena to Chloe.

Serena presses the ultrasound to the glass.

SERENA
Your children are in here.

Chloe sobs.

CHLOE
Please. I cannot breathe.

Adrian turns toward Chloe.

Serena watches him run away from her.

SERENA
Adrian!

He cuts Chloe free.

The first fire door slams down between Serena and the hall.

Smoke fills the corridor. Serena pulls against the cuff until blood runs down her wrist.

She hits the glass with her bleeding hand.

SERENA
My babies...

Her blood handprint slides down the glass and stops at the floor.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 3: The Woman Left Behind

INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - RECOVERY ROOM - NIGHT

Serena wakes under white lights.

Her hand goes straight to her stomach.

The bed sheet is flat.

She looks at the doctor before he speaks.

DOCTOR
I am sorry. We could not save the twins.

Serena's fingers curl around the sheet.

DOCTOR
You survived because someone pulled you from the loading dock before the second fire door sealed.

A television plays above the bed.

ON TV: Adrian stands beside Chloe.

ADRIAN ON TV
Blackwell will stand with Chloe and our child. Serena Hale tried to destroy this company tonight.

Serena reaches for the remote and drops it. Her hand shakes too hard.

Marcus Arden enters and turns the television off.

MARCUS
The old loading dock cameras vanished. So did your name from the rescue case.

Serena stares at the blank screen.

MARCUS
I can give you a new name. Money. Doors. Time.

He places a black folder beside her.

Inside: ARIA NORTH, North Capital formation documents, and a passport.

Serena takes the ruined ultrasound from the bedside tray. The heel mark still cuts across the two dots.

She folds it once. Then again.

SERENA
I do not want time.

She looks at Marcus.

SERENA
I want them to watch what time did.

INT. BLACKWELL CHILDREN'S WING AUCTION - NIGHT - FIVE YEARS LATER

Chloe stands under a donor wall with a ribbon on her dress and Serena's old brooch on her chest.

Adrian speaks with bankers nearby.

The doors open.

A woman in black gloves walks in. Her heels cut through the room's noise.

The bank chairman stops mid-sentence.

Chloe sees the woman's face.

Her smile dies.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 4: She Buys The Wing

INT. BLACKWELL CHILDREN'S WING AUCTION - NIGHT

The room turns toward the woman in black.

Chloe steps in front of the donor wall.

CHLOE
This is a private Blackwell event.

The auctioneer checks a tablet.

AUCTIONEER
Registered bidder, Aria North. North Capital.

Adrian narrows his eyes.

ADRIAN
Serena?

Serena does not answer him.

The auctioneer lifts the gavel.

AUCTIONEER
Naming rights for the new children's wing. Opening at twenty million.

Chloe smiles at Adrian.

CHLOE
For Noah.

SERENA
One hundred million.

The room gasps.

The bank chairman quietly leaves Adrian's side and walks to Serena.

BANK CHAIRMAN
North Capital's wire is already cleared.

The donor wall changes.

ON WALL: NORTH CHILDREN'S WING.

Chloe's smile stiffens.

CHLOE
You can buy walls. You cannot buy a family you failed to keep.

Serena walks to her.

She looks at the Blackwell brooch on Chloe's chest.

SERENA
That was never yours.

Chloe lifts her chin.

CHLOE
Touch me and he will throw you out again.

Serena unpins the brooch.

The room goes silent.

Chloe grabs Serena's wrist. Serena turns the grip, takes the brooch free, and drops it into a champagne bucket.

Ice clinks against the metal.

Reporters raise cameras.

Adrian steps between them.

ADRIAN
What do you want?

Serena looks past him at the glass doors near the service hall.

SERENA
You once closed a door.

Adrian follows her gaze.

SERENA
Tonight, I bought the room around it.

She leaves.

Adrian stands under the renamed donor wall, unable to move.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 5: Next, I Buy Witnesses

INT. BLACKWELL FOUNDATION OFFICE - DAY

Chloe smiles for a livestream beside a wall of children's drawings.

Her assistant rushes in and shows her a phone.

ON PHONE: BRIDGE LOAN FROZEN. NORTH CAPITAL REVIEW ACTIVE.

The donor wall behind Chloe glitches.

ON WALL: BLACKWELL FOUNDATION UNDER NORTH AUDIT.

The livestream comments explode.

Chloe's smile breaks.

INT. NORTH CAPITAL PENTHOUSE - NIGHT

Serena stands before a glass wall above the city. A list sits on her table.

Names: SOUTH GATE STAFF, LAUNCH NIGHT.

Chloe storms in with two private guards.

CHLOE
You froze a children's charity.

SERENA
I froze your costume.

Chloe grabs a framed launch photo and throws it. Glass cuts Serena's forearm.

Serena looks at the blood, then at Chloe.

Chloe raises the broken frame again.

Serena catches her wrist and twists. The frame drops.

Marcus takes one step forward.

Serena lifts a hand. Stop.

She drives Chloe backward until Chloe's shoulders hit the floor-to-ceiling glass.

Below, reporters look up from the street. Camera flashes rise like sparks.

CHLOE
Let me go.

SERENA
The red folder. Who carried it after I was cuffed?

CHLOE
Ask your ghosts.

Serena presses Chloe's palm against the cold glass.

SERENA
Who opened the south gate?

Chloe's eyes flick to the list on the table.

CHLOE
Lydia was paid to look away. That is all.

She realizes too late.

Serena releases her.

Chloe slides to the floor, shaking.

SERENA
Tell your donors the review is only beginning.

Chloe crawls toward the door.

Serena turns to Marcus.

SERENA
Next, I buy witnesses.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 6: Baby A

INT. BLACKWELL UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

Serena's car door opens.

Adrian steps into its path.

His security line blocks the exit. Serena's guards raise their hands to their jackets.

ADRIAN
Call them off.

SERENA
Move.

ADRIAN
Where did you go after the fire?

Serena tries to pass him.

Adrian catches her wrist.

She twists away. His hand brushes her lower stomach.

Serena's whole body locks. She stumbles back as if burned.

Her sleeve pulls up. A pale cuff scar circles her wrist.

Adrian sees it.

ADRIAN
Serena...

SERENA
You do not get to touch the place your choice emptied.

She gets in the car.

The door shuts between them.

INT. OLD BLACKWELL CLINIC RECORDS DESK - NIGHT

Adrian slams his Blackwell card on the counter.

ADRIAN
Launch night. Serena Hale. I want every record.

The clerk does not touch the card.

CLERK
Sealed by executive signature.

ADRIAN
Whose?

The clerk turns the old scan toward him.

Adrian's own signature sits at the bottom.

INT. SOUTH GATE SECURITY BOOTH - NIGHT

Lydia Price waits with a small paper bag.

Adrian steps out of his car.

LYDIA
You used to send people to ask for you.

ADRIAN
Give me what you have.

Lydia holds the bag behind her back.

LYDIA
Say please.

Adrian's jaw works.

ADRIAN
Please.

Lydia gives him the bag.

Inside: a scorched hospital wristband marked BABY A and the shoe-printed ultrasound.

Adrian's fingers shake.

ADRIAN
Baby A?

Lydia looks at him.

LYDIA
There was Baby B.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 7: Daddy Ben

EXT. BLACKWELL CHILDREN'S WING - DAY

A ribbon stretches across the entrance. Cameras crowd the steps.

Chloe holds Noah's hand. Adrian stands beside them, stiff.

CHLOE
Our son is proud to open the wing his father built.

Serena arrives at the bottom of the steps.

Reporters turn.

CHLOE
You are not on the program.

SERENA
Neither was the truth.

Chloe kneels to Noah.

CHLOE
Tell them who your daddy is, sweetheart.

Noah looks at Adrian. Camera flashes burst in his face.

A balloon pops.

Noah screams and pulls away from Chloe.

He runs past Adrian and throws himself at Bennett Cole.

NOAH
Daddy Ben!

Bennett catches him by instinct.

The ribbon drops from Chloe's hand.

Silence rolls across the steps.

CHLOE
Noah. Come to Mommy.

Noah hides his face in Bennett's jacket.

NURSE
We need a guardian signature for the child area release.

She hands a clipboard to Adrian.

Chloe snatches for it.

Serena catches the clipboard first and turns it outward.

ON FORM: EMERGENCY CONTACT: BENNETT COLE.

Bennett's name is printed under FATHER / GUARDIAN.

Reporters lift cameras.

All lenses turn from Bennett to Adrian.

Adrian looks at Chloe.

Chloe opens her mouth and no sound comes out.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 8: You Closed It Too

EXT. BLACKWELL CHILDREN'S WING - CONTINUOUS

The ribbon still hangs half cut. The crowd has not moved.

Adrian grabs Bennett by the collar and drives him against the donor wall.

ADRIAN
Say it.

Noah cries in Bennett's arms.

Bennett looks at the child, then at Chloe.

BENNETT
He is mine.

The donor wall screen flickers behind them.

Chloe drops to her knees.

CHLOE
Adrian, he forced me. I was scared.

Adrian turns on her.

ADRIAN
Five years.

He tears the foundation brooch from Chloe's lapel and throws it at her feet.

CHLOE
I loved you.

Adrian takes one step toward her.

Chloe crawls back and grabs his leg.

CHLOE
Do not put this all on me.

Reporters push against the security rope. Parents hold their children back but keep watching.

CHLOE
You signed the folder.

Adrian goes still.

CHLOE
You saw her bleeding behind the glass.

Serena stands at the edge of the steps, silent.

CHLOE
You turned around. You closed it too.

The crowd hears every word.

Adrian looks at Serena.

Serena takes the Baby A wristband from her coat and places it in his open palm.

He cannot close his fingers.

SERENA
Find the other one.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 9: The Second Band

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - SEALED SERVICE CORRIDOR - NIGHT

Adrian stands alone before a plywood wall marked CONDEMNED.

He rips the first board free. Nails tear his palm.

He keeps pulling.

Behind the boards, the glass corridor waits in the dark.

A black blood streak runs low across the glass.

Adrian steps inside.

His shoe crushes old ash.

He touches the steel rail where Serena's cuff caught. A notch has been cut into the metal.

He sees his reflection in the glass, split by Serena's old blood mark.

ADRIAN
I left them.

His voice dies in the sealed corridor.

INT. SOUTH GATE STORAGE ROOM - LATER

Lydia opens a dented locker.

Adrian stands behind her with blood on both hands.

LYDIA
I kept it because no one wanted two dead babies on a launch report.

She holds a second hospital wristband but does not give it to him.

LYDIA
Say who they were.

ADRIAN
Serena's children.

Lydia closes her fist.

LYDIA
No.

Adrian lowers himself to one knee.

ADRIAN
My wife. My children.

Lydia drops the second wristband into his palm.

Baby A. Baby B.

Adrian bends over both bands until his forehead touches the concrete.

INT. NORTH CAPITAL - PRESS ROOM HALL - NIGHT

The press room lights glow behind frosted glass.

Serena walks toward the door and stops.

Adrian kneels outside it, soot on his shirt, blood on his hands, two tiny wristbands held up like a sentence.

He looks up at her through the glass.

She does not open the door.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 10: Go Live

INT. NORTH CAPITAL - PRESS ROOM - NIGHT

An empty podium faces a row of empty chairs.

Two phones lie on the table.

One screen reads: GO LIVE.

Serena opens the door.

Adrian enters on his knees. The wristbands are in his hands.

He places papers on the floor before her.

ADRIAN
My resignation. My shares. Blackwell voting control.

Serena kicks the papers aside.

SERENA
Paper did not burn.

Adrian looks up.

SERENA
Say what did.

Outside the glass wall, Chloe and board members pound on the locked door.

CHLOE
Adrian, do not do this!

Serena turns one phone toward him. The camera sees his face but the live button is not pressed.

Adrian removes the Blackwell signet ring from his finger.

It hits the floor with a small sound.

ADRIAN
I signed the folder.

He swallows.

ADRIAN
I chose Chloe.

His hand closes around the wristbands.

ADRIAN
I saw my wife behind the glass.

The door pounds harder.

ADRIAN
I left Serena and our twins there.

Serena does not blink.

ADRIAN
Press it.

She slides the GO LIVE phone closer.

ADRIAN
Let them hear me.

Serena's finger hovers over the button.

Outside, Chloe screams.

Inside, Adrian lowers his head to the floor.

The phone reflects Serena's finger, half an inch above GO LIVE.

CUT TO BLACK.
