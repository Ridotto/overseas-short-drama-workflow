# Episode 1: The Replacement

INT. BEXLEY MANSION - KITCHEN - NIGHT

雨水拍在玻璃穹顶上。厨房里只开着一排暖灯。

SERENA VALE 合上笔记本，把一份 `BEXLEY EMERGENCY DEBT RESCUE` 模型锁进橱柜暗格。她指尖停在另一个信封上。

信封里，一张 ultrasound 照片露出半角。旁边是验孕报告。

Serena 把信封压在餐盘下，深吸一口气。她摸了摸还平坦的小腹。

SERENA  
Tonight, Adrian. No more secrets.

大门被推开。雨声涌进来。

ADRIAN BEXLEY 走进大厅，黑色大衣带着水汽。他身边挽着 CHLOE ROWE。Chloe 一手扶着小腹，另一只手攥着 Adrian 的袖口。

Serena 端着餐盘走出来，脚步顿住。

SERENA  
Adrian. You are home.

Adrian 没看餐桌。他把一份离婚协议放在料理台上。纸面滑过 Serena 刚摆好的银叉。

ADRIAN  
Sign it.

Serena 低头看纸。`DIVORCE SETTLEMENT AND EQUITY WAIVER`。

SERENA  
What is this?

Chloe 往 Adrian 身后缩了半步，眼眶立刻泛红。

CHLOE  
I told him not to hurt you like this. But my baby needs a father.

Serena 的视线落在 Chloe 小腹上。

SERENA  
Your baby?

ADRIAN  
My heir.

餐厅尽头，几个 Bexley 家族顾问和佣人都僵在原地，不敢出声。

Adrian 抬手，指向墙上 Bexley 家族旧徽章。

ADRIAN  
This house needs a mother for the next Bexley. Not a woman who hides in my kitchen and plays with spreadsheets.

Serena 的手指收紧。餐盘边缘磕在石台上，发出轻响。

SERENA  
Those spreadsheets kept your company alive last quarter.

ADRIAN  
You mean the charity work you begged to touch?

Chloe 慢慢走向餐台，手碰到 Serena 压着信封的餐盘。

Serena 下意识按住餐盘。

Chloe 盯着她的手，忽然脚踝一歪，撞向旁边的水晶酒架。

玻璃杯碎了一地。

CHLOE  
Adrian!

Adrian 冲过去扶住 Chloe。Chloe 按着小腹，眼泪掉下来。

CHLOE  
She shoved me.

SERENA  
I never touched her.

Adrian 抬手，一记耳光甩在 Serena 脸上。

啪。

Serena 偏过头，嘴角破开。血滴在离婚协议的签名栏。

ADRIAN  
You touch her again, you leave this house with nothing.

Serena 慢慢转回脸。她眼里的水光没有掉下来。

SERENA  
Three years, Adrian.

ADRIAN  
Three years of being useless.

餐盘下的信封被 Chloe 的鞋尖勾出来半截。Ultrasound 露出一角。

Chloe 的目光闪了一下。

Serena 先一步捡起信封。她抽出 ultrasound 影印纸，撕下露出字的那一角，放进嘴里咽下。

Adrian 皱眉。

ADRIAN  
What was that?

SERENA  
Nothing you would care about.

她拿起笔，在离婚协议上签名。笔尖划破纸面，留下一道深痕。

Serena 把笔扔回台面，转身上楼。

INT. BEXLEY MANSION - FOYER - NIGHT

Serena 拖着一个小行李箱走下楼。她经过家族徽章时，没有回头。

Adrian 站在楼梯底部。Chloe 依偎在他怀里，手却轻轻搭在扶手上，指甲敲了两下。

SERENA  
You will regret tonight.

ADRIAN  
I regret wasting three years.

大门在 Serena 身后关上。

EXT. BEXLEY MANSION - BALCONY - NIGHT

Chloe 推开 Adrian，走到二楼阳台。她看着 Serena 的车灯驶出庄园，脸上的柔弱消失。

她拨出一个号码。

CHLOE  
Start the maintenance window at the tower. Tonight.

电话那头传来一声确认。

Chloe 垂眼看向楼下雨幕。

CHLOE  
If she walks away, she talks. Make sure she never walks again.

黑屏。

# Episode 2: The Lift

INT. BEXLEY TOWER - UNDERGROUND LOADING LEVEL - NIGHT

Bexley Tower 的地下装卸层空旷冰冷。红色火警灯突然亮起。

Serena 拖着行李箱走进 freight lift。门刚合上，电梯猛地一震，卡在地下三层。

ON SCREEN: FREIGHT LIFT B3 - FAULT

电池储藏区传来爆裂声。白烟从电梯门缝里钻进来。

Serena 按下紧急对讲。

SERENA  
This is Serena Vale. I am trapped in freight lift B3.

对讲器只有电流声。

她按住小腹，脸色慢慢发白。

INT. BEXLEY TOWER - LOBBY - SAME NIGHT

另一侧，VIP glass lift 卡在大厅上方。透明轿厢里，Chloe 扶着小腹尖叫。

楼下媒体、董事和安保挤成一团。闪光灯乱闪。

ADRIAN 冲进大厅。

CHLOE  
Adrian! My stomach hurts!

救援主管跑到 Adrian 面前，递出平板。屏幕上两个红点同时闪烁。

ON SCREEN: FREIGHT LIFT B3 / VIP GLASS LIFT LOBBY

RESCUE CHIEF  
Sir, backup power can only stabilize one lift first.

Adrian 看向半空中的 Chloe。

对讲器突然传出 Serena 嘶哑的声音。

SERENA (V.O.)  
Adrian! I am in B3. There is smoke. I am pregnant.

Adrian 的手停在平板上。

Chloe 在玻璃轿厢里哭喊，手贴着玻璃。

CHLOE  
Our baby, Adrian! Please!

Serena 的声音再次挤出电流。

SERENA (V.O.)  
Adrian, please. I am carrying your child.

Adrian 的脸冷下来。

ADRIAN  
She is lying.

救援主管愣住。

ADRIAN  
Reroute power to the VIP lift. Now.

RESCUE CHIEF  
Sir, the freight shaft is filling with smoke.

Adrian 一把夺过平板，按下授权。

ON SCREEN: AUTHORIZED BY ADRIAN BEXLEY  
REROUTE POWER TO VIP LIFT

INT. FREIGHT LIFT - SAME NIGHT

灯灭了一半。电梯下坠半层，Serena 摔在地上。

她的手撑在地面，指缝里渗出血。

SERENA  
Adrian!

INT. BEXLEY TOWER - LOBBY - SAME NIGHT

救援吊臂转向 VIP glass lift。Chloe 被抱出来，扑进 Adrian 怀里。

Chloe 趴在他肩上，越过他的西装领，看向地下通道方向。

她轻轻闭了一下眼。

INT. FREIGHT LIFT - SAME NIGHT

门缝外火光变亮。烟把 Serena 的脸熏黑。

她攥住对讲器，指节发白。

SERENA  
I hate you.

对讲器烧断。火光灌进来。

Serena 把身体蜷起来，双手护住小腹。

货梯门缝被一片橙红吞没。

# Episode 3: Northstar

INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL - RECOVERY ROOM - NIGHT

Serena 猛地睁眼。白色灯光刺得她眯起眼。

她第一反应是摸向小腹。

手停住。

病床边，一只空的婴儿小毯被叠在透明袋里。袋子上有烟灰。

DOCTOR  
You survived the fire.

Serena 的嘴唇颤了一下。

SERENA  
My baby.

医生沉默。

Serena 慢慢转头。

DOCTOR  
I am sorry. There were two heartbeats when they brought you in. We lost them both.

空气像被抽空。

Serena 的手指抓住床单。针头被拉动，血从手背溢出来。

门打开。

JULIAN VALE 走进来，身后跟着两个律师。他把一台平板放在 Serena 面前。

屏幕上是新闻直播。Bexley Tower 外，Adrian 抱着 Chloe。记者围住他们。

REPORTER (V.O.)  
Mr. Bexley, is Miss Rowe safe?

ADRIAN (ON SCREEN)  
Chloe and our child will have the full protection of the Bexley family.

Serena 看着屏幕。她的眼泪没有掉。

JULIAN  
I pulled you out after his team left.

Serena 慢慢看向他。

JULIAN  
I also know who built the debt firewall that saved Bexley last year. It was not Adrian.

Serena 没说话。

Julian 把一枚烧黑的婚戒放到床头。

JULIAN  
You can spend the rest of your life being his dead ex-wife. Or you can stand up and make him remember what he buried.

Serena 拿起床边的手术剪。

她剪下婚戒上的 Bexley crest。金属落进托盘，发出清脆一声。

SERENA  
Give me a name he cannot touch.

JULIAN  
Nova Frost.

Serena 盯着新闻里 Adrian 护着 Chloe 的手。

SERENA  
Then bury Serena Vale.

INT. BEXLEY CENTENNIAL SUCCESSION GALA - NIGHT - FIVE YEARS LATER

金色灯光照亮整面 Bexley heir wall。墙上刻着历代继承人的名字。

主持人站在台上。Chloe 穿着白色礼服，牵着五岁的 LEO。Adrian 站在主位，冷峻矜贵。

HOST  
Tonight, the Bexley family honors its future.

一双黑色高跟鞋踏进宴会厅。

大厅门口，Serena 一身黑色高定西装，身后跟着 Northstar Capital 的律师和安保。

喧哗声一点点停下。

Julian 站在她身侧，微微一笑。

JULIAN  
Welcome back, Nova.

Serena 看向台上的 Adrian。

SERENA  
Let us see how much a future costs.

# Episode 4: Credit Freeze

INT. BEXLEY CENTENNIAL SUCCESSION GALA - NIGHT

SERENA

ON SCREEN: BEXLEY CREDIT FACILITY SUSPENDED

接着第二个，第三个。

CHLOE  
Who does she think she is?

SERENA

No. You are dead.

ADRIAN  
Serena?

SERENA  
Nova Frost to the market. Serena Vale to your guilty conscience.

CHLOE  
You crawled back for money? After five years?

啪。

无声。

You cannot throw me out of a house I now own in pieces.

What do you want?

SERENA  
Tonight? I want your family to learn how darkness feels.

她按下平板。

整面 heir wall 的灯一盏盏熄灭，只剩 Bexley crest 在黑暗里摇晃。

Serena 转身离开。

Adrian 站在台上，望着她背影。

CHLOE  
Adrian, do something!

Serena 停在门口，没有回头。

SERENA  
He already did. Five years ago.

# Episode 6: The Scar

INT. UNDERGROUND PARKING GARAGE - NIGHT

Serena 的车队驶入地下车库。出口栅栏突然落下。

一排车灯亮起。Adrian 的车队堵在前方。

Northstar 安保拔枪式伸手摸向腰间电击器。Bexley 安保也围上来。

Serena 推开车门下车。黑色风衣袖口被风带起。

ADRIAN  
Stop running my company from the shadows.

SERENA  
Your company is limping because you never learned what held it up.

Adrian 走近。

ADRIAN  
Where were you for five years?

SERENA  
Alive. Unfortunately for you.

ADRIAN  
You were my wife.

Serena 的眼神冷下来。

SERENA  
Your wife died in freight lift B3.

手腕上一道烧伤疤从腕骨延到小臂，狰狞扭曲。

Adrian 的视线僵住。

Serena 拉开风衣一侧。腹侧另一道疤被灯光扫过。

SERENA  
Your order burned here.

SERENA  
And so did my child.

ADRIAN  
What child?

SERENA  
You signed the reroute. You do the math.

STATUS: SIX WEEKS PREGNANT  
TRAUMA: SMOKE INHALATION / ABDOMINAL IMPACT

Adrian 的手开始发抖。

ASSISTANT  
Sir?

Adrian 抓起档案。

ADRIAN  
Get me every record from that night. Every second.

他抬头，眼里第一次不是怒，是恐惧。
