## Episode 1 - The Replacement

### INT. CALDWELL CLIFF HOUSE - KITCHEN - NIGHT

Serena Vale shuts a rugged laptop. On screen, a rescue submersible schematic collapses into encrypted code.

She slides the laptop into a false kitchen cabinet, wipes the keyboard dust from her fingers, and tucks an ultrasound envelope into her apron.

The front doors open before she can turn off the stove.

Adrian Caldwell enters with Mira Voss tucked under his coat. Mira's hand rests on her rounded belly.

Adrian looks at the dinner Serena made.

**ADRIAN**
Stop cooking.

Serena freezes with the wooden spoon in her hand.

**SERENA**
You're home early.

Adrian drops divorce papers onto the counter. Sauce splashes across the top sheet.

**ADRIAN**
Sign.

Serena looks from the papers to Mira's belly.

**SERENA**
Who is she?

**ADRIAN**
Mira. She's carrying my child.

Mira lowers her eyes, soft and practiced.

**MIRA**
I didn't want to hurt you. But a child needs a father.

Serena's hand goes to the ultrasound envelope.

Mira sways, then drops sideways.

Adrian catches her before she hits the floor. His eyes snap to Serena.

**ADRIAN**
You pushed her.

**SERENA**
I didn't touch her.

Adrian strikes Serena across the face.

The spoon falls. Serena's lip opens. She does not cry.

**ADRIAN**
Three years in this house and all you ever learned was how to be jealous.

He pushes the pen into her hand.

Serena pulls out the ultrasound envelope. For one second she almost opens it.

Mira watches.

Serena folds the envelope, stuffs it into her mouth, and swallows until her eyes water.

Adrian's face hardens, confused despite himself.

Serena signs the papers.

**SERENA**
There. Your house is clean.

She walks upstairs. Moments later she comes down with one suitcase.

At the doorway, she leaves her wedding ring in the bowl where Adrian keeps his keys.

### EXT. CALDWELL CLIFF HOUSE - BALCONY - NIGHT

Mira stands above, the softness gone from her face.

She dials a number.

**MIRA**
Pier Nine. Before dawn.

Serena walks away under the cliff lights.

## Episode 2 - Storm Bay

### EXT. COASTAL ROAD TO PIER NINE - NIGHT

Rain hammers the windshield of Serena's automated harbor car.

She grips a one-way ferry ticket.

A service tug slews out of the storm. Its steel tow cable snaps across the road and whips into the car.

The vehicle flips through the guardrail and slams into the maintenance deck below.

Fuel sprays. The door buckles shut.

Serena presses both hands to her stomach.

**SERENA**
No. No, stay with me.

Across the pier, Mira's launch bumps hard against a piling. Mira screams from inside, safe but loud.

Headlights cut through rain. Adrian and Caldwell rescue men arrive.

A crewman reaches Serena's car first and pulls at the door.

**CREWMAN**
Mr. Caldwell! Serena's trapped!

Adrian sees Mira's launch. Mira pounds the window.

**MIRA**
Adrian! The baby!

Serena slams her palm against the cracked glass.

**SERENA**
Adrian! I'm pregnant!

He hears her.

For one second, he turns.

The deck under both vehicles groans. A safety cable runs from Serena's wreck to the main crane. Another runs to Mira's launch.

Only one can be cut first.

Adrian grabs the rescue cutters.

Serena's eyes find his.

He cuts Serena's cable.

Her wreck drops lower into the burning maintenance pit.

Adrian smashes Mira's window and pulls her out.

**SERENA**
Adrian!

He carries Mira past Serena's trapped car.

**ADRIAN**
You staged this to kill her. Stay there and repent.

The rescue men follow him.

Serena is left alone with rain, fuel, and fire.

She watches Adrian's back disappear.

The fuel ignites.

The maintenance deck explodes.

## Episode 3 - Halcyon

### INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - NIGHT

Serena wakes wrapped in gauze.

Her hand goes to her stomach before her eyes fully open.

The doctor looks away.

**SERENA**
My baby.

The doctor says nothing.

Serena understands.

Her fingers curl into the sheet.

Nikolai Orlov enters with wet snow on his coat. He sets a tablet beside her.

On the screen, Adrian smiles beside Mira at a Caldwell charity event for expectant mothers.

**NIKOLAI**
He booked the whole harbor hall for her.

Serena stares until the image blurs.

**NIKOLAI**
I know who you are. Halcyon. The woman who designed half the world's deep-sea rescue brains.

Serena slowly turns to him.

**NIKOLAI**
You can die as Mrs. Caldwell, or come back as the woman they should have feared.

Serena reaches for a surgical scalpel on the tray.

She presses it into her palm until blood beads.

**SERENA**
Adrian. Mira. Caldwell. I want all of them to pay.

### INT. INTERNATIONAL MARITIME RESCUE SUMMIT - NIGHT - FIVE YEARS LATER

A contract ceremony gleams with glass, steel, and harbor lights.

Adrian raises a paddle.

**ADRIAN**
Twelve million.

Mira leans on his arm, jeweled and smiling.

The doors open.

Red-soled heels cross the marble floor.

The room quiets.

## Episode 4 - The Queen Of Salvage

### INT. MARITIME RESCUE SUMMIT - CONTINUOUS

Serena enters in a black coat, flanked by engineers and private security.

The host falters.

**HOST**
Ma'am, this bidding round is already--

**SERENA**
One hundred million.

The room turns.

Adrian stands.

**ADRIAN**
Who are you to challenge Caldwell?

Serena looks at him at last.

**SERENA**
The woman who owns the rescue systems your fleet leases.

Mira's smile collapses.

**MIRA**
That's impossible. You died.

She recovers and steps forward.

**MIRA**
Or did the dumped wife crawl back for a richer table?

Serena slaps her.

Mira hits the floor. Her earring skitters across the marble.

Adrian pulls his ceremonial captain's pistol from his jacket.

**ADRIAN**
Touch her again and--

Serena lifts the signed rescue contract and presses it down over the gun barrel. Slowly, she forces the muzzle toward the floor.

**SERENA**
Pull it, and every Caldwell rescue line goes dark by morning. Then see how long your name protects you.

Adrian opens the contract.

His eyes catch the signature.

**ADRIAN**
Halcyon.

Serena steps closer and guides the barrel to her own heart.

**SERENA**
You remember Eve? Good. She died at Pier Nine. Serena Vale is what's left.

Adrian cannot move.

**SERENA**
Five years ago you failed to kill me. Here's your second chance.

## Episode 5 - First Collection

### INT. CALDWELL COMMAND ROOM - NIGHT

Red alerts bloom across Caldwell's screens.

West Coast salvage drones: offline.
European emergency suppliers: terminated.
Thirty percent of rescue inventory: frozen.

Adrian sweeps an entire monitor bank off the table.

**ADRIAN**
Find her. Find everything she touched in five years.

Aide after aide avoids his eyes.

**AIDE**
Sir, Halcyon sealed our access with original patent keys. We can't override them.

Adrian dials Serena.

**ADRIAN**
Come back. The cliff house is yours. Name your price.

Serena's voice is cool through the speaker.

**SERENA**
You can't afford what I want.

She hangs up.

### INT. SERENA'S HOTEL SUITE - NIGHT

Mira kicks open the suite door with ten private guards behind her.

**MIRA**
You hit me in public. Now I carve that face.

Serena sits by the window with a glass of red wine.

She looks bored.

**SERENA**
With them?

Hidden doors open.

Nikolai's men step out. In seconds, Mira's guards are on the floor.

One guard is dragged to the balcony, screaming, before being dropped onto the lower awning.

Mira backs away.

Serena rises, takes Mira's hand, and places it flat against the glass coffee table.

**MIRA**
Adrian will kill you.

Serena's heel comes down on Mira's fingers.

Bone cracks.

Mira screams.

**SERENA**
Five years ago you tried to leave me in fire. This is only interest.

## Episode 6 - Scar Ledger

### INT. UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

Serena walks toward her car.

The garage lights snap on.

Adrian and Caldwell security step from every entrance. Serena's team raises their weapons.

**ADRIAN**
Where did you go after Pier Nine?

**SERENA**
Away from the man who cut my safety line.

**ADRIAN**
You stole my contracts.

Serena laughs once.

**SERENA**
Still counting cargo while standing over a grave.

Adrian steps closer.

**ADRIAN**
Why did you become this vicious?

Serena lifts one hand. Her guards lower their guns.

She pulls back her sleeve.

A thick burn scar twists up her wrist.

Adrian sees it and stops.

Serena holds the scar under the fluorescent light.

**SERENA**
This burned while you carried her away.

She lets the sleeve fall.

**SERENA**
So did the child I begged you to save.

She gets into the car. The fleet drives off.

Adrian remains under the lights, gun hanging loose at his side.

### INT. CALDWELL STUDY - LATER

Old medical files slam onto Adrian's desk.

His aide points to a damaged admission record.

**AIDE**
The clinic archived one page. Serena Vale. Pier Nine trauma. Pregnant on arrival.

Adrian grips the page.

The word PREGNANT wrinkles under his thumb.

**ADRIAN**
She was... carrying my child.

## Episode 7 - Public Hearing

### INT. MARITIME ETHICS GALA - NIGHT

Founders, captains, insurers, and rescue families fill the hall.

Adrian sits at the head table with Mira beside him.

The doors open.

Serena enters with Dr. Hale strapped into a steel rescue chair. Old orange rescue pliers are locked across the doctor's wrists.

The room falls silent.

Adrian stands.

**ADRIAN**
Serena, not here.

**SERENA**
Here is where you like to protect the innocent.

She nods to her men. They wheel Dr. Hale to the center of the floor.

Serena places a Caldwell emergency family badge on the table between Adrian and Mira.

**SERENA**
Keep it on her, and you tell this room she still belongs under your protection.

Mira reaches for Adrian's sleeve.

**MIRA**
She's staging this.

Dr. Hale trembles against the pliers.

**DR. HALE**
Mira paid me. The pregnancy file was fake. So was the transfer code used at Pier Nine.

Mira sobs at once.

**MIRA**
He'll say anything. She's threatening him.

Adrian steps in front of Mira.

The room sees it.

Serena picks up the rescue pliers and puts them into Adrian's hands.

He flinches when the cracked handle cuts his palm.

**SERENA**
You know this tool.

Adrian looks down.

The serial number is from Pier Nine.

**SERENA**
You cut one line with it. Mine.

Blood gathers in his palm.

**ADRIAN**
Enough. You will not destroy Caldwell in front of them.

Serena smiles without warmth.

**SERENA**
Still choosing the house.

She lifts two fingers.

A screen behind Mira lights for one second: Mira in a yacht cabin, laughing into another man's mouth.

The screen goes black before the room can turn it into entertainment.

The damage is done.

Adrian turns slowly.

He grabs Mira by both shoulders and drags her away from the head table.

Mira's badge tears off and hits the floor.

## Episode 8 - The Heir Flag

### INT. CALDWELL LEGACY DECK - NIGHT

The Caldwell heir ceremony has already begun. A young boy stands on the raised deck in a navy jacket.

Mira's hand trembles on his shoulder.

A folded heir flag waits in the boy's hands.

Adrian enters with blood still bandaged across his palm.

Serena stands at the far side of the deck.

**SERENA**
Raise it, Adrian. Let your heir claim the fleet.

Mira glares.

**MIRA**
Don't let her poison this too.

The boy lifts the flag.

Serena taps her phone.

The deck's emergency medical panel chirps. A red light flashes beside the boy's Caldwell family band.

NO BIOLOGICAL EMERGENCY MATCH.

Gasps ripple through the room.

Mira goes white.

**MIRA**
It's a system fault.

Adrian walks to the boy.

No one else moves.

The boy looks up.

**BOY**
Dad?

Adrian's jaw breaks for half a second.

He kneels, unclips the heir flag from the boy's shoulder, and folds it with shaking hands.

The room watches the fatherhood leave his body.

He lifts the child off the deck and sets him on the floor beside a Caldwell aide.

**ADRIAN**
Take him out.

Mira crawls toward him.

**MIRA**
Adrian, please. He's yours. He is.

Adrian pulls his pistol and puts it against her forehead.

Mira's face empties.

**MIRA**
Kill me, then. But don't pretend you're cleaner.

His finger tightens.

**MIRA**
Serena's child died because you left her in the fire. You cut that line. You killed your own child.

The gun lowers.

Adrian looks like the floor has vanished.

Serena watches from above the deck rail.

**SERENA**
You learned one truth.

She turns to leave.

**SERENA**
Not the worst one.

## Episode 9 - The Two Vests

### INT. ABANDONED PIER NINE STORAGE - NIGHT

Adrian kicks open a rusted service door.

Rain blows in behind him. His men search with flashlights.

**ADRIAN**
The locker she rented. Find it.

They pry open cabinets. Nothing.

Adrian notices a warped wall panel half under black water.

He grabs a steel bar and strikes it.

Once. Twice. Again.

His knuckles split around the bar.

The panel gives.

Inside is a small metal locker eaten by salt.

Adrian breaks the lock with his bare, bleeding hands.

The door pops open.

He reaches in and pulls out a plastic sleeve.

An ultrasound printout.

TWINS. SIX WEEKS.

His breathing stops.

He reaches deeper.

Two unfinished infant life vests come out in his hands, both stained brown at the edges.

There is also the cheap ring he once said looked childish.

Adrian sinks into the ankle-deep water.

He presses the vests to his chest.

No one speaks.

### EXT. PIER NINE - RAIN - NIGHT

Adrian stumbles into the rain with the two tiny vests.

He drops to his knees on the slick dock.

The ring falls into a puddle.

He slaps himself once.

Then again.

Harder.

Blood runs from his mouth.

**ADRIAN**
Serena!

He slaps himself until his men look away.

**ADRIAN**
I killed them. I killed our children.

The harbor siren moans through the storm.

## Episode 10 - One Life For Two

### INT. SKY HARBOR RESTAURANT - NIGHT

Serena and Nikolai sit above the city, harbor lights turning under the glass floor.

Representatives from half the port world drink around them.

Nikolai raises his glass.

**NIKOLAI**
To half of New York's rescue market.

Serena touches her glass to his.

**SERENA**
Half is not enough.

The doors burst open.

Adrian enters soaked, muddy, and swollen from the beating he gave himself. Guards rush after him.

He shoves past them and goes straight to Serena.

Nikolai stands.

Adrian pushes him aside and drops to his knees so hard the glass under him cracks.

The restaurant falls silent.

Adrian pulls a leather case from inside his coat.

He opens it with both hands.

Caldwell fleet seals. Port deeds. Board transfer papers. The command key to the rescue network.

He lifts them to Serena.

**ADRIAN**
Take it. The fleet. The ports. The name. My life. Take all of it.

Serena looks down at him.

His hands shake, but he does not lower them.

**ADRIAN**
I left you in that fire. I killed them. I know what I am.

He grips the hem of her black dress.

**ADRIAN**
Just let me spend what's left paying for it.

Whispers crawl through the room. No one has ever seen Adrian Caldwell kneel.

Serena takes the leather case.

She lets it fall to the floor.

Papers slide across the glass.

She draws a silver signal pistol from behind her back and presses the cold barrel to his forehead.

Adrian closes his eyes.

He does not move away.

**SERENA**
Forgiveness?

She cocks the pistol.

**SERENA**
One life for two.

Adrian opens his eyes.

**SERENA**
Pull the trigger yourself, and I forgive you.

Her finger tightens over his.

A gunshot cracks through the restaurant.

Black.
