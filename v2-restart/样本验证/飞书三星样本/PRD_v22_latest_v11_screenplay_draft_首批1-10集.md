# Episode 1: The Heir At My Table

INT. BLACKWOOD ESTATE - KITCHEN - NIGHT

雨敲着落地窗。Ava Cross 合上银色笔记本，屏幕上最后一行代码自动消失。

ON SCREEN: VESPER ACCESS CLOSED.

BUTLER
Mrs. Blackwood, Mr. Blackwood is home.

Ava 把信封藏进围裙内侧，端起汤盅走向餐厅。

大门打开。Adrian Blackwood 搀着 Serena Vale 进来。Serena 一手扶着微隆的小腹，另一手戴着 Blackwood 家族祖母绿手链。

AVA
Adrian... you're late.

ADRIAN
Put the soup down.

Ava 的手顿住。Serena 轻轻靠在 Adrian 肩上。

SERENA
I told him we could wait until tomorrow. But the baby shouldn't enter this family as a secret.

ADRIAN
Sign it.

Ava 看向封面。

ON DOCUMENT: Divorce Agreement. Family Trust Withdrawal.

AVA
You brought her to our table to hand me this?

ADRIAN
She is carrying the Blackwood heir. This family needs certainty.

Serena 走到 Ava 的座位前，轻轻摸了摸桌上的名牌。

SERENA
Ava, please don't hate me. A child needs a father. A family needs a future.

她手指一松，Ava 的名牌掉到地上，清脆一声。

Ava 弯腰去捡。Serena 脚下一歪，故意撞向椅角。

SERENA
Adrian!

Adrian 一把扶住 Serena，转身推开 Ava。汤盅翻倒，热汤泼在 Ava 手背上。

ADRIAN
Don't touch her.

AVA
I didn't.

ADRIAN
For three years you lived under this roof and gave this family nothing.

他的声音不高，但整张长桌都听见。

ADRIAN
No child. No value. No reason to stay.

Ava 的手伸进围裙。她摸到医院信封，指节发白。

Serena 看见信封一角，眼神一变。

SERENA
What is that?

Ava 抽出信封。Adrian 伸手要拿。

Ava 后退半步，当着所有人，把信封揉成一团塞进嘴里，硬生生咽下。她眼眶发红，却没有掉泪。

ADRIAN
What did you just swallow?

AVA
Something this family doesn't deserve.

她拿起笔，在离婚协议和信托退出文件上签字。

AVA
You want certainty? Here it is.

她把笔丢到汤渍里，转身离席。

INT. BLACKWOOD ESTATE - GRAND STAIRCASE - NIGHT

Ava 拖着一个小行李箱走下楼。女佣们站在两侧，不敢抬头。

墙上的家族屏幕刷新。

ON SCREEN: AVA CROSS - REMOVED FROM FAMILY TRUST.

她停了一秒，继续往门外走。

二楼阴影里，Serena 看着她的背影，柔弱表情完全消失。她拿出手机。

SERENA
She's leaving through the service road. Make sure she never reaches the airport.

SERENA
The Blackwood heir only needs one mother.
