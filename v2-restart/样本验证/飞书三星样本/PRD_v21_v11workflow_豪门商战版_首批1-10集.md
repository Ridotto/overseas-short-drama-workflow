## Episode 1: The Seat at the Table

INT. VALE ESTATE - PRIVATE KITCHEN - NIGHT

Ava Hart 合上加密笔记本。屏幕最后一行是 Vale 港口融资缺口的模型。

她把笔记本推进橱柜暗格，取出一张 pregnancy scan。她盯着上面小小的影子，手指轻轻发抖。

AVA
Tonight, your father finally hears about you.

楼下传来车队声。Ava 把 scan 塞进项链坠里，端起银盘走向餐厅。

INT. VALE ESTATE - FAMILY DINING HALL - NIGHT

长桌两侧坐着 Vale 家族成员和董事。Ethan Vale 穿着黑色西装走进来，身边扶着 Serena Blake。

Serena 的手放在微微隆起的小腹上。闪光灯从窗外花园照进来，像一场预先安排好的新闻图。

Ava 停在餐厅门口。银盘在她手里轻轻一晃。

ETHAN
Put the tray down, Ava.

Ava 看向 Ethan。

AVA
You said tonight was family dinner.

ETHAN
It is. That is why she is here.

Serena 低头，露出柔弱的笑。

SERENA
I'm sorry, Ava. I didn't want to hurt you.

Ethan 从律师手里拿过文件，放在长桌尽头。

ETHAN
Sign the divorce papers.

餐桌上的董事们低声议论。Ava 没动。

AVA
Three years, Ethan. You bring her to our table and ask me to sign?

ETHAN
This table belongs to the future of Vale. Serena is carrying that future.

Serena 的眼泪立刻掉下来。

SERENA
Please don't make this harder. My baby needs a name.

Ava 手指按住项链坠。

AVA
And what did I need?

Ethan 的脸冷下来。

ETHAN
You had three years to be useful. You decorated rooms. You cooked. You smiled for pictures. That is not a wife. That is furniture.

餐厅里安静到刀叉声都停了。

Serena 往前一步，脚踝一歪，整个人朝桌角倒去。

Ava 下意识伸手，却还没碰到 Serena，Ethan 已经一把推开她。

Serena 被 Ethan 抱住。Ava 撞到椅背，银盘摔在地上，汤汁溅上她的裙摆。

ETHAN
Did you just try to hurt her?

AVA
I never touched her.

SERENA
It's fine. Maybe she didn't mean it.

Ethan 抬手，Ava 的脸被打偏。项链坠弹开，pregnancy scan 露出一角。

Ava 立刻攥住坠子。嘴角渗出血。

ETHAN
Sign. Then leave my house.

Ava 看着他，眼里的光一点点冷下去。

AVA
Your house?

她拿起笔，在 divorce papers 上签下名字。笔尖划破纸面。

AVA
Fine. Keep your house.

她摘下婚戒，放在 Ethan 面前的酒杯里。戒指沉进红酒，发出轻响。

AVA
May it drown with your name.

Ethan 眼神一沉。

ETHAN
Walk out with dignity while I still allow it.

Ava 拉起行李箱，从长桌旁走过。Serena 挽住 Ethan 的手，眼泪消失得很快。

EXT. VALE ESTATE - BALCONY - NIGHT

Ava 的背影穿过花园。她没有回头。

二楼阳台，Serena 拿起手机，声音压低。

SERENA
She is leaving by the helipad path.

电话那头传来男人的声音。

VOICE
You want her delayed?

Serena 看着自己的孕肚，笑得柔软又阴冷。

SERENA
No. I want her gone.

花园灯把她的影子拉得很长。

AVA
We're leaving. Both of us.

ETHAN

AVA

她抬头，Ethan 的背影已经远到看不清。

AVA
You killed us.

金属火门猛地落下。火光吞没玻璃。

SFX: EXPLOSION.

黑屏。

## Episode 10: One Life for Two

INT. SKYBRIDGE RESTAURANT - NIGHT

整座餐厅悬在城市高空。玻璃地板下是车流灯河。

Ava 与 Julian 坐在主桌。投行代表和 Vale 几名董事站在旁边。

Julian 举杯。

JULIAN
To Sable Pier changing hands by morning.

Ava 与他碰杯。

AVA
To Vale learning what debt feels like.

餐厅门突然被撞开。

Ethan 冲进来，满身雨水，脸肿着，嘴角还有干掉的血。

安保要拦，他直接跪下。

全餐厅安静。

Ethan 从怀里掏出一叠文件，双手举到 Ava 面前。

ETHAN
Ava.

Ava 没动。

ETHAN
Every voting share I control. The CEO seal. The family trust proxy. Sable Pier. The estate. All of it.

他把 Vale 的银色 CEO 印章放到她脚边。

ETHAN
Take Vale. Take my name. Take my life.

Julian 站起来，挡在 Ava 身侧。

JULIAN
Get up. You're embarrassing yourself.

Ethan 没看他，只盯着 Ava。

ETHAN
I deserve worse.

Ava 慢慢放下酒杯。

AVA
Worse?

她起身，走到 Ethan 面前。

AVA
You think shares can buy back two heartbeats?

Ethan 的眼眶红得可怕。

ETHAN
No.

AVA
You think kneeling makes you smaller than the fire?

ETHAN
No.

Ava 拿起地上的 CEO 印章，在掌心转了一下，然后扔回他怀里。

AVA
Vale is a carcass. I can take it without your permission.

Ethan 低头，声音哑到几乎听不清。

ETHAN
Then take me.

Ava 看了他很久。

她转身走向餐厅尽头的应急门。门后是屋顶检修通道，风灌进来，警报红光亮起。

SFX: ALARM BEEPING.

安保和董事都愣住。

Ava 按住门，回头看 Ethan。

AVA
One life for two.

Ethan 慢慢抬头。

AVA
Step out there. If you fall, maybe I'll forgive you.

Julian 抓住 Ava 的手臂。

JULIAN
Ava.

Ava 甩开他。

AVA
He asked what I wanted.

Ethan 从地上站起来。膝盖不稳，差点摔倒。

他一步步走向应急门。

ETHAN
If my death gives you one night of peace, take it.

Ava 的眼神终于有一瞬波动，又立刻冷下去。

ETHAN
I should have turned back.

他踏上屋顶边缘的金属平台。高空风吹开他手里的股权文件，纸张飞进夜色。

Ethan 回头看 Ava。

ETHAN
I am turning back now.

警报红光闪烁。Ava 的手指按在门框上，指节发白。

SFX: METAL DOOR SLAMS.

黑屏。
