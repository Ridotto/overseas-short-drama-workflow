## Episode 1: The Seat She Lost

### 1-1 INT. LANCASTER TOWER - SKY DINING ROOM - NIGHT

纽约夜景压在整面玻璃上。长桌只摆了两套餐具。

Ava Sterling 把一份双胎超声报告塞进周年卡。她手指抚过照片上两个小光点。

电梯门开。

Adrian Lancaster 进来，手臂扶着 Celeste Vale。Celeste 穿白裙，一只手护在小腹上。私人护士跟在后面。

Ava 的笑停住。

ADRIAN  
Don't set another plate.

他把离婚协议推到桌上。纸角撞翻一只银勺。

AVA  
It's our anniversary.

ADRIAN  
It was.

Celeste 低头，声音柔软。

CELESTE  
I told him we could wait. But the baby shouldn't be hidden.

Ava 的手摸到周年卡。

AVA  
Your baby?

ADRIAN  
My heir.

旁边的家族律师放下另一份文件：`SPOUSAL TRUST WITHDRAWAL`。

ADRIAN  
Sign both. Leave before the board breakfast.

Ava 抬眼看他。

AVA  
Three years. That's all I am to you? A signature?

ADRIAN  
Three years of silence and warm dinners. Don't turn that into a marriage.

Celeste 后退一步，脚尖碰到椅腿，身体突然歪向桌角。

CELESTE  
Adrian!

Adrian 一把接住她，反手推开 Ava。Ava 撞到玻璃酒柜，酒杯摔碎。周年卡掉在地上，超声报告露出一角。

护士护住 Celeste。律师低头。没人扶 Ava。

ADRIAN  
Don't touch her.

AVA  
I didn't.

ADRIAN  
You were never this desperate when it was just us.

Ava 捡起周年卡。Adrian 伸手。

ADRIAN  
What is that?

Ava 看着他的手，看了很久。

她把超声报告抽出来，揉成一团，塞进嘴里。

Adrian 的手僵在半空。

AVA  
Something this family doesn't deserve.

她咽下去，眼眶红得发亮。她拿笔，只签离婚协议。

ADRIAN  
Sign the trust withdrawal.

AVA  
At eight tomorrow. Read your own bylaws.

Celeste 的指甲掐进掌心。

ADRIAN  
You want to be thrown out?

AVA  
You already did that.

Ava 拖起箱子。她路过 Celeste 时，Celeste 贴近她耳边。

CELESTE  
By morning, no one will remember you were here.

Ava 没回头。

### 1-2 INT. LANCASTER TOWER - PRIVATE HALLWAY - NIGHT

电梯门合上。Ava 的身影被金属门吞掉。

Celeste 站到窗边，脸上的柔弱褪干净。她拨出电话。

CELESTE  
She's taking the service elevator.

她看向餐厅里正背对她的 Adrian。

CELESTE  
No scandal. Just a system failure.

她挂断，重新扶住小腹。

## Episode 2: The Elevator He Chose

### 2-1 INT. LANCASTER TOWER - SERVICE ELEVATOR - NIGHT

Ava 走进服务电梯。门合上。楼层数字跳到 47。

灯闪了一下。

电梯猛停。行李箱撞到门。Ava 扶住墙，手按向小腹。

AVA  
Hello? Control room?

烟从门缝钻进来。

### 2-2 INT. LANCASTER TOWER - ELEVATOR ATRIUM - NIGHT

另一侧，VIP 电梯卡在玻璃井道中央。Celeste 双手拍着玻璃。

CELESTE  
Adrian! It hurts!

火警响起。洒水喷头爆开。

Adrian 带着安保冲进来。安保队长 Marcus 拿着平板，脸色发白。

MARCUS  
Both cars locked. Service elevator has smoke in the shaft.

ADRIAN  
Celeste first.

MARCUS  
Mrs. Lancaster is in the service car.

ADRIAN  
I said Celeste first.

### 2-3 INT. SERVICE ELEVATOR - CONTINUOUS

Ava 听见他的声音，扑到门边。

AVA  
Adrian! I'm here!

对讲机里一阵杂音。

AVA  
I'm pregnant. Please, open the door.

外面安静一秒。

Celeste 的尖叫穿透对讲。

CELESTE  
Our baby, Adrian!

### 2-4 INT. ELEVATOR ATRIUM - CONTINUOUS

Adrian 抬头看向服务电梯的位置。

ADRIAN  
She caused this.

MARCUS  
Sir--

ADRIAN  
Open Celeste's car.

消防员撬 VIP 电梯。金属发出刺耳叫声。Adrian 抢过撬棍，一下砸进门缝。

VIP 电梯门裂开。Adrian 把 Celeste 抱出来。

CELESTE  
You came.

远处服务电梯井传来钢缆绷裂声。

MARCUS  
The service car is dropping!

Ava 在对讲里咳到说不出话，只剩掌心拍门的闷响。

ADRIAN  
Get her to the ambulance.

他抱着 Celeste 转身。

### 2-5 INT. SERVICE ELEVATOR - CONTINUOUS

Ava 滑坐到地上。她的手掌在门上拍出血印。

AVA  
Don't leave us.

灯管爆开。电梯向下一沉。

她抱住小腹。

AVA  
Please.

电梯急坠。火光从门缝卷进来。

黑屏。

## Episode 3: Nocturne Wakes

### 3-1 INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - RECOVERY ROOM - NIGHT

白色天花板。监护仪滴答。

Ava 睁眼，第一下摸向小腹。

她摸到厚厚绷带。

AVA  
My babies.

医生站在床边，摘下口罩。

DOCTOR  
I'm sorry.

AVA  
Say it.

DOCTOR  
We lost them. Twins. Six weeks.

Ava 的手停住。床单被她攥出褶。

电视里传来掌声。Adrian 站在 Lancaster 大厅，Celeste 靠着他。

ADRIAN ON TV  
Celeste and the child are under my protection.

Ava 的输液管里回血。

病房门开。Julian Voss 进来，把一份旧账户文件放在她床边。

JULIAN  
He built a foundation for the woman who sent you into smoke.

Ava 盯着电视，不看他。

AVA  
Who are you?

JULIAN  
The man who pulled you out after his people walked away.

他把文件推近。上面是 Nocturne 原型模型和 Lancaster 旧融资危机。

JULIAN  
Your engine saved his company once. He never knew.

Ava 慢慢转头。

JULIAN  
I can teach you how to collect what he owes.

Ava 拔掉输液针。血滴到文件上。

AVA  
Then teach me how to bankrupt a king.

### 3-2 INT. VAN ARDEN CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT - FIVE YEARS LATER

拍卖厅灯光金黄。屏幕上是 Van Arden Hotel。

Adrian 举牌。Celeste 挽着他，笑得端庄。一个五岁男孩坐在保姆怀里，胸前别着 Lancaster 小徽章。

AUCTIONEER  
Three hundred million from Lancaster Global. Going once.

Celeste 靠近 Adrian。

CELESTE  
Our son deserves that ballroom.

AUCTIONEER  
Going twice--

大门打开。

红底高跟踩上红毯。黑裙停在灯下。

AUCTIONEER  
Ma'am?

Ava 抬起牌。

AVA  
Five hundred million.

## Episode 4: She Owns the Oxygen

### 4-1 INT. VAN ARDEN CHARITY AUCTION - CONTINUOUS

全场转头。Adrian 站起身。

ADRIAN  
Ava.

Celeste 的笑裂了一下。

CELESTE  
Ghosts shouldn't bid.

Ava 没看她，只看拍卖师。

AVA  
Record it.

ADRIAN  
You don't have five hundred million.

AVA  
No. I have your debt.

她把一叠债权文件扔到 Adrian 怀里。银行代表同时低头看手机，后退一步。

Adrian 翻开封面：`NOCTURNE CAPITAL`.

ADRIAN  
You're Nocturne?

Ava 走到 Celeste 面前，拿起一杯红酒。

Celeste 下意识护住白裙。

CELESTE  
Don't you dare.

Ava 把酒慢慢倒下。红色沿白裙淌到地毯。

AVA  
Now the dress tells the truth.

Adrian 按下安保呼叫器。没有人动。

Ava 伸手按住他的手，把呼叫器压回去。

AVA  
Every lender behind Lancaster leaves before dessert if they touch me.

Adrian 的手一点点松开。

拍卖槌落下。

AUCTIONEER  
Sold to Nocturne Capital.

Ava 转身离开。

ADRIAN  
What do you want?

Ava 停在门口。

AVA  
The first payment.

## Episode 5: Cameras Came First

### 5-1 INT. LANCASTER GLOBAL - BOARDROOM - NIGHT

投影屏上，多条融资线变红。

CFO  
Europe suspended. West Coast vendors are asking for cash. Van Arden is gone.

Adrian 把 Nocturne 文件摔到桌上。

ADRIAN  
She was my wife.

CFO  
She was also the anonymous model behind the 2021 rescue package.

会议室安静。

ADRIAN  
Get out.

### 5-2 INT. NOCTURNE PENTHOUSE SUITE - NIGHT

Ava 坐在沙发上翻合同。

套房门被撞开。Celeste 带记者和两个公关打手冲进来。

CELESTE  
Film her. Let everyone see what Adrian's bitter ex does to a pregnant mother.

Ava 抬眼。

AVA  
Cameras. Useful.

墙面屏幕亮起。Celeste 慈善基金账目、假受助人照片、转账记录一页页跳出。

REPORTER  
Miss Vale, is that your foundation?

CELESTE  
Turn it off!

她扑向 Ava。安保扣住她的手，按在玻璃展示柜边。

Ava 合上柜盖。

玻璃压住 Celeste 的手指。

CELESTE  
Ah--!

记者的镜头抖起来，但没人放下。

AVA  
You brought witnesses.

CELESTE  
Adrian will ruin you.

Ava 捡起 Celeste 的手机。屏幕显示 Adrian 来电。

她把手机丢进香槟桶。

AVA  
Tell him bill one cleared.

## Episode 6: The Scar He Never Paid For

### 6-1 INT. LANCASTER TOWER - UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

车库出口卷帘门落下。灯一排排闪烁。

Ava 停步。Lancaster 安保从柱后出现。

Adrian 站在车灯尽头。

AVA  
Still locking women in boxes?

ADRIAN  
Where were you?

AVA  
Under your tower.

ADRIAN  
You expect me to believe--

Ava 摘下手套，拉起袖口。烧伤疤从手腕爬到小臂。

Adrian 的话断掉。

她走近，把手臂伸到他眼前，又在他快碰到时收回。

AVA  
Smoke was in my lungs. Your child was inside me.

车钥匙从 Adrian 手里掉到地上。

ADRIAN  
No.

AVA  
That was your answer that night too.

她上车。车门关上。

Adrian 站在原地，没有弯腰捡钥匙。

### 6-2 INT. LANCASTER TOWER - MEDICAL ARCHIVE - LATE NIGHT

锁被砸开。Adrian 推门进档案室。

纸箱被他一箱箱掀翻。旧病历散满地。他的手被纸边划破。

ADRIAN  
The fire night. Every file.

助理不敢动。

Adrian 从底层抽出一个封存袋。

纸页滑出。

`AVA STERLING. PREGNANCY POSITIVE. SIX WEEKS.`

Adrian 盯着那行字。

远处日光灯嗡嗡作响。

ADRIAN  
She was pregnant.

他靠着档案架滑坐到地。

ADRIAN  
She was pregnant.

## Episode 7: The First Knife

### 7-1 INT. LANCASTER FAMILY TRUST GALA - NIGHT

水晶灯下，董事、媒体、名流围着主桌。

Adrian 坐在主位。Celeste 坐在他身边，那个五岁男孩被保姆抱着。

门开。

Ava 走入。律师跟在她身后。安保押着 Dr. Hall 到大厅中央。

Celeste 抓住 Adrian 的手臂。

CELESTE  
Make her leave.

ADRIAN  
Ava. Not here.

AVA  
Here is where lies get funded.

她把一张转账单拍在医生胸口。

AVA  
Who changed Celeste's pregnancy timeline?

医生发抖。

DOCTOR HALL  
Miss Vale.

Celeste 猛地站起。

CELESTE  
He's lying.

大屏亮起：伪造孕周记录、工程师收款、事故前电梯维修单、Celeste 的语音。

CELESTE ON SCREEN  
Make sure Mrs. Lancaster never comes back.

全场爆开。

Adrian 的脸色变了，但他仍站到 Celeste 前面。

ADRIAN  
You are humiliating my family.

Ava 看着他，笑了一下。

AVA  
I was your family when you left me burning.

Adrian 抬手。安保向 Ava 走去。

Ava 第二次按下遥控。

大屏切到酒店走廊。Celeste 和一个男人拥吻着进门。

CELESTE  
Stop it!

男人即将转脸，画面停住。

Adrian 慢慢看向 Celeste。

ADRIAN  
Who is he?

Ava 收起遥控。

AVA  
Ask the mother of your heir.

## Episode 8: No Blood Relation

### 8-1 INT. LANCASTER FAMILY TRUST GALA - CONTINUOUS

画面继续。男人转脸，是 Nate Royce。

Celeste 摇头，眼泪立刻涌出来。

CELESTE  
It's fake.

Ava 点头。律师把一个白色盒子放到主桌上。

LAWYER  
Family trust heir verification. The bylaws allow immediate testing.

Celeste 抱住 Adrian 的腿。

CELESTE  
Don't do this to our son.

董事们看着 Adrian。

BOARD MEMBER  
If he is the heir, prove it.

孩子被抱过来，哭声在大厅里回荡。

医生取样。棉签划过 Adrian 口腔。又划过孩子口腔。

Celeste 的手抖得扶不住椅背。

ADRIAN  
Look at me.

CELESTE  
He is yours.

ADRIAN  
Then stop shaking.

结果页打印出来。律师展开。

LAWYER  
No biological relation.

大厅死静。

闪光灯随后炸开。

Adrian 看着 Celeste。

ADRIAN  
You let me raise another man's son.

Celeste 被他甩开，摔到地上。她爬过去抓住他的裤脚。

CELESTE  
Don't judge me.

她抬头，妆被眼泪冲花。

CELESTE  
I rigged the elevators. I bought the engineer.

Adrian 僵住。

CELESTE  
But I didn't choose which door to open.

所有镜头转向 Adrian。

CELESTE  
I lied about whose child he was. You chose me while your wife burned.

Adrian 手里的信托签字笔掉在白地毯上。黑色墨水慢慢渗开。

Ava 走到他面前。

ADRIAN  
Ava--

AVA  
Now you know.

他伸手。

她后退一步。

AVA  
Too late.

## Episode 9: The Room He Never Entered

### 9-1 INT. AVA'S OLD DESIGN ROOM - NIGHT

封条被撕开。

Adrian 推开门。灰尘在手电光里翻起。

墙上还贴着 Ava 画过的餐桌设计。角落里，一台缝纫机蒙着布。

老管家站在门口。

BUTLER  
She asked that this room be left alone.

ADRIAN  
I left her alone for three years.

他抡起消防斧砸墙板。木屑飞溅。墙内保险柜露出来。

斧头一下下落下。Adrian 的手裂开，血顺着斧柄流。

柜门弹开。

第一张纸滑出来：`TWINS. SIX WEEKS.`

第二件是半织好的婴儿毯，边缘有暗黑血迹。

第三件是廉价尾戒。

### FLASHBACK - INT. SKY DINING ROOM - NIGHT

Ava 举起小毯子，笑得小心。

AVA  
I started something.

Adrian 盯着手机。

ADRIAN  
Board call.

AVA  
Just look once.

门关上。她的笑一点点消失。

### BACK TO PRESENT

Adrian 跪在地上，尾戒陷进掌心。

ADRIAN  
I never looked.

他狠狠扇自己一巴掌。

又一下。

老管家上前半步。

BUTLER  
Sir--

ADRIAN  
I never looked.

### 9-2 EXT. LANCASTER TOWER - NIGHT

暴雨砸下。

Adrian 跪在塔楼门口，怀里抱着超声单和婴儿毯。

ADRIAN  
Ava!

没人回应。

他又扇自己一巴掌。雨水和血一起流下。

ADRIAN  
They were real.

他抬头看着整座亮着灯的塔。

ADRIAN  
They were real.

玻璃门后，安保们不敢靠近。

## Episode 10: The Confession Button

### 10-1 INT. SKYLINE ROTATING RESTAURANT - NIGHT

旋转餐厅俯瞰纽约。Ava 和 Julian 坐在窗边。

桌上是 Lancaster 债权清单和 Van Arden 转让文件。

JULIAN  
You can take the rest by Friday.

AVA  
Debt ages better than mercy.

门口传来撞击。

Adrian 湿透冲入，脸上有雨水、血和掌印。他怀里抱着文件。

安保拦他。他推开，踉跄到 Ava 桌前。

文件散了一地：`CEO RESIGNATION`、`TRUST VOTING TRANSFER`、`PUBLIC CONFESSION`、`PERSONAL ASSET AUTHORIZATION`。

Adrian 重重跪下。

餐厅安静。记者举起相机。董事站在原地不敢动。

ADRIAN  
Take it.

Ava 没碰文件。

ADRIAN  
Lancaster. My vote. My name. Every dollar.

AVA  
Five years late.

ADRIAN  
I know.

他把认罪声明举起来，手抖得纸页响。

ADRIAN  
I'll sign anything.

Ava 没看文件。她把桌上的手机推到他面前。屏幕上是一个未启动的直播窗口，标题已经打好：

`ADRIAN LANCASTER STATEMENT TO SHAREHOLDERS`

AVA  
Not to me.

Julian 皱眉。

JULIAN  
Enough.

Ava 点了一下屏幕。红色倒计时出现：`GO LIVE IN 10`.

Adrian 的脸白了。

AVA  
To everyone.

倒计时跳到 7。记者们反应过来，纷纷抬起相机。

Adrian 低头看着散落的文件，又看向 Ava。

倒计时跳到 3。

他跪直身体。

ADRIAN  
I heard Ava in the elevator.

直播灯亮起。

ADRIAN  
I heard her say she was pregnant.

餐厅死静。

ADRIAN  
I saved Celeste anyway.

董事们的手机同时震动。屏幕弹出直播推送。

ADRIAN  
I left my wife in the fire. I left my children there with her.

Ava 的眼神没有软。

Adrian 把签好的信托投票权转让书推过去。

ADRIAN  
Take Lancaster.

Ava 没接。

AVA  
No.

她把另一台平板转向他。

屏幕上是董事会紧急决议：

`REMOVE ADRIAN LANCASTER AS CEO AND TRUST VOTING HOLDER`

底部两个按钮：`CANCEL` / `CONFIRM`.

AVA  
You press it.

Adrian 看着那行字。这个按钮比文件重。

ADRIAN  
Will you forgive me?

Ava 俯身，声音很轻。

AVA  
Press it and find out.

Adrian 的手指按下 `CONFIRM`。

大屏同步亮起：

`REMOVAL PENDING - SHAREHOLDER LIVE VOTE OPEN`

全场手机疯狂震动。董事们看向 Ava。

Julian 盯着她的手。

Ava 的指尖停在手机上的 `SEND FINAL CONFIRMATION`。

Adrian 跪在地上，抬头看她。

黑屏。
