英文名暂定：His Ex-Wife Holds the Empire Live

类型：海外豪门商战 / 家族企业 / 女性复仇 / 追妻火葬场

主要人物：

- Serena Hale / Aria North：Adrian 的前妻。五年前被丈夫和 Blackwell 家族抛弃，失去双胎；五年后以 North Capital 控制人身份回归。
- Adrian Blackwell：Blackwell Global 继承人兼 CEO。前期傲慢、护短、只信 Chloe 和家族颜面。
- Chloe Whitmore：Adrian 的旧爱，Blackwell Foundation 主席。伪造救市功劳和继承人叙事。

## Episode 1: Her Name Goes Into the Red Folder

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - LAUNCH BACKSTAGE - NIGHT

A countdown clock bleeds red on the wall.

ON SCREEN: 00:09:18.

Serena Hale sits alone behind a row of black curtains. Her evening dress is zipped only halfway. Her hair is pinned with one hand while the other hand works fast across a laptop.

The last green line appears.

ON SCREEN: ATLAS RISK CORE PATCHED.

Serena exhales. Her hand shakes from lack of sleep.

Beside the laptop lies a small ultrasound photo. Two white dots inside a dark oval.

Serena touches the photo with one fingertip.

SERENA  
After tonight, your father finally gets good news.

She slides the ultrasound into a silver anniversary card.

The backstage doors open.

Adrian Blackwell enters with Chloe Whitmore on his arm. Chloe wears a white dress and a temporary Blackwell family brooch. One hand rests lightly over her stomach.

Behind them: two lawyers, three board members, security.

Serena stands.

SERENA  
Adrian, the launch starts in nine minutes. Why is she wearing my brooch?

Adrian does not look at the laptop. He drops one divorce packet on the table.

ON DOCUMENT: DIVORCE SETTLEMENT.

A red folder sits under it. Serena sees her own name on the tab.

SERENA  
Responsible for what?

ADRIAN  
For tonight. Sign and leave before you damage us again.

SERENA  
I just saved your launch.

Chloe steps closer, soft voice, wet eyes.

CHLOE  
Serena, please don't do this tonight. Adrian needs peace. The baby needs peace.

Serena's hand goes still.

SERENA  
The baby?

Adrian puts one hand on Chloe's back.

ADRIAN  
Chloe is carrying my heir.

The room goes silent except for the countdown clock.

Serena's fingers close around the anniversary card in her bag.

SERENA  
You brought her here to tell me this in front of your board?

ADRIAN  
I brought witnesses so you can't twist it later.

Serena laughs once. It comes out too thin.

SERENA  
Then let her open the rescue case.

Chloe's smile tightens.

Serena points to a silver case on the table. A palm reader glows on its lid.

SERENA  
If that deal is hers, her hand opens it.

Chloe lowers her eyes.

CHLOE  
Adrian, she's trying to embarrass me.

Adrian grips Serena's wrist.

ADRIAN  
Enough.

He pulls hard. Serena's hip hits the table. The anniversary card falls out of her bag.

The ultrasound slips halfway free.

Serena lunges for it.

Chloe's heel comes down on the edge of the photo.

Serena freezes.

Chloe looks down, then up, innocent.

CHLOE  
Oh. I didn't see it.

Serena bends to pick it up.

Adrian tears the Blackwell spouse badge from Serena's dress.

The pin rips through fabric and skin. A red line opens below her collarbone.

ADRIAN  
You do not get to touch Blackwell systems again.

He presses a pen into her hand.

ADRIAN  
Sign the divorce.

Serena picks up the ultrasound. Chloe's shoe print has smeared across the image.

She wipes it with her thumb. The ink smears worse.

The countdown hits 00:06:00.

Serena signs only the divorce page.

SERENA  
I won't sign your lie.

Adrian's jaw hardens.

ADRIAN  
Then leave with nothing.

He nods to security.

Two guards take Serena by the arms. She keeps the ruined ultrasound clenched in her fist.

At the door, she turns once.

SERENA  
When I come back, I won't ask for a place in your family.

The guards push her out.

Chloe waits until the door closes.

Then she takes a red federal referral folder from the lawyer's case.

Serena Hale is printed on the tab.

Chloe slides it toward a waiting investigator at the side entrance.

CHLOE  
Before she runs.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 2: The Glass Door

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - MAIN LAUNCH HALL - NIGHT

The launch hall is packed with reporters, bankers, board members, and cameras.

The countdown hits zero.

The product video never plays.

Instead, the stock ticker plunges across the giant wall.

ON SCREEN: BLACKWELL GLOBAL -31.7%.

The crowd erupts.

Federal investigators push through the aisle.

INVESTIGATOR  
Mr. Blackwell. We need the named suspect.

The side doors burst open.

Serena is dragged in by security. Her collarbone is still bleeding. Her hands are free, but two guards hold her so tightly her shoulders twist.

SERENA  
Adrian, don't give them that folder. Chloe doesn't have the author seal.

Cameras swing toward her.

Chloe clutches Adrian's arm and bends over, breathing fast.

CHLOE  
She's doing it again. She's trying to ruin the launch.

Serena sees the red folder in the investigator's hand.

SERENA  
I am pregnant.

The noise dies.

Serena pulls the damaged ultrasound from her fist. The photo is creased, gray with Chloe's footprint.

SERENA  
Twins. Eight weeks. Yours.

Adrian looks at the dirty ultrasound.

For one second, his face cracks.

Chloe whimpers and grabs her stomach.

CHLOE  
Adrian, please. Our baby.

The board chair thrusts the referral folder at Adrian.

BOARD CHAIR  
Sign. If she is not removed now, the banks walk out.

Serena steps toward him.

SERENA  
Look at me.

Adrian's eyes lift.

Serena is crying now, but she does not wipe it away.

SERENA  
Please. Just this once.

Adrian takes the pen.

He signs the referral.

The investigators cuff Serena in front of the cameras.

SERENA  
No.

She tries to move, but the cuffs bite her wrists.

Then a loud metallic crack tears through the hall.

Above the stage, a lighting rig drops. Sparks shower down. Drapes catch fire.

People scream and surge toward the exits.

Chloe is knocked down near the front row. She reaches for Adrian.

CHLOE  
Adrian!

Serena is shoved into a glass service corridor by the crowd and the investigators. Her cuff chain catches on a steel handrail.

Smoke rolls into the corridor.

Serena pulls once, twice. The cuff does not move.

Blood appears under her dress.

SERENA  
Adrian!

Adrian turns.

Through the glass, he sees Serena on her knees, one wrist trapped, one hand pressed to her stomach.

Serena pounds the glass with the cuff chain.

SERENA  
The babies. Open the door.

Chloe crawls behind him, clutching his trouser leg.

CHLOE  
Adrian, I can't breathe.

Security has bolt cutters in hand.

SECURITY  
Sir! Her cuff or Miss Whitmore's rail?

Adrian stands between them.

Serena stares at him through the glass.

SERENA  
Please.

Adrian grabs the bolt cutters.

He turns away from Serena.

He cuts Chloe free.

Serena stops hitting the glass.

Adrian lifts Chloe into his arms.

SERENA  
Adrian.

He does not look back.

ADRIAN  
Keep Hale with the investigators.

The fire door begins to descend between the hall and the glass corridor.

Serena tries to crawl under it. The cuff jerks her back.

The door slams down inches from her face.

Adrian carries Chloe through the crowd.

Serena watches him disappear through smoke and camera flashes.

Her hand slides down the glass, leaving a bloody streak.

SERENA  
My babies...

She collapses behind the glass.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 3: The Woman Who Walked Back In

INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - RECOVERY ROOM - NIGHT

Serena wakes with a tube in her arm and a bandage around one wrist.

She reaches for her stomach before she opens her eyes.

Flat.

She pulls at the blanket.

SERENA  
No. No, no.

A doctor catches her hand.

DOCTOR  
Miss Hale.

Serena looks at him.

SERENA  
Where are they?

The doctor cannot hold her eyes.

DOCTOR  
I'm sorry. The bleeding was too severe. Both fetuses were gone before surgery.

Serena's mouth opens. No sound comes out.

Her hand curls around empty sheets.

On the wall TV, the Blackwell launch aftermath plays.

Adrian stands at a podium with Chloe wrapped in a blanket beside him.

ADRIAN ON TV  
Chloe Whitmore saved Blackwell tonight. Anyone who used our family name to sabotage this company will answer for it.

Chloe lowers her head into Adrian's shoulder.

Serena stares at the screen until her eyes go dry.

The door opens.

Marcus Arden walks in with two men behind him.

He sets a black folder on the bed.

MARCUS  
Your husband left through the front entrance. I pulled you out through the loading dock.

Serena does not answer.

Marcus opens the folder. Inside: passports, bank cards, a new identity.

MARCUS  
Serena Hale is finished. Aria North can still stand.

Serena looks at the TV again.

Chloe is smiling now.

Serena reaches for the ruined ultrasound on the bedside table. A nurse must have saved it from her fist.

She folds it once. Slowly. Cleanly.

SERENA  
What do you want?

MARCUS  
Blackwell gutted. Chloe exposed. Adrian on his knees.

Serena's breathing changes.

Marcus leans in.

MARCUS  
You built their empire in the dark. Build your own in the light.

Serena closes her hand around the black folder.

SERENA  
No. Not in the light.

She looks at the TV.

SERENA  
I want them to see it burn first.

INT. BLACKWELL FOUNDATION CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT - FIVE YEARS LATER

Crystal chandeliers. Champagne. A stage covered in white roses.

Adrian sits at the front table. Chloe sits beside him, wearing the Blackwell family brooch.

An auctioneer lifts the gavel.

AUCTIONEER  
The naming rights for the children's wing. Opening bid, ten million.

Adrian raises his paddle.

ADRIAN  
Fifteen.

Chloe smiles for the cameras.

The back doors open.

A woman in black enters.

Only her heels are visible at first. Then the hem of a black coat. Then dark glasses.

The room quiets table by table.

Adrian turns.

Chloe's smile disappears.

The woman stops at the aisle.

She does not remove her glasses.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 4: She Buys the Room

INT. BLACKWELL FOUNDATION CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT

The woman in black walks down the aisle.

No one speaks.

The auctioneer lowers the gavel without realizing it.

Chloe grips Adrian's sleeve.

CHLOE  
Who is she?

The woman reaches the front row.

She removes her glasses.

Serena Hale looks back at them.

A glass slips from Chloe's hand and breaks.

CHLOE  
Impossible.

Adrian rises.

ADRIAN  
Serena?

Serena does not answer him.

She looks at the auctioneer.

SERENA  
One hundred million.

The room explodes.

BOARD MEMBER  
Who cleared her?

The bank chairman stands from a side table.

BANK CHAIRMAN  
North Capital cleared before dinner.

Chloe forces a laugh.

CHLOE  
North Capital? Serena, you couldn't keep a marriage. Now you're pretending to buy hospitals?

Serena turns to Chloe.

Chloe lifts her chin, playing to the room.

CHLOE  
Everyone here knows what she did to Blackwell five years ago.

Serena walks up the stage steps.

The auctioneer backs away.

Serena picks up the white ribbon meant for Chloe's donor photo.

SERENA  
You want a photo?

She steps close to Chloe.

CHLOE  
Don't touch me.

Serena slaps her.

The sound cracks across the room.

Chloe hits the edge of the table. Red blooms at the corner of her mouth.

Adrian moves toward Serena.

ADRIAN  
Enough.

MARCUS  
Careful. Half your lenders came with her.

ON PLAQUE: CHLOE WHITMORE, SAVIOR OF BLACKWELL.

SERENA  
Wet paper. That's all her legacy is.

SERENA  
Five years ago, you smiled while I bled.

ADRIAN  
What did you say?

Serena finally looks at him.

SERENA  
Not tonight.

SERENA  
One hundred million. And I want the children's wing renamed before sunrise.

AUCTIONEER  
Sold.

The gavel comes down.

Chloe stands in front of the cameras with blood on her lip and no plaque behind her.

Adrian stares at Serena like he is seeing a ghost with a pulse.

Serena stands barefoot in front of floor-to-ceiling windows. Below her, Blackwell Tower glows like a crown she has already touched.

Marcus pours whiskey behind her.

MARCUS  
You made him look small tonight.

SERENA  
Not small enough.

The elevator doors burst open.

Chloe storms in with six private guards.

CHLOE  
Get out of my city, Serena.

Serena does not turn.

CHLOE  
You hear me? Adrian let you live once. I won't.

Marcus moves.

Serena lifts one finger. He stops.

Chloe snatches a framed photo from the table: North Capital's first acquisition.

She throws it. Glass shatters against the wall beside Serena's head.

Serena turns.

A small cut opens on her cheek.

Chloe sees the blood and smiles.

CHLOE  
There. Still human.

Serena walks toward her.

Chloe's guards step forward.

The penthouse side doors open.

North Capital security floods in, fast and silent.

Chloe's men are slammed against the floor before they can draw.

Chloe backs up.

SERENA  
You came to my floor with borrowed men.

Serena catches Chloe by the hair and drags her to the window.

Chloe screams and grabs at Serena's wrist.

SERENA  
Look down.

Below, reporters crowd the sidewalk, cameras pointed up.

SERENA  
Wave.

CHLOE  
You're insane.

Serena forces Chloe's palm against the glass.

SERENA  
Five years ago, who told the investigators I was running?

Chloe clenches her jaw.

Serena presses harder. Chloe's cheek flattens against the window.

SERENA  
Who moved the red folder?

Chloe's eyes flick to the right.

Serena follows the look.

On Chloe's phone screen, a message preview glows before it locks.

TEXT PREVIEW: Mara - Side entrance cameras are gone.

SERENA  
There it is.

Serena lets go.

Chloe drops to her knees, gasping.

SERENA  
Run back to Adrian. Tell him I am done buying rooms.

Serena crouches, face close.

SERENA  
Next, I buy witnesses.

Chloe crawls backward, one heel broken, hair torn loose.

At the elevator, she looks back with wet hatred.

CHLOE  
He will never choose you.

Serena's hand goes unconsciously to her lower stomach.

The movement is tiny.

Marcus sees it.

Serena catches herself and drops her hand.

SERENA  
He already made his choice.

CUT TO BLACK.

Serena walks toward her car with two bodyguards.

The garage lights snap on row by row.

Adrian stands between her and the car.

His security closes the exits.

Serena does not stop.

SERENA  
Move.

ADRIAN  
Who are you now?

SERENA  
Someone you can't remove with a signature.

Adrian steps close.

ADRIAN  
Five years ago, you disappeared.

SERENA  
No.

She looks him in the eye.

SERENA  
You left.

Adrian reaches for her arm.

Serena lets him catch her wrist.

Then his other hand brushes her lower abdomen by accident.

Serena flinches so violently her back hits the car.

For a second, the mask drops.

FLASH CUT:

Smoke behind glass. A cuff chain jerks Serena's wrist. Blood hits white tile.

BACK TO SCENE.

Adrian sees it.

ADRIAN  
Serena.

Serena slaps his hand away.

SERENA  
Don't touch there.

Her sleeve rides up. Burn scars twist around one wrist where the cuff cut deep.

Adrian stares.

ADRIAN  
What happened to you after that night?

Serena laughs, but her eyes shine.

SERENA  
You signed my name into a red folder. You watched me bleed behind glass. What else do you need?

ADRIAN  
I didn't know.

Serena steps close enough that he can see the scar below her collarbone from the torn badge.

SERENA  
You didn't want to know.

As the car pulls away, Adrian stands in the headlights until his driver yells.

INT. ST. AGNES PRIVATE HOSPITAL - LOST PROPERTY ROOM - NIGHT

Adrian pushes through a narrow door behind an old nurse.

A security guard blocks the inner archive cage.

The nurse does not open it.

NURSE

The nurse does not look at it.

NURSE  
Your name is why it was sealed.

Through the cage, he sees a row of gray evidence boxes.

ON LABEL: HALE, SERENA - BLACKWELL TOWER INCIDENT - PERSONAL EFFECTS.

Below it, in small handwriting:

TWO INFANT WRISTBANDS. ULTRASOUND PHOTO. NO FAMILY PRESENT.

Adrian's hand tightens around the bar until his knuckles bleach white.

Chloe.

Chloe.

## Episode 10: The Live Button

INT. NORTH CAPITAL - PRESS ROOM - NIGHT

Rows of empty chairs. A black backdrop. Three cameras on tripods.

Serena stands behind the podium.

Adrian kneels on the floor below her.

The white hospital box sits between them.

Marcus watches from the shadows by the door.

Serena places a phone on the podium.

ON PHONE: GO LIVE.

She does not press it.

SERENA  
Say it first.

Adrian lifts his head.

ADRIAN  
Five years ago, I signed the referral.

Serena's face does not move.

ADRIAN  
I watched my pregnant wife beg behind glass.

His voice breaks.

He swallows it down.

ADRIAN  
I chose Chloe.

Serena's fingers tighten around the phone.

ADRIAN  
Serena lost our twins because I walked away.

Marcus looks at Serena.

MARCUS  
You don't owe him a stage.

Serena keeps her eyes on Adrian.

SERENA  
No. He owes me one.

Adrian reaches into his jacket.

Serena's eyes harden.

SERENA  
If that's paper, keep it.

He takes out his phone instead.

The screen is unlocked to Blackwell's internal live channel.

ON PHONE: BROADCAST TO ALL EMPLOYEES, LENDERS, SHAREHOLDERS.

ADRIAN  
No lawyers. No statement.

ADRIAN  
Press live, and I bury the name myself.

For one second, her throat moves.

Adrian sees it and crawls one step forward.

ADRIAN  
Serena.

She steps back.

The heel of her shoe stops beside the white box.
