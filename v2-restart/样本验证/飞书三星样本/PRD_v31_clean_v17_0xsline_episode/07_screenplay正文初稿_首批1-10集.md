## Episode 1

### INT. CROSS FOUNDATION - BOARD PREP ROOM - NIGHT

Serena Vale stands alone at the long table, marking a red clause in a donor agreement. A folded ultrasound photo is tucked beneath her palm.

The doors open. Adrian Cross walks in with Bianca Rose on his arm. Bianca wears a pale maternity dress and Serena's old foundation pin.

Adrian places two folders in front of Serena.

Adrian: Sign both.

Serena opens the first folder: divorce papers. The second: foundation resignation.

Serena: The emergency grant closes tonight. If you remove my signature, CrossCare loses the state network.

Adrian: You were never the reason this family survived.

Bianca presses a hand to her stomach and sways.

Bianca: Adrian... the baby.

Adrian grabs Serena's wrist and pins her hand to the table. The ultrasound bends under her fingers.

Adrian: Stop making this about you.

Serena looks at Bianca. Bianca's eyes are wet for Adrian and sharp for Serena.

Serena signs. Her pen cuts through the paper.

Adrian takes the donor badge from Serena's jacket and clips it onto Bianca.

Adrian: The Cross name needs a mother people can trust.

Serena folds the ultrasound into a small square and closes her fist around it.

Later, Serena walks through the service corridor with one suitcase. Behind the glass wall, Bianca lifts a phone.

Bianca: Dr. Crane? She just left. Make sure she doesn't walk out clean.

## Episode 2

### INT. CROSSCARE OLD MATERNITY WING - NIGHT

Red alarms flash. Sprinklers hiss over the locked archive corridor.

Serena pounds on a glass fire door. Smoke rolls behind her.

Serena: Adrian! I'm in here!

Downstairs, Bianca lies on the lobby floor, one hand clutching her stomach. Adrian rushes in with security.

Bianca: It hurts. Adrian, please.

A guard points to the old wing monitor.

Guard: Mrs. Cross is trapped in the sealed corridor.

Adrian snatches the master badge from him.

Adrian: Open Bianca's route first. Keep Serena contained until police get here.

The guard hesitates.

Guard: Sir, she's in the smoke zone.

Adrian: She broke into the system. She can wait.

Serena slams the ultrasound photo against the glass.

Serena: Adrian! I'm pregnant!

Adrian turns. For one second, he looks toward the old wing.

Bianca screams.

Bianca: Adrian!

Adrian carries Bianca out through the open emergency route. The light above Serena's door stays red.

Serena slides down the glass. Smoke covers the ultrasound. Blood spreads across her white sleeve.

The last thing she hears is the door lock clicking harder.

## Episode 3

### INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL ROOM - NIGHT

Serena wakes with bandages around her wrists. Her first move is to touch her stomach.

Doctor: Mrs. Vale...

Serena: Don't call me that. My babies?

The doctor cannot look at her.

Doctor: We lost them both.

The room goes silent.

Mason Reed enters and places a tablet beside her. On screen, Adrian stands beside Bianca at a press event.

Adrian on screen: Bianca and our child will have everything Cross can give.

Serena removes her hospital wristband. There are two tiny blank baby bands beside it.

Mason: I can bury CrossCare for you.

Serena closes the bands inside a black Vesper case.

Serena: No. Build me a system they cannot breathe without.

### EXT. CROSS LEGACY GALA - FIVE YEARS LATER - NIGHT

Cameras flash. A reporter turns as a black car stops at the carpet.

Serena steps out in a silver suit. The Vesper logo glows on her invitation.

The Cross security gate scans her palm. Every screen turns green.

WELCOME, SERENA VALE. VESPER CONTROLLING CHAIR.

Inside, Adrian looks up.

## Episode 4

### INT. CROSS LEGACY GALA - NIGHT

The gala has frozen. The donation wall reads: AWAITING VESPER APPROVAL.

Adrian approaches Serena.

Adrian: You have ten seconds to explain why my gala stopped.

Serena: Your gala? That's sweet.

Bianca steps forward, wearing the Cross foundation pin.

Bianca: You were thrown out years ago. Crawling back with a rented dress doesn't make you important.

Serena looks at the pin. She reaches out and unclasps it from Bianca's chest.

Bianca gasps.

Serena drops the pin into Bianca's champagne flute. It sinks with a soft clink.

Serena: Cross charity property. You were never cleared to wear it.

Adrian: Enough.

He gestures to security. No one moves.

Mason enters, handing Serena a tablet.

Mason: Vesper network authorization is waiting on you.

Serena signs with one finger. The wall changes.

CROSSCARE CERTIFICATION: THIRTY PERCENT SUSPENDED.

The donors whisper. A board member steps away from Adrian.

Adrian: What the hell are you?

Serena stops beside him.

Serena: The woman you locked out.

## Episode 5

### INT. CROSS BOARDROOM - MORNING

Phones ring across the table. Executives talk over one another.

Executive: Three hospital vendors paused delivery.

Executive: Insurers are asking for Vesper clearance.

Executive: Our West Coast maternal network is offline.

Adrian calls Serena. She answers on speaker.

Adrian: Undo this.

Serena: You cannot afford what I want.

She hangs up.

### INT. VESPER HOTEL SUITE - NIGHT

Bianca storms in with two private guards.

Bianca: You stole Cross property.

Serena sits by the window, calm.

Serena: This suite has a compliance panic lock. Thank you for triggering it.

The doors shut. Bianca's guards are stopped by hotel security.

Serena places a damage form on the coffee table.

Serena: You broke the door. Sign.

Bianca: Adrian will ruin you.

Serena presses Bianca's charity card to the terminal. DECLINED flashes on the screen.

Serena: He should hurry. Your name is getting expensive.

### INT. ADRIAN'S OFFICE - LATE NIGHT

Adrian watches old access logs. One line flickers.

DR. LYLE CRANE - MANUAL OVERRIDE - MATERNITY WING.

Adrian's face hardens.

## Episode 6

### INT. UNDERGROUND PARKING GARAGE - NIGHT

Serena walks toward her car. Adrian steps out from the shadows.

Adrian: Five years ago. Where did you go after the fire?

Serena: Away from the locked door.

Adrian: Serena.

She turns. Her sleeve slips, showing a raised burn scar around her wrist.

Adrian stares.

Serena: You don't recognize your family's work?

Adrian: I didn't know.

Serena steps closer.

Serena: You did not leave a wife behind. You left a locked door.

She gets into the car. The taillights vanish.

### INT. CROSS PRIVATE ARCHIVE - NIGHT

Adrian tears through sealed medical folders.

He freezes on a damaged page.

SERENA VALE. PREGNANCY CONFIRMED. EMERGENCY LOSS SUSPECTED.

The rest is blacked out.

Adrian's hand shakes.

Adrian: Open the old maternity wing.

## Episode 7

### INT. CROSS LEGACY GALA - NIGHT

Bianca stands with a little boy beside the Cross heir bell. Donors smile. Cameras wait.

Serena enters with Dr. Lyle Crane. His doctor badge hangs from a shaking hand.

Adrian: Serena, not here.

Serena: Where else should a family lie be born?

She points to the sealed display beside the bell: CROSSCARE OLD WING ACCESS HISTORY.

Dr. Crane swipes his badge. The screen opens.

Two records appear from the night of the fire.

BIANCA ROSE PRENATAL FILE CREATED - 8:41 PM.  
OLD MATERNITY WING LOCKDOWN OVERRIDE - 8:44 PM.

The same doctor badge.

The room murmurs.

Bianca clutches Adrian's arm.

Bianca: She forged this.

Adrian steps in front of her.

Adrian: This is a Cross event. You don't get to drag my family through mud.

Serena looks at Dr. Crane.

Serena: Say who paid you.

Dr. Crane breaks.

Dr. Crane: Bianca. She paid me to create the pregnancy file. She paid me to alter the old-wing access log.

Bianca: Liar!

Serena nods toward the boy.

Serena: Then let the Cross heir ring the bell.

## Episode 8

### INT. CROSS LEGACY GALA - CONTINUOUS

The little boy places his palm inside the gold biometric lock.

The bell stays silent.

A red line appears.

NOT ELIGIBLE FOR CROSS FAMILY TRUST.

Adrian takes the boy's hand and tries again.

Red.

Bianca's breathing turns ragged.

Donors stare. Cameras lower, then rise again.

Serena removes the Cross heir pin from the boy's jacket and holds it out to Adrian.

Serena: If you still believe her, put it back on him.

Adrian looks at the boy. Then at Bianca.

His hand closes around the pin. He does not put it back.

Bianca drops to her knees.

Bianca: Adrian, please. He's still your son in every way that matters.

Adrian: Whose blood is he?

Bianca: Don't do this.

Adrian's voice breaks.

Adrian: Whose?

Bianca points at Serena with a shaking finger.

Bianca: Ask her about blood. Ask her about the babies you left behind that night. You killed the real ones, Adrian. You killed yours.

The pin slips from Adrian's hand and hits the marble floor.

Serena watches him.

Serena: Now you are starting to hear me.

## Episode 9

### INT. CROSSCARE OLD MATERNITY WING - NIGHT

Adrian stands before the sealed fire door. The corridor is dark except for his phone light.

He tries the handle. It is jammed.

Adrian pulls until metal cuts his palm. The door groans open.

Inside, the old alarm panel flickers to life.

Recorded Adrian: Open Bianca's route first. Keep Serena contained until police get here.

Adrian goes still.

Recorded Serena: Adrian! I'm pregnant!

The recording ends in static.

Adrian stumbles into Room 12.

On a rusted shelf: a warped ultrasound photo, two tiny hospital bands marked BABY VALE-CROSS A and BABY VALE-CROSS B, and half of a knitted blanket.

Flashback: Serena on a sofa, holding the blanket up.

Serena: I know it's early, but I wanted them to have something soft.

Adrian, not looking: Boring.

Back in the room, Adrian sinks to the floor.

He takes off his CEO badge and slams it into the fire door. Once. Twice. Again. The metal bends.

Adrian: I kept the door shut.

His voice breaks.

Adrian: I killed them.

## Episode 10

### INT. CROSS ANNUAL BOARD DINNER - NIGHT

The board dinner is mid-toast. Serena stands at the front with Mason.

Mason: Vesper is prepared to assume emergency control of the CrossCare network.

The doors bang open.

Adrian walks in, shirt smeared with old ash and fresh blood from his hand. He carries a small black box.

The room falls silent.

Adrian reaches the board table and sets down his bent CEO badge, the Cross family seal, and his voting proxy.

Adrian: Serena Vale has full proxy over my shares and my seat.

Board Member: Adrian, don't do this here.

Adrian removes the nameplate that reads ADRIAN CROSS - CEO and places it in front of Serena.

Adrian: I should have lost the name five years ago.

He opens the black box. Inside are the two baby bands.

Serena's face does not change.

Adrian: I am sorry.

Serena: Sorry is what men say when they want the room to move on.

She lifts the live microphone from the podium and holds it out.

Serena: Say what you did.

Adrian takes the mic. His knuckles whiten.

Adrian: Five years ago, I ordered a locked door kept shut. My pregnant wife was behind it.

The lights cut out.

The old Cross emergency siren screams through the ballroom.

The screens go black.
