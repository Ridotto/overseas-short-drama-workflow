# screenplay 正文初稿_首批1-10集

> 本稿按 `06_场景施工稿_首批1-10集.md` 展开。`EP7-9` 的证据只做触发器，主戏落到人物被迫动作和可见损失。

## EPISODE 1: THE SEAT THAT WAS HERS

INT. VALE BAY ESTATE - DINING HALL - NIGHT

Serena stands at the entrance with a folded clinic paper in her hand.

Adrian enters with Chloe. His palm rests on Chloe's stomach.

ADRIAN
Chloe is carrying the Vale heir.

The room rises for Chloe. Serena does not move.

A trustee slides Serena's nameplate away from the main table. A servant's side door opens behind her.

Chloe smiles and holds out her hand.

CHLOE
She should not have to wear the family pin tonight.

Adrian steps to Serena. He unhooks the Vale access pin from her dress and fastens it to Chloe.

Serena's fingers close around the clinic paper.

A waiter bumps her elbow. Red wine spills across the page. The word `pregnant` disappears first.

CUT.

## EPISODE 2: LEAVE HER BURNING

EXT. HARBOR BRIDGE - NIGHT

A cold-chain tanker jackknifes. Metal screams. Fire blooms under the bridge.

Serena is trapped behind cracked glass. Chloe sobs from the other wreck.

SERENA
Adrian! I'm pregnant!

Adrian freezes.

Chloe reaches for him.

CHLOE
The baby...

Adrian turns away from Serena and lifts Chloe out.

Serena slams her palm against the glass. Fire fills the frame.

CUT.

## EPISODE 3: NIGHT HARBOR OPENS

INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL ROOM - NIGHT

Serena wakes to a flat, quiet room.

DOCTOR
I'm sorry.

The TV above her bed shows Adrian cutting the ribbon for Chloe's heir foundation.

Serena pulls her wedding ring off. Skin tears under it.

Viktor Sokolov places a black folder on the bed.

VIKTOR
There is a port network looking for a queen.

Five years later, at a maritime auction, every screen flashes:

`NIGHT HARBOR BID ACCEPTED`.

CUT.

## EPISODE 4: FREEZE THE LINE

INT. MARITIME CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT

Adrian's phone lights up. Three Vale cold-chain routes freeze at once.

Serena walks onto the stage and places her palm on the port console.

The room recognizes her.

Chloe's foundation shipment stalls outside the harbor. Donors turn.

CHLOE
You can't touch Vale property.

Serena slaps her once. Clean. Public.

SERENA
I designed the lock before you learned the door.

Adrian stares at her back as she leaves with the winning bid.

CUT.

## EPISODE 5: CRUSH WHAT SHE USES TO TOUCH YOU

INT. SERENA'S PENTHOUSE - NIGHT

Chloe storms in with two men.

CHLOE
Give me what you took.

The wall opens. Serena's security surrounds them.

Chloe reaches for Serena's old ring on the table.

Serena pins Chloe's hand under her heel.

SERENA
You lived on a child.
Then we start with the child card.

Chloe bites back a scream.

CUT.

## EPISODE 6: TEN WEEKS

INT. UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

Adrian blocks Serena with a gun.

ADRIAN
Tell me what happened that night.

Serena rolls up her sleeve. Burn scars cross her skin.

SERENA
You were there.

Later, in the Vale archive, Adrian tears through sealed emergency files.

One line stops him:

`SERENA QUINN - 10 WEEKS PREGNANT`.

The paper wrinkles in his fist.

CUT.

## EPISODE 7: HOLD THE LIE YOURSELF

INT. VALE FOUNDATION GALA - NIGHT

Chloe stands beneath a giant portrait of her son.

Serena walks in with Dr. Pierce. No speech. No long confession.

She puts a maternity verification wristband in Adrian's hand.

SERENA
Put it in yourself.

Every donor watches.

Adrian steps to the heir display. He inserts the band.

The light stays dead.

Chloe's maternity donor badge flashes red.

Adrian reaches for the power switch and kills the display.

Serena lets him do it.

SERENA
There.
You held the lie with your own hand.

She places a registry invitation on the stage.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Tomorrow, bring the heir.

Chloe's face drains.

CUT.

## EPISODE 8: TAKE OFF THE NAME

INT. VALE TRUST HALL - DAY

The heir registry ceremony is already live.

Adrian tries to step back.

SERENA
If he is Vale blood, this room becomes hers forever.
Press it.

Trustees wait. Cameras wait. Chloe clutches the child.

Adrian pushes the sealed swab into the registry port.

The machine hums.

Chloe keeps whispering.

CHLOE
He's yours. He's yours.

The screen flashes:

`NO VALE PATERNAL MATCH`.

Silence hits the hall.

Serena does not explain.

Adrian walks to the child and removes the gold `Vale Heir` tag from his jacket.

Chloe's `Mother of the Heir` pin drops to the marble.

Adrian turns to Chloe.

ADRIAN
What else did you make me bury?

Serena places a rusted harbor key in his palm.

SERENA
Open what you locked away.

CUT.

## EPISODE 9: TWO HEARTBEATS

INT. ABANDONED HARBOR STORAGE - NIGHT

Adrian unlocks the storage alone.

The old Vale signet fits the safe.

The first tray slides out. A burned baby sweater.

The second tray. An ultrasound. Two circles marked in Serena's handwriting.

The third box falls open.

Two tiny unused harbor heir tags hit the floor.

Adrian bends to pick them up. His hand fails.

He drops to his knees.

No one tells him what it means.

He finally understands.

EXT. OLD HARBOR STREET - RAIN

Serena's car stops.

Adrian kneels in the rain, the two tags pressed to his chest.

ADRIAN
Serena...

That is all he can say.

CUT.

## EPISODE 10: ONE LIFE FOR TWO

INT. ROOFTOP REVOLVING RESTAURANT - NIGHT

Serena's victory dinner is still on livestream.

The doors open.

Adrian enters soaked, carrying the Vale trust seal, berth deeds, and voting transfer.

He drops to both knees.

ADRIAN
Take it. Take all of it.

He lays the empire at Serena's shoes.

Serena looks at the documents, then at the two tiny tags in his hand.

She pushes a gun back toward him.

SERENA
Two heartbeats.
One life.
Pull it yourself.

Adrian lifts the gun.

CUT TO BLACK.
