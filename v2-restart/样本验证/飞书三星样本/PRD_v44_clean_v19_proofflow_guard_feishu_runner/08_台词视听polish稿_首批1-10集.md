# 台词视听polish稿_首批1-10集

> 本稿只压台词和视听动作，不改 `S5` 主结构。`EP7-9` 保留 proof-flow guard：证据触发动作，动作制造损失。

## EPISODE 1: THE SEAT THAT WAS HERS

INT. VALE BAY ESTATE - DINING HALL - NIGHT

Serena stops at the dining room doors, one folded clinic slip hidden in her palm.

Adrian walks in with Chloe under the chandeliers. His hand is on Chloe's stomach.

ADRIAN
Chloe is carrying the Vale heir.

The room stands for Chloe.

No one stands for Serena.

A trustee removes Serena's nameplate from the main table. The service door opens behind her.

Chloe touches the family pin on Serena's dress.

CHLOE
That must feel heavy now.

Adrian unhooks the pin himself.

Serena does not beg.

He fastens the pin to Chloe.

A waiter brushes Serena's wrist. Red wine spills over the clinic slip.

The word `pregnant` bleeds into red.

## EPISODE 2: LEAVE HER BURNING

EXT. HARBOR BRIDGE - NIGHT

A cold-chain tanker tears across the bridge. Metal folds. Fire rolls under the guardrail.

Serena is pinned behind cracked glass.

Across the wreckage, Chloe coughs just loud enough.

SERENA
Adrian!

He turns.

SERENA (CONT'D)
I'm pregnant.

Adrian hears it.

Chloe reaches from the other car.

CHLOE
Our baby...

Adrian runs to Chloe.

Serena's palm slams the glass once. Twice.

Adrian carries Chloe into the blue lights.

The fire takes Serena's hand from view.

## EPISODE 3: NIGHT HARBOR OPENS

INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL ROOM - NIGHT

Serena wakes to a room too white to forgive.

DOCTOR
I'm sorry.

The television shows Adrian cutting a ribbon beside Chloe.

`VALE HEIR FOUNDATION LAUNCH`.

Serena pulls off her wedding ring. Skin tears.

Viktor Sokolov lays a black folder on the sheet.

VIKTOR
Dead women do not owe mercy.

Serena opens the folder.

Five years later, an auction screen turns black.

`NIGHT HARBOR BID ACCEPTED`.

Serena steps into the room before anyone sees her face.

## EPISODE 4: FREEZE THE LINE

INT. MARITIME CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT

Adrian's phone vibrates once. Then every Vale executive's phone follows.

Three cold-chain routes freeze on the wall map.

Chloe's smile thins.

Serena walks onto the stage and puts her palm on the port console.

The system unlocks for her.

Chloe's foundation shipment turns red outside the harbor.

CHLOE
This is Vale property.

Serena slaps her.

Not hard enough to look messy. Hard enough for every donor to hear.

SERENA
Then ask Vale to move it.

Adrian steps toward Serena.

She does not look back.

## EPISODE 5: CRUSH WHAT SHE USES TO TOUCH YOU

INT. SERENA'S PENTHOUSE - NIGHT

Chloe bursts in with two hired men.

CHLOE
Still hiding behind Russian money?

The wall opens behind her.

Serena's security takes the men down before Chloe finishes breathing.

Chloe grabs for the old ring on the table.

Serena's heel pins her fingers to the marble.

CHLOE
Let go!

SERENA
You liked taking things with this hand.

Serena presses down.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Keep it. You will need something to crawl with.

## EPISODE 6: TEN WEEKS

INT. UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

Adrian blocks Serena beside her car.

ADRIAN
How much of this is revenge?

Serena rolls up her sleeve.

Burn scars cross the skin like rope.

SERENA
Enough to remember where you stood.

Later, in the Vale archive, Adrian rips open a sealed emergency file.

One line stops him:

`10 WEEKS PREGNANT`.

The paper folds under his thumb.

For the first time, he cannot aim blame at anyone else.

## EPISODE 7: HOLD THE LIE YOURSELF

INT. VALE FOUNDATION GALA - NIGHT

Chloe stands beneath the portrait of her son.

CHLOE
For every mother who protects the future--

The doors open.

Serena enters with Dr. Pierce.

No prepared video. No speech.

Serena puts a maternity verification wristband into Adrian's hand.

SERENA
Use it.

Adrian looks at the donors, then at Chloe.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Or tell them the heir never needed one.

Adrian walks to the gala heir display.

He inserts the band.

The cradle light stays dark.

Chloe's maternity donor badge flashes red.

Adrian reaches for the switch and kills the display.

A murmur runs through the room.

Serena watches his hand on the dead switch.

SERENA
You always know where the fire is.
You just choose what to save.

She leaves a registry invitation on the stage.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Tomorrow. Bring the heir.

## EPISODE 8: TAKE OFF THE NAME

INT. VALE TRUST HALL - DAY

The Vale crest glows over the heir registry.

Chloe holds the child in front of the trustees.

Adrian stands frozen beside the sample port.

SERENA
If he is Vale blood, she wins.

She steps back.

SERENA (CONT'D)
So let Vale decide.

Adrian pushes the sealed swab into the registry.

Chloe whispers through a smile.

CHLOE
He's yours.

The registry hums.

`NO VALE PATERNAL MATCH`.

No one speaks.

Serena does not explain the machine.

Adrian walks to the child. His fingers shake around the gold `VALE HEIR` tag.

He removes it.

The tag lands in his palm like it weighs more than the room.

Chloe's `MOTHER OF THE HEIR` pin unlocks and drops to the marble.

ADRIAN
What else?

Chloe cannot answer.

Serena places a rusted harbor key in Adrian's open hand.

SERENA
Open what you locked away.

## EPISODE 9: TWO HEARTBEATS

INT. ABANDONED HARBOR STORAGE - NIGHT

Adrian unlocks the door alone.

No lawyer. No guards. No Chloe.

His old Vale signet opens the safe.

A burned baby sweater slides out first.

Adrian stops breathing.

The second tray holds an ultrasound.

Two circles.

Two names that were never finished.

The third box slips from the tray. Two tiny harbor heir tags scatter across the floor.

Adrian reaches for them.

His hand misses once.

He gets them on the second try and breaks at the waist.

There is no voiceover. No explanation.

The room tells him.

EXT. OLD HARBOR STREET - RAIN

Serena's car stops.

Adrian kneels in the headlights, two tiny tags pressed to his chest.

ADRIAN
Serena.

She looks at the tags. Then at him.

She does not get out.

## EPISODE 10: ONE LIFE FOR TWO

INT. ROOFTOP REVOLVING RESTAURANT - NIGHT

The victory dinner is still live.

Adrian enters soaked through.

In his arms: the Vale trust seal, berth deeds, voting transfer.

He drops to his knees before the cameras.

ADRIAN
Take Vale.
Leave me whatever punishment is left.

Serena looks at the empire on the floor.

Then at the two tiny tags in his fist.

She pushes a gun across the table.

SERENA
Two heartbeats.

Adrian looks up.

SERENA (CONT'D)
One life.

She turns the muzzle toward him.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Pull it yourself.

Adrian lifts the gun.

CUT TO BLACK.
