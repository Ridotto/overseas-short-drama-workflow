# 首批台词 / 视听 polish 稿（EP1-10）

## 本层说明

- 只处理表达，不改主结构。
- 重点动作：
  - 压掉解释句；
  - 拉开 Serena / Adrian / Chloe / Viktor 的声线；
  - 让狠话更像策略，不像作者总结。

---

# EPISODE 1: WRONG WIFE, WRONG HEIRESS

## INT. VALE MANOR KITCHEN - NIGHT

SERENA QUINN locks an encrypted route tablet behind a spice rack, then touches the ULTRASOUND tucked inside her blazer.

Black truffle pasta simmers. She opens a voice memo.

SERENA
(soft, testing it)
Adrian... we're--

The front doors slam.

ADRIAN VALE walks in with CHLOE HART on his arm. Chloe's palm rests over her polished baby bump like a crown.

Adrian stops at the kitchen island.

ADRIAN
Don't plate dinner.

Serena turns with a spoon in her hand.

SERENA
You brought company.

CHLOE
Only the woman who's staying.

Adrian drops DIVORCE PAPERS onto the marble.

ADRIAN
Sign them.
Chloe and the heir move in tonight.

Serena's grip tightens on the spoon.

SERENA
You waited until I was cooking for you.
That's almost thoughtful.

ADRIAN
You wanted honesty.
There it is.

Chloe notices the hospital print edge in Serena's pocket.

CHLOE
I didn't want this ugly.

SERENA
Then why are you standing in my kitchen?

Chloe gives a small, deliberate sway and gasps.

CHLOE
Adrian--

Adrian catches her, then wheels on Serena.

ADRIAN
Enough.

SERENA
I didn't touch her.

ADRIAN
You don't have to.
You make rooms rot on your own.

He SLAPS her. Sauce spatters. The spoon clatters away.

Serena tastes blood. She reaches into her pocket.

SERENA
I was going to show you--

Adrian doesn't even look.

ADRIAN
Whatever game that is, keep it.

Serena stares at him. Then she crushes the ultrasound into a tight ball, shoves it into her mouth, and swallows.

Chloe's eyes widen.

SERENA
There. Solved.

She signs.

SERENA (CONT'D)
You wanted me gone.
Try not to choke on how easy that was.

## INT. VALE MANOR BEDROOM / FOYER - NIGHT

Serena strips framed wedding photos from the wall as she packs. Glass cracks under her suitcase wheels.

At the door, Adrian waits like staff.

ADRIAN
The driver will take you to the airport.

SERENA
Still outsourcing difficult work.

Chloe lingers on the staircase.

CHLOE
Serena--

Serena doesn't turn.

SERENA
Save the sweet voice.
You might need it later.

She exits.

## EXT. VALE MANOR COURTYARD / BALCONY - NIGHT

The sedan pulls away through the gates.

Above, Chloe steps into shadow and uses a second phone.

CHLOE
Bridge route. Now.
No survivors if you can help it.

She hangs up, smiling as the taillights disappear.

CUT TO BLACK.

# EPISODE 2: BRIDGE OF FIRE

## EXT. HARBOR BRIDGE - NIGHT

Rain needles the windshield.

Serena sits alone in the back seat, thumb over her stomach, plane ticket creased in her fist.

Her phone screen glows with an unsent text:

`You chose wrong.`

Headlights explode behind them.

A VALE COLD CHAIN TANKER smashes into the sedan.

Metal folds. The car whips into the barrier. Serena slams sideways, breath gone.

Fuel drips under the twisted frame.

Ahead, Chloe's SUV sits wrecked at just the right angle.

## EXT. ACCIDENT SITE - NIGHT

Vale security floods the bridge. Adrian is out before the tires stop rolling.

A GUARD fights Serena's jammed door.

GUARD
Sir! Serena's trapped!

Then Chloe cries out.

CHLOE (O.S.)
Adrian... my baby...

Serena pounds the window.

SERENA
Adrian!

He looks at her.

SERENA (CONT'D)
I'm pregnant.

Chloe reaches from the SUV, blood bright at her hairline.

CHLOE
Please--

Adrian tears a tire iron from a guard, smashes Chloe's window, and pulls her free.

SERENA
Adrian! Look at me!

He does. His face shuts like steel.

ADRIAN
You wanted drama.
Stay and watch it burn.

SERENA
I didn't do this!

CHLOE
(clinging to him)
Take me home.

Adrian turns away with Chloe in his arms.

SERENA
Please!

He never breaks stride.

## INT./EXT. SEDAN / HARBOR BRIDGE - CONTINUOUS

Smoke thickens. Fire crawls under the chassis.

Serena claws at the seat belt release, slams her shoulder into the dead door, coughs.

The convoy lights fade into the rain.

She stops fighting just long enough to watch him leave.

The flame catches.

The sedan BLOWS.

WHITE OUT.

# EPISODE 3: NIGHT HARBOR RISES

## INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL SUITE - NIGHT

Serena jolts awake and reaches for the flatness under the blanket.

A DOCTOR steps in before hope can form.

DOCTOR
Don't.

She reads the answer on his face anyway.

SERENA
No.

The doctor lowers his eyes.

VIKTOR SOKOLOV enters without asking permission. He switches on the TV.

On-screen: Adrian beside Chloe at a foundation event.

ADRIAN (ON TV)
My family protects its own.
My child will inherit more than I did.

Serena doesn't blink.

Viktor places a black PORT ACCESS TOKEN beside her.

VIKTOR
He built a throne on your hands and doesn't even know the shape of them.

Serena watches Adrian raise a glass for another woman's child.

VIKTOR (CONT'D)
Lie here as Serena Quinn.
Or get up as Night Harbor.

Serena takes a scalpel from the tray, slices her palm, and stains the token with blood.

SERENA
Book the ship.

## INT. MARITIME RELIEF AUCTION - FIVE YEARS LATER - NIGHT

Glittering donors. Old money. Cameras.

Adrian lifts his paddle.

ADRIAN
Twelve million.

Chloe smiles for the room. Their boy sits nearby in a miniature tuxedo.

The great doors open.

Heels on marble.

A corridor parts around Serena. No smile. No softness. No wife left.

Adrian looks up.

His breath catches.

CUT.

# EPISODE 4: THE WOMAN WHO OWNS THE HARBOR

## INT. AUCTION HALL - CONTINUOUS

The AUCTIONEER blinks at Serena.

AUCTIONEER
Do you have a number, ma'am?

SERENA
One hundred million.

Shock tears through the room.

ADRIAN
That berth belongs to Vale.

SERENA
Then Vale should've learned to keep it.

Chloe finally places the face.

CHLOE
God. It really is you.
You came back dressed expensive.

SERENA
And you still sound rented.

Chloe steps closer.

CHLOE
You were thrown out for a reason.

Serena SLAPS her across the face. Hard enough to send her into the edge of the bid table.

Phones rise.

ADRIAN
Serena.

He draws his gun.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
Not one more step.

Serena throws a slim folder into his chest. He catches it.

Route freezes. Berth seizures. Terminal suspensions.

SERENA
Pull the trigger.
At midnight, your reefer line dies in six countries.

Adrian's hand tightens.

Serena closes the distance and presses the gun muzzle to her own sternum.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Do it.
You had better light last time.

He can't fire.

Serena lowers the barrel with two fingers.

SERENA (CONT'D)
That's what I thought.

She walks away while the room watches Adrian fail to be dangerous.

# EPISODE 5: CRUSH WHAT SHE USES TO TOUCH YOU

## INT. VALE STUDY - NIGHT

Route maps cover the walls. Rain streaks the windows.

Adrian flips through a dossier of Serena's rise:

- dock ownership transfers
- cold storage acquisitions
- customs breaches rerouted through shell firms
- photos of Serena in hard hat and black coat

CHIEF OF STAFF
Night Harbor didn't inherit this.
She built it.

Adrian keeps staring at one photo: Serena stepping off a vessel she clearly commands.

ADRIAN
Get out.

He calls her himself.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
Come back.
The manor is still yours.

SERENA (V.O.)
No. It was never mine.
That's why it burned so easily.

Click.

A SECURITY DIRECTOR bursts in.

SECURITY DIRECTOR
Three West Coast reefer hubs just switched off Vale routing.
European partners are freezing us out.

Adrian lowers the phone slowly.

## INT. SKYSHORE PRESIDENTIAL SUITE - NIGHT

Chloe storms in with private guards.

CHLOE
Break every mirror.
I want her looking cheap before I see her bleed.

Serena sits with a wine glass and doesn't rise.

SERENA
You brought employees.
I brought loyalty.

Secret doors slide open. Night Harbor security floods the suite and crushes Chloe's men in seconds.

One goes through a coffee table. Another is pinned to the floor by the neck.

Chloe stumbles back. Serena finally stands, crosses to her, and plants a stiletto on Chloe's hand.

CHLOE
Adrian will--

Serena presses down. Bone POPS.

CHLOE (CONT'D)
Please--

SERENA
You used this hand to sign my death warrant.

She leans closer.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Now it remembers me.

Chloe sobs. Serena lifts her heel.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Leave before I start charging interest properly.

# EPISODE 6: LOOK AT WHAT BURNED

## INT. UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

Serena's convoy stops. Vale security closes in.

Adrian steps between pillars, gun low but ready.

ADRIAN
Where were you for five years?

SERENA
Somewhere useful.

ADRIAN
You were my wife.

SERENA
Not on the bridge.

She signals her security to stand down first.

Her sleeve slips. A river of burned skin twists around her wrist.

Adrian sees it and loses the next breath.

SERENA (CONT'D)
There.
Since you liked not looking.

ADRIAN
Serena--

SERENA
That name died screaming at a car window.

She gets into the vehicle.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Night Harbor doesn't ask twice.

The convoy rolls away.

## INT. VALE ARCHIVE ROOM - LATER

Flood-warped boxes. Mold. Ruined shelving.

Adrian tears through emergency records with his bare hands until he finds Serena's intake chart.

The line stops him cold:

`10 weeks pregnant`

Below it: trauma. fetal loss.

He reads it again. Then again, as if the letters might move.

ADRIAN
No.

His voice is smaller now. A man's after the war has already gone wrong.

# EPISODE 7: YOU CHOSE THE LIE AGAIN

## INT. VALE FOUNDATION GALA - NIGHT

Donors mingle under chandeliers. A giant display screen loops images of Adrian, Chloe, and the smiling boy she calls his heir.

Serena walks in with DR. PIERCE and the former VALE DRIVER.

She doesn't wait for music to stop.

SERENA
Keep every camera on.

Her security shoves both witnesses under the stage lights.

Chloe's smile cracks.

CHLOE
What are you doing?

SERENA
Taking your maternity ward public.

The giant screen flickers to:

- Chloe entering Dr. Pierce's clinic
- a wire transfer
- a route map of the Harbor Bridge
- the driver pocketing cash

DR. PIERCE
She paid me to fake the scans.

DRIVER
And the bridge timing.

The room erupts.

Adrian doesn't go to Serena. He goes to the sound booth.

ADRIAN
Kill the feed.

Serena lets that land.

SERENA
Of course.
Save the gala.
Let the dead wait.

Adrian signals security.

ADRIAN
Remove her.

SERENA
You had one choice tonight.
You picked the lie again.

She glances at the giant portrait of Chloe's son.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Tomorrow, I cut deeper.

She leaves the room with everyone still staring at Adrian, not Chloe.

# EPISODE 8: NOT YOUR SON, NOT YOUR INNOCENCE

## INT. VALE TRUST HALL - DAY

Birthday balloons, trustees, press, silver family crest.

Chloe raises a glass over Miles' cake.

CHLOE
To Vale's future.

Serena steps in with THEO KANE and a LAB TECH carrying a sealed mobile testing case.

SERENA
Don't toast until you know the bloodline.

Adrian is already on his feet.

ADRIAN
You're done here.

SERENA
Not until your family stops clapping for another man's son.

Files hit the cake table. Chloe's face drains white.

Theo steps forward.

THEO
Tell him.

CHLOE
I don't know him.

SERENA
Then the machine can meet him for you.

Fast chain-of-custody. Swabs. Seal breaks.

Miles begins to cry. Trustees lean in despite themselves.

The screen flashes:

`ADRIAN VALE - EXCLUDED`
`THEO KANE - MATCH`

Silence.

Adrian grabs Chloe by the wrist.

ADRIAN
Look at me.

CHLOE
You're hurting me--

ADRIAN
Good.
Whose son?

Theo spits the answer first.

THEO
Mine. She said your last name was worth the lie.

Chloe breaks, ugly and fast.

CHLOE
Fine! He's not yours!
But don't stand there acting clean!
You left Serena in that fire!
You left your own child in that car!

Adrian lets go as if her skin burned him.

Serena watches him fold inward.

SERENA
That's one truth.
You still don't know the whole bill.

The gun slips from Adrian's hand to the marble floor.

# EPISODE 9: TWO HEARTBEATS IN THE ASHES

## INT. ABANDONED HARBOR STORAGE - NIGHT

Adrian breaks open the hidden floor safe himself.

Inside:

- a twin ultrasound
- a half-burned baby sweater
- a tiny silver signet ring
- Serena's folded note: `Tell him tonight.`

The ultrasound reads:

`TWO FETAL HEARTBEATS`

Adrian stops moving.

Then memory hits in blades:

Serena smiling in the kitchen.
Her hand in her blazer pocket.
His palm across her face.
His back turning from the burning bridge.

He drops to his knees.

ADRIAN
Twins.

He picks up the sweater. Soot darkens his hands.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
No.

He slaps himself, once, then again harder, until blood brightens his lip.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I killed them.

His men freeze. None of them dare touch him.

## EXT. OLD PORT STREET - NIGHT

Rain lashes stone and steel.

Adrian kneels in the mud with the sweater and ultrasound crushed to his chest.

ADRIAN
Serena!

The street gives him nothing back.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I left you there.
I left all three of you there.

He bends double and screams into the storm.

Cars slow. No one steps out.

He stays on his knees like he deserves the flood.

# EPISODE 10: ONE LIFE FOR TWO

## INT. SKYLINE ROTATION RESTAURANT - NIGHT

The city turns slowly beyond glass.

Serena sits with Viktor at the best table in the room. Harbor lights burn below.

VIKTOR
Half the eastern cold chain reports to you now.

SERENA
Half doesn't interest me.

The doors slam open.

Adrian staggers in soaked, muddy, wild-eyed, carrying legal folios and the heavy SILVER TRUST SEAL.

Conversations die mid-breath.

Viktor rises.

VIKTOR
Throw him out.

Adrian shoves past him and drops to both knees at Serena's feet.

Documents spill across the floor:

- berth deeds
- trust proxies
- transfer orders

ADRIAN
Take it.

He holds up the trust seal in both hands.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
The family.
The routes.
The ports.
All of it.

Serena doesn't touch a page.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I know what I did.
I know what I burned.

He grips the hem of her dress.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
Tell me how to pay.

Serena draws a silver pistol and places it against his forehead.

SERENA
You don't buy this with paperwork.

She turns the gun and presses it into his hand instead.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Two heartbeats.
One life.
Make the math work.

Adrian takes the gun. His hand shakes so hard the barrel trembles.

Serena watches him with no mercy in her face.

Adrian lifts the gun to his own temple.

Viktor goes still.
The room goes silent.

BANG.

BLACK.
