# EPISODE 1: WRONG WIFE, WRONG HEIRESS

## INT. VALE MANOR KITCHEN - NIGHT

SERENA QUINN closes an encrypted tablet full of route maps and dock schematics. She wipes the keyboard marks off her fingers, slides the device into a hidden slot behind spice jars, then touches an ULTRASOUND PHOTO inside her blazer.

On the stove, black truffle pasta simmers.

Serena opens her voice memo app.

SERENA
(trying the words)
Adrian... we're having--

The front doors BANG open.

ADRIAN VALE walks in with CHLOE HART on his arm. Chloe's hand rests over a polished baby bump under an ivory dress.

Adrian doesn't stop in the living room. He walks Chloe all the way to Serena's island.

ADRIAN
Stop cooking.

Serena turns, still holding the spoon.

SERENA
You're home.
(sees Chloe)
Who is she?

CHLOE
I think you know.

Adrian pulls a folder from under his arm. DIVORCE PAPERS slap onto the marble island.

ADRIAN
Her name is Chloe. She's carrying my heir.
Sign.

Serena doesn't move.

SERENA
Three years. You bring her into my kitchen and call that a conversation?

ADRIAN
This stopped being your kitchen a long time ago.

Chloe glances at the sauce, the plates, the ultrasound edge peeking from Serena's pocket.

CHLOE
I told him we could do this gently.

SERENA
Then why are you standing where my husband kisses me goodnight?

Adrian's jaw hardens.

ADRIAN
Because she's the future of this house.

Serena reaches for the ultrasound photo.

SERENA
Adrian, I need to show you--

Chloe gives the island edge the slightest bump with her hip. She GASPS and staggers.

CHLOE
My baby--

Adrian catches her. He whirls on Serena.

ADRIAN
Did you touch her?

SERENA
No.

ADRIAN
Don't lie to my face.

He SLAPS her. The spoon hits the floor. Sauce spatters across the white cabinet.

Serena tastes blood.

Chloe settles into Adrian's chest, eyes glittering over his shoulder.

CHLOE
She hates me. I understand.

SERENA
No. You don't understand anything.

She pulls out the ultrasound photo. For one second Adrian sees the hospital logo.

Then Serena crushes the page in her fist, shoves it into her mouth, forces herself to swallow.

Chloe freezes. Adrian stares.

SERENA
There. Nothing for you to ruin.

She signs the divorce papers with a hard, shaking hand. Then she pushes them back.

SERENA (CONT'D)
You wanted me out.
Now watch me leave.

She walks past them.

## INT. VALE MANOR BEDROOM / FOYER - NIGHT

A suitcase rolls over hardwood. Serena strips wedding photos from frames, leaves the broken glass where it falls.

Adrian waits in the foyer, arms crossed.

ADRIAN
The car will take you to the airport.

SERENA
How generous.

CHLOE
Serena--

Serena stops without looking back.

SERENA
Don't use my name in my own house.

She exits.

## EXT. VALE MANOR COURTYARD / BALCONY - NIGHT

Serena loads her suitcase into a black sedan and gets in.

Upstairs, Chloe steps onto the balcony and pulls out a second phone.

CHLOE
(low)
She's on the Harbor Bridge now.
Make it look like panic.

The sedan disappears through the estate gates.

CUT TO BLACK.

# EPISODE 2: BRIDGE OF FIRE

## EXT. HARBOR BRIDGE - NIGHT

The black sedan speeds through rain over the lit industrial harbor.

Serena sits in the back seat, one hand over her stomach, the other around a plane ticket.

Her phone glows with an unsent message:

`You chose wrong.`

Headlights explode in the side mirror.

A VALE COLD CHAIN TANKER rams the sedan broadside.

Metal SCREAMS. The sedan slams into the barrier and spins. Glass rains across Serena's lap. Her door buckles shut.

Serena doubles over, pain ripping through her abdomen.

SERENA
No... no, no--

Fifty yards ahead, Chloe's SUV sits crooked against the rail, front smoking. Too neat. Too staged.

## EXT. ACCIDENT SITE - NIGHT

Vale security SUVs flood the bridge. Adrian jumps out before the convoy fully stops.

A guard runs to Serena's car and yanks at the twisted door.

GUARD
Mr. Vale! Serena's trapped!

From the other wreck, Chloe cries out.

CHLOE (O.S.)
Adrian!

Adrian turns toward her voice.

Serena slams both palms against the window.

SERENA
Adrian! Adrian, please!

He looks at her through shattered glass.

SERENA (CONT'D)
I'm pregnant!

Chloe reaches from her broken SUV, blood on her forehead.

CHLOE
My baby--

Adrian grabs a tire iron from a guard, smashes Chloe's window, and hauls her free.

Serena keeps pounding.

SERENA
Adrian! Look at me!

He does. His face shuts down.

ADRIAN
You wanted her scared. You got what you wanted.

SERENA
I didn't do this!

ADRIAN
Stay there and think about what you've done.

He carries Chloe toward the convoy.

SERENA
Adrian! Please! Don't leave me!

Adrian never turns around.

## INT./EXT. SEDAN / HARBOR BRIDGE - CONTINUOUS

Fuel snakes under the crushed frame.

Serena claws at the seat belt release, slips, coughs smoke.

The convoy doors slam. Headlights pull away.

Fire licks under the engine.

Serena stares at Adrian's taillights growing smaller in the rain.

Something in her face goes dead.

The car ERUPTS.

WHITE FLASH.

# EPISODE 3: NIGHT HARBOR RISES

## INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL SUITE - NIGHT

Monitors BEEP softly.

Serena wakes under bandages. Her hand flies to her stomach.

A DOCTOR steps close.

DOCTOR
Don't move too fast.

SERENA
My baby.

The doctor looks away.

DOCTOR
I'm sorry.

Silence. Then the door opens. VIKTOR SOKOLOV enters in a dark coat, carrying wet night air with him.

He clicks on the wall TV.

On-screen, Adrian stands beside Chloe at a glittering foundation event.

ADRIAN (ON TV)
The Vale family protects its own.
My child will inherit a stronger empire than I did.

Serena stops breathing for a beat.

Viktor sets a black metal PORT ACCESS TOKEN on her tray table.

VIKTOR
You built half that empire and he doesn't even know what your hands made.

Serena keeps watching Adrian smile beside Chloe.

VIKTOR (CONT'D)
Get up as Serena Quinn, and he'll bury you again.
Get up as Night Harbor, and he'll drown trying to crawl back.

Serena reaches for the surgical tray. She takes a scalpel, slices her own palm, and lets blood drip onto the token.

SERENA
Then he drowns.

## INT. MARITIME RELIEF AUCTION - FIVE YEARS LATER - NIGHT

A chandeliered hall full of shipping barons and old-money donors.

Adrian raises a paddle.

ADRIAN
Twelve million.

Chloe, now polished into high-society perfection, smiles with one hand on a five-year-old boy's shoulder.

CHLOE
You always win.

The great doors open.

Heels strike marble.

A corridor of black coats parts around Serena.

She wears a severe black gown, red lips, no softness left.

People lower their voices one by one until the room goes still.

Adrian looks up.

For the first time in five years, he sees her.

CUT.

# EPISODE 4: THE WOMAN WHO OWNS THE HARBOR

## INT. AUCTION HALL - CONTINUOUS

The AUCTIONEER falters.

AUCTIONEER
Ms... do you have a bid?

SERENA
One hundred million.

The room GASPS.

Adrian rises slowly.

ADRIAN
That's Vale Harbor property.

SERENA
No.
(beat)
It's survival.
And I own that now.

Chloe squints, then recognizes her.

CHLOE
You should've stayed dead.

Serena turns.

CHLOE (CONT'D)
Or did you come back because you ran out of rich men to cling to?

Serena crosses the space between them and SLAPS her so hard Chloe falls against the bid table.

Phones rise. Donors whisper.

SERENA
Still talking with my air in your mouth.

Adrian pulls a pistol from inside his jacket and points it at Serena.

ADRIAN
Touch her again and I put you down.

Serena opens a slim folder and throws it into his chest.

He catches it on instinct. Inside: route suspension notices, berth freezes, reefer contract cancellations.

SERENA
Pull that trigger.
At midnight, every Vale refrigerated lane from Newark to Halifax goes dark.

Adrian scans the pages. His hand tightens. Not enough to fire.

Serena steps closer until the gun barrel presses to the center of her chest.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Do it.
You left me to burn once.
Be brave enough to finish the job in public.

Adrian's arm trembles.

Chloe pushes up from the floor, humiliated.

CHLOE
Adrian--

Serena takes the barrel between two fingers and lowers it.

SERENA
That's what I thought.

She turns and walks out while every eye in the room stays on Adrian.

# EPISODE 5: CRUSH WHAT SHE USES TO TOUCH YOU

## INT. VALE STUDY - NIGHT

A wall of shipping maps. Rain on the windows.

Adrian stands over a dossier while his CHIEF OF STAFF talks fast.

CHIEF OF STAFF
Night Harbor controls six reefer terminals, two bonded storage islands, and most of the gray routes moving through the coast.
Sokolov backed her first, but the network runs on her design.

Adrian flips photos: Serena at docks, Serena in hard hat, Serena stepping off a cargo vessel.

ADRIAN
She built all this in five years?

CHIEF OF STAFF
She started before she left you.

Adrian dials Serena. She answers on speaker in a room full of quiet glass.

ADRIAN
Come back to the manor.
Whatever you want, I'll give it to you.

SERENA (V.O.)
You can't afford what I want.

She hangs up.

The study doors burst open. A SECURITY DIRECTOR rushes in.

SECURITY DIRECTOR
Sir, West Coast reefer access is locked.
Three terminals just switched to Night Harbor routing.

Adrian looks back at Serena's photo. For the first time, he looks afraid.

## INT. SKYSHORE PRESIDENTIAL SUITE - NIGHT

Chloe kicks the suite doors open with half a dozen private guards behind her.

CHLOE
Serena!

Serena sits on a sofa, one leg crossed, red wine in hand.

SERENA
You brought men.
That's cute.

Chloe points.

CHLOE
Break everything. Start with her face.

Hidden doors slide open. Night Harbor security pours out from the bedroom wall and service corridor. Chloe's guards are dropped in seconds.

One crashes into a glass table. Another hits the carpet, arm bent wrong.

Chloe backs up, loses a heel, and falls.

Serena rises at last, walks over, and places her stiletto on Chloe's hand.

CHLOE
Adrian will kill you--

Serena presses down. Bone CRACKS.

CHLOE (CONT'D)
AAH--

SERENA
Five years ago, you sent a truck to kill me.
This is interest.

She leans closer, voice almost gentle.

SERENA (CONT'D)
We're not even at the first payment.

Chloe sobs against the carpet as Serena lifts her heel.

# EPISODE 6: LOOK AT WHAT BURNED

## INT. UNDERGROUND GARAGE - NIGHT

Serena's motorcade stops between concrete pillars.

Adrian steps out of the shadows with Vale security. Weapons lift on both sides.

ADRIAN
Where did you go after the bridge?

SERENA
Somewhere your name couldn't reach.

ADRIAN
You're still my--

SERENA
Don't.

She waves her security down first. Her sleeve slips. Burned skin winds around her wrist.

Adrian sees it and stops cold.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Look at it.
This is what burned while you carried her away.

ADRIAN
Serena--

SERENA
No.
You don't get my name when you're trying to rewrite the night you left me.

She steps past his men.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Eve died on that bridge.
Night Harbor came back.

She gets into the car and the convoy leaves Adrian standing in exhaust and silence.

## INT. VALE ARCHIVE ROOM - LATER

Dust. Flood-damaged files. Broken shelves.

Two staffers dig through hospital boxes while Adrian tears open labels himself.

A NURSE LOG drops free. Beside it, an emergency intake form.

He reads:

`PATIENT: Serena Quinn`
`STATUS: approximately 10 weeks pregnant`

Adrian's knees almost give.

ADRIAN
No.

He turns the page with shaking fingers.

There are notes about abdominal trauma. Fetal loss.

He grips the shelf to stay upright.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
Get me everything.
Everything.

He doesn't sound angry anymore.
He sounds haunted.

# EPISODE 7: YOU CHOSE THE LIE AGAIN

## INT. VALE FOUNDATION GALA - NIGHT

A charity banner. Cameras. Donors with champagne.

Adrian and Chloe smile for flashes beside a giant photo of the so-called VALE HEIR.

The room hushes as Serena enters with two people:

DR. PIERCE, late fifties, expensively nervous.
A FORMER VALE DRIVER, wrists zip-tied.

Serena doesn't wait to be announced.

SERENA
Keep the cameras on.

She gestures. Her people push Dr. Pierce and the driver under the stage lights.

CHLOE
What is this?

SERENA
Your maternity ward.

The giant screen behind them flickers alive:

- Chloe entering a private fertility clinic.
- Wire transfers.
- A route map from the Harbor Bridge.
- The driver taking an envelope.

Murmurs ripple through the room.

DR. PIERCE
She paid me to fake the scans--

CHLOE
Shut up!

DR. PIERCE
--and told me Mr. Vale only needed to believe the child was his long enough to move her in.

The driver breaks next.

DRIVER
She gave me the bridge timing.
Said the black sedan had to hit the guardrail first.

Adrian goes pale, but his first move is toward the production booth.

ADRIAN
Kill the feed.

SERENA
Of course.
Save the gala before the truth.

CHLOE
She's faking this!

Adrian signals security.

ADRIAN
Escort her out.
Now.

Serena looks at him, stunned only because she is not surprised.

SERENA
You chose the lie again.

She looks past him, toward the smiling birthday portrait of Chloe's son.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Tomorrow I burn the cradle too.

She walks out on her own.

# EPISODE 8: NOT YOUR SON, NOT YOUR INNOCENCE

## INT. VALE TRUST HALL - DAY

A lavish birthday luncheon for CHLOE'S SON, MILES.

Trustees, society press, oversized cake, a silver family crest behind the head table.

Chloe lifts a glass.

CHLOE
To the future of Vale.

Serena steps in before anyone can drink. Behind her comes THEO KANE, a rough-faced former dock foreman, and a LAB TECH with a mobile testing kit.

Every face turns.

SERENA
Don't toast him until you know whose blood bought the candles.

Adrian stands.

ADRIAN
You don't get to do this here.

SERENA
This is exactly where I get to do it.

She throws files onto the cake table. Chloe's hand starts to shake.

THEO
Tell him, Chloe.

CHLOE
I don't know this man.

SERENA
Then let's make the room meet him with you.

The lab tech moves fast: sealed samples, documented chain, a result screen everyone can see.

Miles starts crying. Chloe tries to grab him. Serena's guard blocks her.

On the monitor:

`ADRIAN VALE - EXCLUDED`
`THEO KANE - MATCH`

The room goes silent.

One trustee drops his glass.

ADRIAN
Chloe.

Theo steps forward.

THEO
You told me he was leverage.
You said one rich idiot was worth ten honest men.

Adrian seizes Chloe's wrist.

ADRIAN
Look at me.
Whose son is he?

CHLOE
You're hurting me--

ADRIAN
Whose son?

She breaks.

CHLOE
Fine!
Not yours!
But don't look at me like I'm the only monster here!

Everyone freezes.

CHLOE (CONT'D)
You left Serena in that fire!
You left your own child in that car!

Adrian lets go like she burned him.

Serena watches him go white.

SERENA
Now you hear it.

Adrian's gun slips from his hand and thuds onto the marble.

# EPISODE 9: TWO HEARTBEATS IN THE ASHES

## INT. ABANDONED HARBOR STORAGE - NIGHT

A condemned warehouse by the old docks.

Adrian and two men rip open wall panels until they uncover a rusted floor safe.

He smashes the lock himself.

Inside:

- a twin ultrasound, edges yellowed
- a half-burned baby sweater
- a tiny silver signet ring
- a folded note in Serena's handwriting: `Tell him tonight.`

Adrian stares at the ultrasound.

`TWO FETAL HEARTBEATS`

His fingers fail. The page nearly falls.

FLASH CUTS:

Serena in the kitchen.
Her hand in her blazer.
His hand striking her.
His back walking away from the burning bridge.

Back in the warehouse, Adrian drops to his knees.

He clutches the sweater to his chest and hits himself across the face.

ADRIAN
I killed them.

Again.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I killed them.

He keeps striking himself until one of his men tries to stop him.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
Don't touch me!

## EXT. OLD PORT STREET - NIGHT

Rain hammers empty cobblestones.

Adrian stumbles into the street with the ultrasound and sweater. Then he sinks into the mud.

ADRIAN
Serena!

No answer. Just rain.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I left you there.
I left them there.

He bends over the tiny sweater and howls into the storm.

Cars slow. Strangers stare. He doesn't notice.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I don't deserve to live!

He keeps kneeling, drenched and broken, until the pages in his hands begin to bleed ink.

# EPISODE 10: ONE LIFE FOR TWO

## INT. SKYLINE ROTATION RESTAURANT - NIGHT

Glass walls above the city. Shipping magnates, politicians, investors.

Serena sits with Viktor at the best table in the room. Beyond them, the harbor glows.

VIKTOR
Half the eastern cold chain belongs to you now.

SERENA
Half is a corridor.
I want the whole harbor on its knees.

The restaurant doors BURST open.

Adrian staggers in, rain-soaked, muddy, eyes wild. He carries a stack of legal folios and a heavy SILVER TRUST SEAL.

Gasps all around the room.

Viktor rises.

VIKTOR
He shouldn't be here.

Adrian shoves him aside without taking his eyes off Serena.

Then he drops to both knees so hard the sound cracks through the room.

He lays everything at Serena's feet:

- the Vale trust seal
- berth deeds
- voting proxies
- transfer documents

ADRIAN
Take it.
Take all of it.

No one moves.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
The harbors, the routes, the board, the family name--
it's yours.
My life too.

He grips the hem of her dress with both hands.

ADRIAN (CONT'D)
I know what I did.
I know what I killed.
Please.

Serena looks down at him as if she is examining roadkill.

SERENA
Please what?

ADRIAN
Let me pay.
Tell me how.

Serena draws a silver pistol.

The room recoils.

She places the muzzle against his forehead.

SERENA
Two heartbeats.
One life.

She presses the gun into his hand instead.

SERENA (CONT'D)
Use yours.
Then maybe I'll listen to your apology.

Adrian takes the gun.

His hand shakes.

Viktor watches Serena. The room watches Adrian.

Adrian lifts the barrel to his own temple.

Serena does not blink.

BANG.

BLACK.
