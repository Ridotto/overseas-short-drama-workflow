## S7 处理口径

本轮不重设主线，不新增桥段，只处理三类问题：

- 台词压短：删掉解释腔，让人物在攻击、防守、逼迫、拒绝中说话。
- 视听强化：把“知道真相”变成 badge、door、scanner、heartbeat、warmer、identity bands 等可见动作。
- 卡点收紧：每集停在未完成动作或未回答问题前一拍。

## 全局台词替换

| 位置 | 初稿倾向 | polish 后 |
|---|---|---|
| Ethan 对 Serena | “你享受 Cross 名字够久了” | “You wore my name. Now take it off.” |
| Serena 对 Ethan | “你锁错了门” | “You locked the wrong door.” 保留，短且有钩子 |
| Chloe 自辩 | “她在毁基金” | “She wants my baby erased.” 更贴继承权恐惧 |
| Serena 对 Chloe | “证明你清白” | “Put your hand down, Chloe.” 更像逼迫动作 |
| Ethan 崩溃 | “我听见她” | “I heard her. And I still chose you.” 更刺中罪责 |
| Serena E10 | “This is not forgiveness” | 保留，作为首批核心态度 |

## Episode 1 polish

强化动作：

- Serena 修 Aster 的动作从“关闭警报”改为“把即将红灯的 neonatal demo 拉回绿灯”，暗示她救 Cross 于无形。
- Ethan 摘 bracelet 时加入手腕勒痕，让羞辱更身体化。
- Chloe 的最后一句保持低声，不让 Ethan 听见，制造反派主动性。

修后关键段：

```text
Ethan presses the latch. The bracelet bites into Serena's wrist before it opens.
Ethan: You wore my name. Now take it off.
Serena covers the clinic bracelet with her sleeve.
Chloe, low: North Wing doesn't open for her tonight.
```

## Episode 2 polish

强化动作：

- North Wing 玻璃门、胎心声、CEO card 的三件物建立为全集主刀。
- Ethan 的罪责由台词变成动作：他听见 Serena 后仍刷卡。

修后关键段：

```text
Serena: Ethan! They're yours!
Ethan stops. Chloe grips his lapel.
Chloe: She wants my baby gone.
Ethan swipes the black card.
System: CEO override accepted.
Inside North Wing, two heartbeats stutter into one flat tone.
```

## Episode 3 polish

强化动作：

- 失子段不加长哭，保留金属托盘和 A/B bands。
- 五年后归来用系统识别 Serena，不让她自报身份。

修后关键段：

```text
Dr. Ellis sets down the steel tray.
Two tiny bands slide together: A and B.
Serena closes her hand over them. No tears fall.
Five years later, the Aster wall reads: FOUNDER ACCESS REQUIRED.
Serena steps into the light.
```

## Episode 4 polish

强化动作：

- 关停演示时加“模拟心跳灯一盏盏熄灭”，避免专利律师函感。
- Serena 对 Ethan 只留一句门钩子。

修后关键段：

```text
Serena puts her palm on the podium.
Every simulated heartbeat on the Aster wall goes dark.
Reporters turn from Ethan to Serena.
Ethan: Who are you?
Serena: You locked the wrong door.
```

## Episode 5 polish

强化动作：

- Chloe 的反扑不是哭，而是闯入抢 drive。
- 打脸不用讲账目，biometric vault 锁手腕，donor 起身离开。

修后关键段：

```text
Chloe: She wants my baby erased.
Serena turns the glass vault toward her.
Serena: Put your hand down, Chloe.
The audit cuff snaps shut around Chloe's wrist.
Donors rise without a word.
```

## Episode 6 polish

强化动作：

- Serena 不解释伤疤，只把“问你的 override card”留给 Ethan。
- 档案不完整揭完，卡在 physical CEO card 缺失。

修后关键段：

```text
Ethan sees the scar.
Ethan: What did I do?
Serena: Ask your override card.
Archive text: SIX WEEKS. TWINS.
Archive text: OVERRIDE APPROVED BY ETHAN CROSS.
Video locked: PHYSICAL CEO CARD REQUIRED.
```

## Episode 7 polish

强化动作：

- Ethan 被迫推 infant warmer 到台中央，这比 Serena 自己推更刺。
- 直播源由 Aster 锁住，不让 board 切掉。

修后关键段：

```text
Serena: Push it, Ethan.
Ethan's hands close on the warmer rail.
The wheels squeal across the marble.
Recording: Ms. Hart's vitals are stable.
Serena points to the registry.
Serena: Register him now.
```

## Episode 8 polish

强化动作：

- Noah 不作为现场羞辱对象，使用 sealed swab capsule。
- Chloe 的反咬必须打到 Ethan 的罪责。

修后关键段：

```text
Ethan inserts the sealed swab.
Screen: NO CROSS PATERNAL MATCH.
The gold heir tag hits the marble.
Chloe: You signed the North Wing lockdown.
Chloe: You killed her babies before I ever lied about mine.
Serena puts the black card in Ethan's hand.
```

## Episode 9 polish

强化动作：

- Ethan 的崩溃只靠空间、旧录音、A/B bands，不让他长篇忏悔。
- 去掉“我听见她”后重复解释，改为更短。

修后关键段：

```text
System: Route the team to Chloe. Lock North Wing.
The warmer drawer sticks. Ethan pulls.
Two bands fall to the floor.
Ethan: I heard her.
Ethan: And I still chose you.
He tears off the CEO badge and lays it beside the bands.
```

## Episode 10 polish

强化动作：

- Ethan 的“跪”保留，但不给“浪漫求原谅”，只作为公开权力失位。
- Serena 不开情绪口子，所有权力落到 trust scanner 倒计时。

修后关键段：

```text
Ethan kneels in the live camera light.
Ethan: I locked North Wing.
Ethan: I left you and our children behind that door.
Serena points to the trust scanner.
Serena: Put your thumb there.
Screen: CROSS HEIR WING DISSOLUTION REQUESTED.
Serena: This is not forgiveness.
Serena: This is the door you owe me.
```
