# The Heiress They Locked Out

## Episode 1

### 1-1 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN MEDICAL GROUP - GALA ATRIUM - NIGHT

人物：Serena, Ethan, Chloe, Cross board, security

△ A glass atrium glows above the city. A rehearsal board table is set for the Cross Heir Trust announcement.

△ Behind a curtain, Serena Vale closes a hospital alert on a tablet. The Aster system returns to green. Her sleeve slips: a pale clinic bracelet circles her wrist.

△ The doors open. Ethan Cross walks in with Chloe Hart on his arm. Chloe wears white, one hand resting on a small “heir monitoring band.”

**Ethan:** Everyone. Chloe is carrying the Cross heir.

△ Board members stand. Camera phones lift. Serena freezes behind the curtain.

**Chloe:** Serena, I hope this won't be awkward. The baby needs a family.

△ Ethan lays a dissolution packet on the board table. Then he takes Serena's wrist.

**Serena:** Ethan. Not here.

**Ethan:** Here. You enjoyed the Cross name long enough.

△ He presses the latch. Serena's Cross access bracelet clicks open.

△ Ethan places the bracelet in Chloe's palm. A board assistant removes Serena's nameplate from the spouse seat.

**Ethan:** Sign. Chloe takes the spouse seat tonight.

△ Chloe steps toward the seat, brushes Serena's shoulder, then stumbles with a soft cry.

**Chloe:** Ah!

△ Ethan catches Chloe, then turns on Serena.

**Ethan:** You still have the nerve to hurt her?

**Serena:** I didn't touch her.

**Ethan:** You never touch anything. You just wait for someone else to clean your mess.

△ He pulls the Cross matriarch pin from Serena's dress. The fabric tears slightly. He pins it to Chloe.

△ Serena covers the clinic bracelet with her sleeve. Her hand shakes once, then goes still.

**Serena:** Fine.

△ She signs. The pen leaves a black line through her married name.

△ Serena walks toward the service exit. Behind her, Chloe's smile thins.

**Chloe** *(low, to security)*: North Wing doesn't open for her tonight.

△ The North Wing access panel on the wall turns red.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 2

### 2-1 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN - NORTH WING CORRIDOR - NIGHT

人物：Serena, Ethan, Chloe, doctor, security

△ Serena hurries through the white corridor, one hand pressed to her stomach. Blood stains her fingers.

△ The North Wing glass doors slide halfway, then lock.

**Serena:** Open the door. Please.

△ She slams the emergency button. Red light flashes.

**Security:** CEO override. North Wing is sealed.

△ Down the hall, Chloe screams from the VIP lobby. Ethan runs to her. Chloe collapses into his arms.

**Chloe:** Ethan, the baby. Something's wrong.

△ A doctor looks between Chloe and the locked North Wing.

**Doctor:** We have one maternal team on site.

△ Serena pounds the glass. Her voice cracks.

**Serena:** Ethan! They're yours!

△ Ethan hears. He stops for half a second.

**Ethan:** She is trying to scare you.

**Serena:** Ethan, please!

△ Ethan swipes his black CEO card. The North Wing inner lock slams shut.

**Ethan:** Route the team to Chloe.

△ The doctors wheel Chloe toward the VIP suite. Serena slides down the glass, leaving a red smear.

△ A fetal monitor inside North Wing stutters: two small heartbeats, uneven, then one long flat tone.

**Serena** *(whisper)*: No. No, please.

△ Through the glass, Ethan carries Chloe past the corridor window. He never looks back.

△ Serena's hand falls open against the locked door.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 3

### 3-1 INT. PRIVATE RECOVERY ROOM - DAWN

人物：Serena, Dr. Ellis, Mason

△ Serena wakes under a white ceiling. Her hand moves to her stomach.

△ Dr. Mara Ellis stands beside the bed. She cannot meet Serena's eyes.

**Serena:** Tell me.

△ Dr. Ellis sets a small steel tray on the blanket. Two tiny identity bands rest inside: A and B.

**Dr. Ellis:** I'm sorry. We couldn't bring them back.

△ Serena touches the bands. Her fingers go rigid.

△ On the wall TV, Ethan stands beside Chloe at a press podium.

**Ethan** *(TV)*: The Chloe Hart Heir Wing will protect every mother and child under the Cross name.

△ Serena stares at Chloe's smile on screen.

**Serena:** Turn it off.

△ A man in a dark coat steps in: Mason Reed. He places a folder beside the tray.

**Mason:** Aster's original founder agreement. You wrote the system Cross built its empire on.

**Serena:** Why are you here?

**Mason:** To ask how long Cross lives if you take its heart back.

△ Serena closes her fingers around the two bands.

**Serena:** Not long.

### 3-2 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN GLOBAL LAUNCH - FIVE YEARS LATER - NIGHT

人物：Ethan, Chloe, board, reporters

△ A giant Aster neonatal wall glows with animated heartbeats. Reporters crowd the hall.

△ Ethan adjusts his cuff. Chloe poses with a child-sized gold heir tag on a silver tray nearby.

**Chloe:** Tonight fixes everything.

△ The Aster wall flickers.

TEXT ON SCREEN: FOUNDER ACCESS REQUIRED.

△ The main doors open.

△ Serena walks in, black dress, calm eyes. Her heels stop at the edge of the light.

TEXT ON SCREEN: ASTER FOUNDER DETECTED.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 4

### 4-1 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN GLOBAL LAUNCH - CONTINUOUS

人物：Serena, Ethan, Chloe, Mason, board, reporters

△ The room goes silent. Chloe looks Serena up and down.

**Chloe:** You came back for money?

**Ethan:** Security.

△ Two guards step forward. Their badges flash red. The security gate refuses them.

**Serena:** Your building still knows who built its spine.

△ She walks to the Aster control podium. Ethan blocks her.

**Ethan:** That system belongs to Cross.

**Serena:** No. Cross rented it from a woman you locked out.

△ Serena presses her palm onto the glass console.

TEXT ON SCREEN: FOUNDER OVERRIDE ACCEPTED.

△ Every simulated heartbeat on the neonatal wall goes dark. One by one. The sound drops out of the hall.

△ Reporters surge forward.

**Reporter:** Did Cross lose the Aster license?

△ Chloe's Aurora Children Fund badge blinks red: ACCESS REVOKED.

**Chloe:** Ethan, do something!

△ Ethan stares at Serena as if seeing her for the first time.

**Ethan:** Who are you?

△ Serena steps close enough that only he hears.

**Serena:** Five years ago, you locked the wrong door.

△ Ethan's face drains.

**Serena:** Start there.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 5

### 5-1 INT. SERENA'S HOTEL SUITE - NIGHT

人物：Serena, Chloe, donors, audit chair, security, Ethan

△ Chloe kicks the suite door open with two security men.

**Chloe:** Serena Vale is stealing Cross property. Take every drive in this room.

△ The lights turn on. Around the table sit Aurora Fund donors and an audit chair. Serena is at the head, pouring water.

**Serena:** You're early. The audit just started.

△ Chloe stops. Her face changes, then softens into tears.

**Chloe:** She is trying to ruin a children's fund.

**Serena:** Then clear your name.

△ Serena turns a transparent biometric vault toward Chloe.

**Serena:** Hand on the glass.

△ Chloe hesitates. Donors watch. She presses her palm down.

TEXT ON VAULT: THREE MILLION USD FLAGGED. BENEFICIARY: HART PRIVATE HOLDINGS.

△ A clear audit cuff snaps around Chloe's wrist.

**Chloe:** Get this off me!

**Audit Chair:** Ms. Hart, your fund authority is suspended.

△ Her “Mother of the Heir” smile breaks. Donors stand and leave.

△ Ethan arrives at the open door. Chloe is on her knees beside scattered fund papers.

**Chloe:** Ethan, she trapped me!

△ Ethan looks at Serena.

**Ethan:** What else did you take?

△ His lawyer leans close, whispering.

**Lawyer:** Sir. She can freeze the Cross voting trust.

△ Serena raises one eyebrow.

**Serena:** I haven't started.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 6

### 6-1 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN - LOADING BAY - NIGHT

人物：Serena, Ethan, guards

△ Serena walks toward a black car. Ethan steps from the shadows and grabs her wrist.

**Ethan:** Enough. Stop tearing my company apart.

△ Her sleeve slides back. A pale scar cuts across her wrist, old and hard.

△ Ethan loosens his grip.

**Ethan:** What is that?

**Serena:** Ask your override card.

**Ethan:** Stop speaking in riddles.

**Serena:** You signed the riddle.

△ She pulls free and gets in the car.

### 6-2 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN ARCHIVE CAGE - LATER

人物：Ethan, records clerk

△ Ethan swipes his CEO card. A metal cage door opens. Boxes of sealed North Wing records sit under cold lights.

**Records Clerk:** These files were locked after the emergency.

**Ethan:** Open them.

△ A file appears on a terminal.

TEXT: PATIENT: SERENA VALE CROSS. SIX WEEKS. TWINS.

△ Ethan's hand freezes on the mouse.

**Ethan:** Twins?

△ Another line loads.

TEXT: OVERRIDE APPROVED BY ETHAN CROSS. MATERNAL TEAM ROUTED TO CHLOE HART.

△ Ethan backs into a shelf.

**Ethan:** No.

△ A surveillance thumbnail appears: Serena's hand pressed to North Wing glass, blood on the door.

△ Ethan clicks it.

TEXT: PHYSICAL CEO CARD REQUIRED.

△ He searches his wallet. The old card is gone.

△ Cut to Serena's hand in the car. The black CEO override card rests between her fingers.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 7

### 7-1 INT. CROSS HEIR TRUST CEREMONY - DAY

人物：Serena, Ethan, Chloe, Dr. Ellis, board, donors

△ A silver tray holds a small gold tag: CROSS HEIR.

△ Chloe stands beside Ethan, wearing a jeweled pin that reads MOTHER OF THE HEIR.

**Chloe:** Noah is the future of this family.

△ Serena enters with two attendants pushing a covered infant warmer.

**Ethan:** This ceremony is closed.

**Serena:** Then close it after you see what you opened.

△ The cover comes off. The warmer is empty except for an old speaker and a sealed call chip.

**Chloe:** This is disgusting.

△ Serena looks at Ethan.

**Serena:** Push it to the center.

**Ethan:** I don't take orders from you.

△ Serena touches the trust scanner. The room screens split into live feeds: board, donors, press.

**Serena:** Then the board can watch you refuse.

△ Ethan grips the warmer and pushes it to the center. The wheels squeak loudly.

△ Serena presses PLAY.

**Nurse June** *(recording)*: Ms. Hart's vitals are stable. There is no fetal distress.

△ Dr. Ellis steps forward.

**Dr. Ellis:** Chloe Hart was not in medical danger that night.

△ Chloe grips Ethan's arm.

**Chloe:** They're lying.

△ Serena points to the heir registry beside the podium.

**Serena:** Good. Prove it. Register Noah now.

△ Chloe's face turns white.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 8

### 8-1 INT. CROSS HEIR TRUST CEREMONY - CONTINUOUS

人物：Serena, Ethan, Chloe, board, donors

△ The heir registry opens with a soft chime. A sealed swab capsule waits in the tray.

**Ethan:** This is not the time.

**Serena:** If he is your son, Chloe leaves this room untouchable.

△ Board members lean forward. Cameras keep rolling.

△ Ethan picks up the swab capsule. His hand shakes once.

**Chloe:** Ethan, don't let her humiliate our child.

**Serena:** This isn't about the child. It's about the lie you wrapped around him.

△ Ethan inserts the capsule.

TEXT ON SCREEN: ANALYZING PATERNAL MATCH.

△ Chloe whispers prayers. The room holds its breath.

TEXT ON SCREEN: NO CROSS PATERNAL MATCH.

△ The gold heir tag springs off the tray and clatters to the marble floor.

△ Chloe's pin flashes red. The words MOTHER OF THE HEIR fade from its face.

**Ethan:** Chloe.

△ Ethan grabs her shoulders.

**Ethan:** Tell me it's wrong.

**Chloe:** You want the truth? Fine.

△ She points at him, crying and furious.

**Chloe:** You signed the North Wing lockdown. You killed her babies before I ever lied about mine!

△ Ethan lets go as if burned.

△ Serena takes the old black CEO override card from her clutch and places it in Ethan's palm.

**Serena:** Open North Wing yourself.

△ Ethan stares at the card. The room noise fades.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 9

### 9-1 INT. CROSS MERIDIAN - SEALED NORTH WING - NIGHT

人物：Ethan

△ The corridor is dark. Dust covers the North Wing sign.

△ Ethan stands alone before the sealed glass door. He swipes the old CEO card.

△ One lock opens. Then another. Then the inner door.

△ Cold light spills across his face.

**System Audio:** Override approved by Ethan Cross.

△ Ethan walks in. His steps echo.

**Ethan** *(recording)*: Route the team to Chloe. Lock North Wing.

△ Ethan stops at the glass door from five years ago. A dark stain remains near the base, scrubbed but not gone.

△ The old infant warmer sits under a sheet.

△ Ethan pulls the drawer. It sticks. He yanks harder.

△ Two tiny identity bands fall out and hit the floor.

TEXT ON BANDS: CROSS / VALE A. CROSS / VALE B.

△ Ethan's knees buckle. He drops before the glass.

**Ethan:** I heard her.

△ His breath breaks.

**Ethan:** I heard her and I locked the door.

△ He tears the CEO badge from his lapel and places it on the floor beside the bands.

△ He records a voice message, barely able to speak.

**Ethan:** Serena. Tell me where to kneel. I will bring Cross with me.

△ The message sends.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 10

### 10-1 INT. GLOBAL PEDIATRIC SUMMIT - NIGHT

人物：Serena, Ethan, Mason, Cross board, reporters, donors

△ Serena stands at the keynote stage. Behind her, a new Aster partnership glows on screen.

△ Mason stands near her, satisfied. Cross board members sit stiffly in the front row.

**Mason:** After tonight, Cross begs you for air.

**Serena:** They already did.

△ The hall doors burst open.

△ Ethan walks in, pale, unshaven, still in the suit from North Wing. Reporters turn.

△ He carries three things in both hands: the CEO badge, the Cross Heir Trust deed, and the black override card.

△ He stops in the red light of the live camera, then kneels.

**Ethan:** Serena.

△ Gasps move through the hall.

**Board Chair:** Cut the feed.

△ The broadcast light stays red. Aster locks the signal.

**Ethan:** I signed the override. I locked North Wing. I left you and our children behind that door.

△ Serena's face does not move.

**Ethan:** Take Cross. Take my name. Take everything. Just tell me how to pay for them.

△ He lifts the deed and badge.

△ Serena steps down from the stage. She does not touch him.

**Serena:** You don't pay the dead. You answer to them.

△ She points to a glass trust scanner at center stage.

**Serena:** Put your thumb there.

**Board Chair:** Serena, don't.

**Serena:** He owns the override. Let him use it.

△ Ethan stands with effort. He places his thumb on the scanner.

TEXT ON SCREEN: CROSS HEIR WING DISSOLUTION REQUESTED.

TEXT ON SCREEN: CONFIRMING BY CEO BIOMETRIC.

△ The hall erupts. Board members surge to their feet.

**Ethan:** Do it.

△ The countdown begins.

TEXT ON SCREEN: 03.

△ Serena leans close.

**Serena:** This is not forgiveness.

TEXT ON SCREEN: 02.

**Serena:** This is the door you owe me.

TEXT ON SCREEN: 01.

CUT TO BLACK.
