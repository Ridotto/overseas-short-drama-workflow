# PRD v15-v4workflow 豪门商战版首批 1-10 集

剧名暂定：《前妻按下帝国直播键》  
英文名暂定：His Ex-Wife Holds the Empire Live

类型：海外豪门商战 / 家族企业 / 女性复仇 / 追妻火葬场

主要人物：

- Serena Hale / Aria North：Adrian 的前妻。五年前被丈夫和 Blackwell 家族公开抛弃，失去双胎；五年后以 North Capital 控制人身份回归。
- Adrian Blackwell：Blackwell Global 继承人兼 CEO。前期傲慢、护短，只信 Chloe 和家族体面。
- Chloe Whitmore：Adrian 的旧爱，Blackwell Foundation 主席。伪造救市功劳和继承人叙事。
- Marcus Arden：跨境资本人。五年前救下 Serena，扶她成为 Aria North。
- Bennett Cole：Blackwell CFO，Chloe 的秘密情人。
- Lydia Price：五年前 Blackwell 发布会南门主管，知道 Serena 被押走和事故物被封存的细节。

## Episode 1: The Brooch Comes Off

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - LAUNCH BACKSTAGE - NIGHT

A countdown clock flashes above a black curtain.

ON SCREEN: 00:09:18.

Serena Hale sits at a narrow table behind the stage. One hand holds a phone to her ear. The other hand slides a final printed page into a silver rescue case.

Inside the open case: a contract, a family seal stamp, and a glowing palm plate.

The plate turns green under Serena's hand.

SERENA  
Tell the banks the rescue case is live. Blackwell launches tonight.

She hangs up.

Her fingers tremble as she takes a small ultrasound photo from her purse. Two white dots float inside the dark oval.

Serena smiles for half a second.

SERENA  
Your father gets his company and his babies back in the same night.

She slips the ultrasound into an anniversary card.

The backstage doors open.

Adrian Blackwell enters with Chloe Whitmore on his arm. Chloe wears a white dress and Serena's Blackwell spouse brooch. Her hand rests on her stomach.

Behind them: lawyers, board members, two security guards.

Serena stands.

SERENA  
Why is she wearing my brooch?

Adrian drops a divorce packet on the table.

ADRIAN  
Sign before the launch starts.

Serena stares at the packet, then at Chloe.

SERENA  
You brought her here for this?

Chloe's eyes fill.

CHLOE  
Serena, please. Adrian needs peace tonight. The baby needs it too.

Serena goes still.

SERENA  
The baby?

Adrian puts a hand on Chloe's back.

ADRIAN  
My heir.

The board members look away. No one looks at Serena.

Serena's hand closes around the anniversary card inside her purse.

SERENA  
Then let her open the rescue case.

Chloe's smile tightens.

Serena pushes the silver case toward Chloe.

SERENA  
If she saved Blackwell, her palm opens it.

Chloe does not move.

CHLOE  
She's trying to humiliate me.

ADRIAN  
Enough.

He grabs Serena's wrist and yanks her back from the case. Serena's hip hits the table. The anniversary card falls from her purse.

The ultrasound slips halfway out.

Serena lunges for it.

Chloe steps down first. Her heel grinds the corner of the photo into the floor.

Serena freezes.

Chloe looks down, innocent.

CHLOE  
Oh. I didn't see it.

Serena bends to pick it up.

Adrian tears the Blackwell spouse brooch off Serena's dress.

The pin rips fabric and skin. A red line opens below her collarbone.

ADRIAN  
You don't touch Blackwell again.

He presses a pen into her hand.

ADRIAN  
Sign.

Serena picks up the ultrasound. Chloe's shoe print smears over the two dots.

She wipes it with her thumb. The ink smears worse.

The countdown hits 00:06:00.

Serena signs the divorce page only.

SERENA  
I won't sign your lie.

Adrian's jaw hardens.

ADRIAN  
Then leave with nothing.

He nods.

Two guards take Serena by the arms. She keeps the ruined ultrasound clenched in one fist.

At the door, she turns.

SERENA  
When I come back, I won't ask for a place in your family.

The guards push her out.

The door shuts.

Chloe wipes one fake tear from her cheek.

Then she takes a red federal folder from a lawyer's case.

Serena Hale is printed on the tab.

Chloe slides it to an investigator waiting by the side entrance.

CHLOE  
Before she runs.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 2: The Glass Door

INT. BLACKWELL GLOBAL - MAIN LAUNCH HALL - NIGHT

Reporters pack the launch hall. Bankers stand under the huge product screen. Board members whisper near the stage.

The countdown reaches zero.

The product video never plays.

Instead, the stock ticker plunges red across the wall.

ON SCREEN: BLACKWELL GLOBAL -31.7%.

The crowd erupts.

Federal investigators push through the aisle.

INVESTIGATOR  
Mr. Blackwell. We need Serena Hale.

The side doors burst open.

Serena is dragged in by security. Her collarbone still bleeds where the brooch was torn away.

SERENA  
Adrian, don't give them that folder. Chloe can't open the rescue case.

Cameras swing toward her.

Chloe folds over, clutching Adrian's sleeve.

CHLOE  
She's doing it again. She wants us to lose everything.

Serena sees the red folder in the investigator's hand.

SERENA  
I am pregnant.

The noise dies.

Serena pulls the damaged ultrasound from her fist.

SERENA  
Twins. Eight weeks. Yours.

Adrian looks at the gray shoe print across the photo.

For one breath, his face cracks.

Chloe whimpers.

CHLOE  
Adrian, please. Our baby.

The board chair thrusts the folder at Adrian.

BOARD CHAIR  
Sign. If she stays in this building, the banks walk out.

Serena steps toward Adrian.

SERENA  
Look at me. Just this once.

Adrian takes the pen.

He signs.

Serena's face empties.

The investigators cuff her in front of the cameras.

SERENA  
No.

A metallic crack splits the hall.

Above the stage, a lighting rig drops. Sparks rain down. Drapes catch fire.

People scream and rush for the exits.

Chloe is knocked against the front row rail.

CHLOE  
Adrian!

The panicked crowd shoves Serena into a glass service corridor. Her cuff chain catches around a steel handrail.

Smoke rolls into the corridor.

Serena pulls once.

The cuff bites deeper.

She pulls again.

Blood appears beneath her dress.

SERENA  
Adrian!

Adrian turns.

Through the glass, he sees Serena on her knees. One wrist is trapped. One hand is pressed to her stomach.

She pounds the glass with the cuff chain.

SERENA  
The babies. Open the door.

Chloe crawls behind him and grabs his trouser leg.

CHLOE  
I can't breathe.

A security guard holds out bolt cutters.

SECURITY  
Sir! Her cuff or Miss Whitmore's rail?

Adrian stands between them.

Serena stares through the glass.

SERENA  
Please.

Adrian grabs the bolt cutters.

He turns away from Serena.

He cuts Chloe free.

Serena stops hitting the glass.

Adrian lifts Chloe into his arms.

SERENA  
Adrian.

He does not look back.

ADRIAN  
Keep Hale with the investigators.

The fire door begins to descend between the hall and the glass corridor.

Serena crawls for the opening.

The cuff jerks her back.

The door slams down inches from her face.

Adrian carries Chloe away through smoke and camera flashes.

Serena's hand slides down the glass, leaving a red streak.

SERENA  
My babies...

She collapses behind the glass.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 3: The Woman Who Walked Back In

INT. PRIVATE CLINIC - RECOVERY ROOM - NIGHT

Serena wakes before she opens her eyes.

Her hand moves to her stomach.

Flat.

She tears the blanket back.

SERENA  
No.

A doctor catches her wrist.

DOCTOR  
Miss Hale.

Serena looks at him.

SERENA  
Where are they?

The doctor cannot hold her eyes.

DOCTOR  
I'm sorry. The bleeding was too severe. Both fetuses were gone before surgery.

Serena's mouth opens. No sound comes out.

Her hand curls around empty sheets.

On the wall TV, Adrian stands at a podium. Chloe is wrapped in a blanket beside him.

ADRIAN ON TV  
Chloe Whitmore saved Blackwell tonight. Anyone who used our family name to sabotage this company will answer for it.

Chloe lowers her head into Adrian's shoulder.

Serena watches until her eyes go dry.

The door opens.

Marcus Arden walks in with two men.

He places a black folder on the bed.

MARCUS  
Your husband left through the front entrance. I pulled you out through the loading dock.

Serena does not answer.

Marcus opens the folder. Inside: passports, bank cards, a new identity.

MARCUS  
Serena Hale is finished. Aria North can still stand.

Serena reaches for the ruined ultrasound on the bedside table. Someone saved it from her fist.

She folds it once. Slowly. Cleanly.

SERENA  
What do you want?

MARCUS  
Blackwell gutted. Chloe exposed. Adrian on his knees.

Serena's breathing changes.

MARCUS  
You built their empire in the dark. Build your own in the light.

Serena closes the black folder.

SERENA  
No.

She looks at the TV. Chloe is smiling now.

SERENA  
I want them to see it burn first.

INT. BLACKWELL FOUNDATION CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT - FIVE YEARS LATER

Crystal chandeliers. White roses. Cameras.

Adrian sits at the front table. Chloe sits beside him, wearing the Blackwell family brooch.

An auctioneer lifts the gavel.

AUCTIONEER  
Naming rights for the children's wing. Opening bid, ten million.

Adrian raises his paddle.

ADRIAN  
Fifteen.

Chloe smiles for the cameras.

The back doors open.

A woman in black enters.

Only her heels are visible at first. Then the hem of a black coat. Then dark glasses.

The room quiets table by table.

Adrian turns.

Chloe's smile disappears.

The woman stops at the aisle.

She does not remove her glasses.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 4: She Buys the Room

INT. BLACKWELL FOUNDATION CHARITY AUCTION - NIGHT

The woman in black walks down the aisle.

No one speaks.

The auctioneer lowers his gavel without noticing.

Chloe grips Adrian's sleeve.

CHLOE  
Who is she?

The woman reaches the front row.

She removes her glasses.

Serena Hale looks back at them.

A glass slips from Chloe's hand and breaks.

CHLOE  
Impossible.

Adrian rises.

ADRIAN  
Serena?

Serena does not answer him.

She looks at the auctioneer.

SERENA  
One hundred million.

The room explodes.

BOARD MEMBER  
Who cleared her?

The bank chairman stands from a side table.

BANK CHAIRMAN  
North Capital cleared before dinner.

Chloe forces a laugh.

CHLOE  
North Capital? Serena, you couldn't keep a marriage. Now you're pretending to buy hospitals?

Serena turns to Chloe.

Chloe lifts her chin, performing for the cameras.

CHLOE  
Everyone here remembers what you did to Blackwell.

Serena walks up the stage steps.

The auctioneer backs away.

Serena picks up the white ribbon meant for Chloe's donor photo.

SERENA  
You wanted a photo?

She steps close.

CHLOE  
Don't touch me.

Serena slaps her.

The sound cracks through the hall.

Chloe hits the edge of the table. Blood blooms at the corner of her mouth.

Adrian moves toward Serena.

ADRIAN  
Enough.

Marcus appears beside Serena before Adrian reaches her.

MARCUS  
Half your lenders came with her.

Serena takes Chloe's donor plaque from the table.

ON PLAQUE: CHLOE WHITMORE, SAVIOR OF BLACKWELL.

Serena drops it into a champagne bucket.

The paper label softens and peels under the champagne.

SERENA  
Wet paper. That's all her legacy is.

Chloe lunges.

Serena catches Chloe's wrist and bends it down until Chloe gasps.

SERENA  
Five years ago, you smiled while I bled.

Adrian's face changes.

ADRIAN  
What did you say?

Serena finally looks at him.

SERENA  
Not tonight.

She raises her paddle again.

SERENA  
One hundred million. Rename the wing before sunrise.

The gavel comes down.

AUCTIONEER  
Sold.

Chloe stands before the cameras with blood on her lip and no plaque behind her.

Adrian stares at Serena like he is seeing a ghost with a pulse.

CUT TO BLACK.

Serena stands barefoot before floor-to-ceiling windows. Blackwell Tower glows below.

Marcus pours whiskey behind her.

MARCUS  
You made him look small tonight.

SERENA  
Not small enough.

Chloe storms in with six private guards.

CHLOE  
Get out of my city, Serena.

Serena does not turn.

CHLOE  
Adrian let you live once. I won't.

Serena lifts one finger.

He stops.

Chloe snatches a framed photo from the table: North Capital's first acquisition.

She throws it.

Glass shatters against the wall beside Serena's head.

A small cut opens on Serena's cheek.

Chloe sees the blood and smiles.

CHLOE  
There. Still human.

Serena walks toward her.

Chloe's guards step forward.

The penthouse side doors open.

North Capital security floods in, fast and silent.

Chloe's men hit the floor before they can draw.

Chloe backs up.

SERENA  
You came to my floor with borrowed men.

Serena catches Chloe by the hair and drags her to the glass.

Far below, reporters crowd the sidewalk, cameras pointed up.

SERENA  
Look down.

Chloe claws at Serena's wrist.

SERENA  
Wave.

CHLOE  
You're insane.

Serena forces Chloe's palm against the window.

SERENA  
Five years ago, who told the investigators I was running?

Chloe clenches her jaw.

SERENA  
Who moved the red folder before the launch?

Chloe's eyes flicker.

SERENA  
Say her name.

CHLOE  
I don't know--

Serena pushes harder. Chloe's cheek flattens against the glass.

CHLOE  
Lydia. South gate. She signed people in.

Serena lets go.

SERENA  
Run back to Adrian.

Serena crouches close.

SERENA  
Tell him I am done buying rooms.

Chloe crawls backward, one heel broken, hair torn loose.

At the elevator, she looks back with wet hatred.

CHLOE  
He will never choose you.

Serena's hand moves to her lower stomach.

The movement is tiny.

Marcus sees it.

Serena catches herself and drops her hand.

SERENA  
He already did.

She looks to Marcus.

SERENA  
Find Lydia Price.

CUT TO BLACK.

Serena walks toward her car with two bodyguards.

The garage lights snap on row by row.

Adrian stands between her and the car.

Move.

ADRIAN  
Who are you now?

Someone you can't remove with a signature.

Five years ago, you disappeared.

No.

SERENA  
You left.

Serena lets him catch her wrist.

His other hand brushes her lower abdomen by accident.

Serena flinches so hard her back hits the car.

FLASH CUT:

BACK TO SCENE.

Adrian sees it.

ADRIAN  
Serena.

SERENA  
Don't touch there.

What happened after that night?

I didn't know.

You didn't want to know.

ADMINISTRATOR  
Blackwell sealed that incident file five years ago.

I am Blackwell.

ADMINISTRATOR  
Then you sealed it.

EXT. ST. AGNES HOSPITAL - SOUTH SERVICE GATE - NIGHT

Lydia Price, older now, smokes under the awning.

Adrian steps from a black car.

LYDIA  
I told Miss North I didn't want money.

Then what do you want?

LYDIA  
For one Blackwell to ask instead of ordering.

ADRIAN  
Please.

LYDIA  
They said there was no baby to log.

Inside: a torn hospital wristband. The ink is faded.

ON WRISTBAND: BABY A HALE.

Adrian cannot close his hand around it.

LYDIA  
There was a second band. Somebody took it.

Adrian looks up.

LYDIA  
Ask your foundation queen.

Sunlight floods white walls, balloons, cameras, and children in small wheelchairs.

Chloe stands at the ribbon with five-year-old Noah beside her.

Noah holds a toy plane and Adrian's hand.

Reporters crowd in.

CHLOE  
Blackwell gives children a future. That's what Adrian and I wanted for our son.

Adrian stiffens at "our son," but cameras are everywhere.

Serena enters from the back with Marcus.

Parents whisper. Board members shift.

Chloe smiles brighter.

CHLOE  
Miss North. Or should I say Mrs. Blackwell's ghost?

Serena walks to the ribbon.

SERENA  
You named a children's wing after a lie.

Chloe steps in front of Noah.

CHLOE  
Stay away from my child.

Serena looks at Noah.

The boy hides behind Adrian's leg.

Serena's face tightens for half a second. Then it is gone.

SERENA  
Then let his mother tell the truth.

Chloe laughs.

CHLOE  
The truth is that Adrian protected us from you.

Noah drops his toy plane.

Another flash. A balloon bursts.

Noah screams.

He tears away from Adrian and runs straight to Bennett Cole, standing near the donors.

NOAH  
Daddy! Don't let her take me!

Bennett catches him before he thinks.

The room freezes.

Chloe's face goes white.

ADRIAN  
Bennett.

Noah clings harder.

NOAH  
Daddy, no.

Chloe lunges and clamps a hand over Noah's mouth.

CHLOE

NOAH  
Daddy.

Serena.
