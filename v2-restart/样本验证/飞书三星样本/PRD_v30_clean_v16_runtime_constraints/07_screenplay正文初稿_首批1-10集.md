# S6 screenplay 正文初稿：首批 1-10 集

> 正文对白默认英文。  
> 本稿按 S5 writer 施工稿生成。

## Episode 1

### 1-1 INT. VALE GROUP BOARDROOM - NIGHT

The projection wall flickers red: SAFETY MODEL FAILED.

Ava Reid stands alone at the console, sleeves rolled, hair pinned messily. She types one final line. The wall turns green.

Ava exhales. Her phone buzzes: THOMAS REID - CRITICAL CARE PAYMENT DUE TONIGHT.

Before she can answer, the boardroom doors open.

Adrian Vale walks in with Celeste Gray on his arm. Celeste wears a copper key on a chain. The board rises. Cameras click.

Celeste gives a soft smile.

CELESTE: I never wanted a seat. I only wanted to help this family survive.

The chairman removes Ava's nameplate from the main table. He places Celeste's there.

Ava reaches for the chair. A junior assistant quietly pushes it to the wall.

AVA: That seat carries the Reid proxy.

ADRIAN: Not anymore.

He slides a folder across the table: SEPARATION. NON-DISPARAGEMENT. SPOUSE PROXY TERMINATION.

Ava's phone buzzes again. Hospital. She does not look away from Adrian.

AVA: My father's medical trust is tied to this vote.

ADRIAN: Your father is not a weapon.

He signs a freeze order. On the wall, Thomas Reid's care account changes to HOLD.

Ava grips the table edge.

AVA: You froze his oxygen fund.

CELESTE: Adrian is protecting the company. Don't make him the villain.

Ava looks at Celeste's copper key.

AVA: You brought a prop and took a chair.

Adrian's hand slams the table.

ADRIAN: She saved me when this company almost burned. You cooked dinners and hid in my house.

The words hit harder than the slap he does not throw.

The board stares. Cameras keep flashing.

Ava removes her wedding ring. It catches at her knuckle. She drags it against the table edge until her skin opens. Blood dots the signature page.

AVA: Fine.

She signs. Her hand trembles only once.

Celeste bends, picks up Ava's old nameplate, and places it in Ava's palm like trash.

CELESTE: This room was never yours.

Ava closes her fist over the nameplate. In her other sleeve, a black data cuff blinks once.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 2

### 2-1 INT. VALE OLD R&D WING - NIGHT

Alarms scream. White smoke crawls under a laboratory door.

Thomas Reid coughs over a metal case. Ava rushes in, one hand pressed to her phone.

AVA: Dad, move. Now.

THOMAS: Take the core first.

He shoves a black drive into her hand. Above them, the nitrogen system counts down.

Across campus, Celeste pounds on the glass wall of the new showroom. Cameras outside catch every tear.

CELESTE: Adrian! I can't breathe!

### 2-2 INT. VALE COMMAND ROOM - CONTINUOUS

Adrian faces two live feeds: Celeste in the showroom, Ava and Thomas in the old lab.

The system speaks coldly.

SYSTEM: Emergency override allows one door.

Board members crowd behind him.

CHAIRMAN: The showroom is live. Investors are watching.

On the lab screen, Ava slams her fist against the glass.

AVA: Adrian! My father is in here!

Celeste coughs into the camera, copper key glinting.

CELESTE: Please. I saved you once. Don't leave me.

Adrian's finger hovers.

AVA: Don't do this. Open Lab B.

Adrian looks at the press feed, then at the board.

ADRIAN: Open the showroom.

He presses CONFIRM.

The showroom door unlocks. The old lab turns red: SEALED.

### 2-3 INT. OLD R&D WING - CONTINUOUS

Ava throws a chair into the lab glass. It cracks but holds. Her palm tears open.

Thomas pulls her toward a maintenance hatch.

THOMAS: Ava, listen. Blackline is yours. Not Vale's. Not his.

AVA: I'm not leaving you.

THOMAS: Live. Make him see the door.

He pushes the drive into her coat, then shoves her into the hatch. The door between them seals.

Through the glass, Thomas drops to one knee. Ava beats the hatch frame until her hand bleeds.

THOMAS: Don't come back!

Nitrogen fog swallows him.

### 2-4 EXT. SHOWROOM ENTRANCE - NIGHT

Adrian carries Celeste out. Reporters surge.

REPORTER: Mr. Vale! What happened in the old wing?

Adrian looks toward the dark R&D building.

ADRIAN: Sabotage. Lock it down.

In the service tunnel, Ava crawls out, half-burned, clutching the black drive. She hears him.

The old wing lights go out.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 3

### 3-1 INT. PRIVATE BURN UNIT - NIGHT

A nurse cuts Ava's warped wedding ring. The metal drops into a tray.

Clink.

Ava wakes to the sound. Her right hand is wrapped in thick white bandages.

MARCUS ROURKE stands beside the bed, watching a wall-mounted television.

On screen, Adrian supports Celeste at a press conference.

ADRIAN (ON TV): Celeste Gray saved my life and Vale Group's future.

Celeste leans into him. Flashbulbs burst.

Ava tries to reach the screen. Her fingers fail.

MARCUS: Thomas Reid died at 2:13 a.m.

Ava's eyes do not move. A tear slides into her hairline.

MARCUS places a pen between her left fingers and a purchase order on the bed.

MARCUS: Vale's debt is cheap tonight.

Ava signs. The signature is crooked.

MARCUS: Again.

She signs the next page. Straighter.

### 3-2 INT. REHAB ROOM / VARIOUS - MONTAGE

Ava squeezes a rubber ball. Drops it.

Signs with her left hand.

A screen lights: BLACKLINE ORIGINATOR - AVA REID.

Port suppliers sign transfer agreements.

Vale board members toast Celeste on television.

Ava's bandages come off. The palm is scarred and tight.

She slips a black signet ring onto her left hand.

### 3-3 INT. GLOBAL ENERGY SUMMIT - FIVE YEARS LATER - NIGHT

Adrian stands under a giant VALE GROUP logo, flawless in a black suit. Celeste sits at the front, still wearing the copper key.

AUCTIONEER: Final license lot. Opening at eight hundred million.

ADRIAN raises a red bid card.

AUCTIONEER: Vale Group, eight hundred million.

A black bid card rises at the back.

The room turns.

AUCTIONEER: Meridian Capital. One point two billion.

The camera does not show the bidder's face. Only a black ring tapping the card.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 4

### 4-1 INT. GLOBAL ENERGY SUMMIT - NIGHT

Black heels cross the aisle.

Ava steps into the light. Adrian's red bid card lowers.

CELESTE: No.

Ava stops opposite Adrian.

AUCTIONEER: Meridian Capital also submits transfer claims on three port battery warehouses.

The screen behind Adrian drops from green to red.

ADRIAN: Who authorized that?

AVA: Your creditors.

Celeste rises, forcing a laugh.

CELESTE: Ava Reid? Adrian's ex-wife? Did Marcus dress you up for charity?

Ava looks at the sapphire pin on Celeste's dress.

AVA: Foundation access pin. Do you know its safety number?

CELESTE: I don't answer servants.

Ava plucks the pin off Celeste's dress and presses it to the scanner on the table.

SCANNER: Access revoked.

The staff removes Celeste's front-row placard. Cameras swing toward her.

Celeste grabs Adrian's arm.

CELESTE: Stop her.

ADRIAN: Security.

Two guards step toward Ava.

Ava places her scarred palm on the license terminal.

TERMINAL: Blackline originator accepted.

Every screen in the hall switches to MERIDIAN CAPITAL - LICENSE AWARDED.

Adrian freezes.

ADRIAN: Ava?

AVA: Five years late.

He reaches for her. She steps closer instead.

AVA: Touch me, and every Vale battery in three ports goes dark.

His hand falls.

Ava leans in.

AVA: Five years ago, you chose a door. Tonight I choose which doors stay open for you.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 5

### 5-1 EXT. PORT WAREHOUSE - DAY

A shipping container hangs above the dock, swaying in the wind. Workers shout below.

The crane screen flashes: MERIDIAN LIEN HOLD.

### 5-2 INT. VALE EXECUTIVE OFFICE - DAY

Adrian listens as alarms and voices pour through his phone.

SUPPLY MANAGER (PHONE): Every battery lock is frozen. The port won't release a single crate.

Celeste snatches the phone.

CELESTE: I'll handle Ava.

ADRIAN: Celeste-

She is already gone.

### 5-3 INT. MERIDIAN PROTOTYPE ROOM - NIGHT

Celeste storms in with hired security. Ava stands behind a glass workbench where a black battery module hums.

CELESTE: You were a wife. Stop pretending you're a queen.

She grabs a hammer and swings at the prototype.

Ava does not move.

The hammer hits. Metal clamps spring out and snap around Celeste's wrist, pinning her hand to the bench.

CELESTE: Get it off!

Ava walks closer.

CELESTE: Adrian will ruin you.

AVA: He already tried.

Celeste fumbles with the copper key and jams it into the clamp lock. The clamp tightens. Bone cracks softly.

CELESTE screams.

On the glass wall, live port footage appears. Workers paste MERIDIAN CLAIM notices on Vale containers.

AVA: My father waited three minutes for a door.

She starts a timer on the bench: 3:00.

CELESTE: You psycho!

AVA: No. Just punctual.

The timer hits zero. Ava presses her scarred palm to the release panel. Celeste collapses, clutching her wrist.

AVA: Tell Adrian the accounts have opened.

Celeste crawls behind a pillar and dials with shaking fingers.

CELESTE: Find me the old safety chief. I want the night Ava burned.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 6

### 6-1 INT. VALE TOWER PRIVATE ELEVATOR - NIGHT

Ava steps into the elevator. Adrian follows and hits a black emergency key.

The elevator stops between floors. Red light strips glow.

AVA: Still locking women in rooms?

ADRIAN: What are you?

AVA: The bill.

The elevator panel speaks.

SYSTEM: Originator palm required.

Ava keeps her hand at her side.

ADRIAN: Open it.

AVA: You won't like what opens.

He reaches for the manual alarm. The elevator drops half a foot. Celeste's security override has trapped them both.

Ava curses under her breath and presses her right hand to the glass.

SYSTEM: Blackline originator - Ava Reid.

Adrian stares at the scarred palm.

ADRIAN: Your hand.

Ava pulls away.

AVA: You locked the wrong door.

The elevator opens. Ava walks out.

### 6-2 INT. ADRIAN'S OFFICE - LATER

Adrian throws old files across his desk. A pale safety chief stands before him.

ADRIAN: The old wing. Full logs.

SAFETY CHIEF: Most were corrupted.

ADRIAN: I did not ask for most.

The man slides over a torn printout: CEO OVERRIDE / COMMAND 7B / OLD LAB SEALED.

Adrian's face drains.

ADRIAN: Who signed it?

SAFETY CHIEF: Sir... it's incomplete.

The safety chief leaves. In the doorway shadow, Celeste watches.

Adrian grips the command number until the paper tears.

Outside, Celeste opens a tablet and deletes a file named 7B_FULL.

The delete freezes.

On her tablet: MERIDIAN REMOTE HOLD.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 7

### 7-1 INT. VALE FOUNDATION STAGE - NIGHT

A transparent rescue door stands center stage. A volunteer engineer waits behind it with an oxygen mask. A countdown reads 00:30.

Celeste smiles at cameras, copper key lifted.

CELESTE: This key once saved Adrian Vale. Tonight it will save thousands more.

Ava sits in the front row.

AVA: Then open it.

The room quiets.

Adrian rises.

ADRIAN: Ava, enough.

AVA: Thirty seconds is generous.

The countdown starts.

Celeste inserts the key. It doesn't turn.

She forces it. The alarm grows louder.

CELESTE: She changed the lock.

AVA: Rescue doors unload pressure before they turn. You knew that, didn't you?

Celeste tries again. Her smile breaks.

00:09.

Adrian steps toward the stage.

ADRIAN: Stop the demonstration.

Ava places her scarred palm on the manual pad. She waits one beat longer.

00:03.

She presses. The door opens. The engineer steps out, breathing hard.

From the back, an older technician limps forward. He places a broken steel key on the podium.

TECHNICIAN: This was in Thomas Reid's hand.

Celeste shakes her head.

CELESTE: He's paid.

Ava looks at Adrian.

AVA: Ask her how she saved you without the real key.

Adrian looks at the cameras, the board, Celeste.

ADRIAN: Cut the livestream.

Ava smiles without warmth.

Backstage, Celeste shoves papers into a shredder. The blades spin, then stop.

The screen on the shredder lights up: MERIDIAN REMOTE HOLD.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 8

### 8-1 INT. EMERGENCY SHAREHOLDER MEETING - NIGHT

An empty chair is pushed to the center of the main table.

A brass plate reads: THOMAS REID.

Celeste sits beside it, pale but powdered. Adrian stands behind his chair. Ava stands at the head of the table.

AVA: Foundation chair Gray will unlock the accident archive she claims she created.

CELESTE: I won't perform for you.

AVA: You performed for cameras for five years.

The terminal rises from the table. Celeste puts her palm on it.

TERMINAL: Access denied.

Whispers spread.

Ava nods. Celeste tries again.

TERMINAL: Access denied.

CELESTE: It's rigged.

AVA: Third time.

Celeste presses harder.

TERMINAL: Safety record signed by proxy. No rescue clearance.

Board members stand, one by one, moving away from her.

Adrian walks to Celeste. He takes the nameplate from before her hands.

CELESTE: Adrian.

He does not answer.

CELESTE: You want me to fall alone?

His hand stops.

CELESTE: I forged a record. You gave the order. You locked the old wing.

The room goes still.

CELESTE: He died behind your door.

Adrian looks at Ava. She does not look away.

Celeste grabs his sleeve.

CELESTE: You are as guilty as I am.

On the back of the nameplate, taped in black ink: COMMAND 7B.

Adrian drops the plate and runs.

Celeste screams after him.

CELESTE: You hear me? You're guilty!

Ava turns Thomas Reid's empty chair toward the door Adrian exited.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 9

### 9-1 INT. OLD R&D WING - NIGHT

Adrian tears down the seal tape.

Dust lifts from the white corridor. Old frost marks stain the walls.

He reaches Lab B. The keypad flickers when he enters 7B.

SYSTEM: Accident replay mode.

ADRIAN: Open the door.

SYSTEM: CEO confirmation required.

He presses CONFIRM.

The hallway lights flash red. On the screen, a five-year-old command appears.

OPEN SHOWROOM. SEAL OLD LAB.

Signature: ADRIAN VALE.

Adrian steps back as if struck.

Then Thomas Reid's voice cracks through the speaker.

THOMAS (RECORDING): Ava, don't come back. Take the core. Live.

Adrian runs to the lab door and grabs a fire axe. He swings at the glass.

The glass does not break.

SYSTEM: Oxygen critical.

He swings again. Blood smears from his palms onto the axe handle.

THOMAS (RECORDING): Adrian chose wrong. Don't let his choice bury you too.

Adrian drops the axe. His knees hit the floor.

Behind the glass, on the scorched floor, a half-burned letter A is scratched into the dust.

Adrian presses his bloody hand to the glass. It is still cold.

He pulls off his CEO signet ring and forces it into the crack under the door.

He calls Ava.

ADRIAN: I found the room.

Silence.

ADRIAN: Tell me what you want me to lose.

CUT TO BLACK.

## Episode 10

### 10-1 INT. FOUNDER MEMORIAL HALL - DAY

Thomas Reid's name glows on a glass memorial wall.

Shareholders, cameras, and board members fill the hall. Celeste sits in the back row between two guards. Marcus stands near Ava.

The doors open.

Adrian enters in a wrinkled white shirt stained with old blood. He carries a black case.

He walks to Thomas Reid's empty chair and places his CEO badge on it.

Then the Vale family voting trust.

Then the chairman authorization.

The voting screen changes: ADRIAN VALE - REMOVED.

Gasps ripple through the hall.

ADRIAN: I signed Command 7B.

Celeste's face twists.

ADRIAN: I opened the showroom. I sealed the old lab. Thomas Reid died behind my order.

He turns to Ava.

ADRIAN: I chose the wrong door.

Ava's face does not move.

ADRIAN: Take Vale. Take my name off every wall. Leave me only what I owe.

The main table staff removes his nameplate. The space in front of him goes blank.

Ava points to a transparent safety chamber beside the memorial wall.

AVA: Stand inside.

Murmurs explode. Marcus leans close.

MARCUS: Ava.

She does not look at him.

Adrian walks into the chamber. The door seals.

Red light fills the glass.

SYSTEM: Purge simulation armed.

Adrian presses his bleeding palm to the glass, opposite Ava's scarred hand.

ADRIAN: Choose what I deserve.

The countdown begins.

THREE.

TWO.

Marcus lowers his voice.

MARCUS: If he dies in there, you inherit a corpse, not a company.

Ava's finger rests on the red button.

ONE.

She presses.

The alarm cuts to silence.

BLACK.
