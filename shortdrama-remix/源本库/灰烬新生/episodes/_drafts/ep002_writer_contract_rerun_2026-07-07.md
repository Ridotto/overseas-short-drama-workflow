# Episode 2: The Daughter They Chose

## Scene 1

**INT. ASHFORD LAW OFFICE - PRIVATE ROOM - DAY**

The telephone rings. Mr. Graves listens, then sets the receiver down.

**MR. GRAVES:** "The funds cleared."

Evelyn still has the pen marks on her fingers from the contract.

**EVELYN:** "All of it?"

**MR. GRAVES:** "Enough to pull Whitmore out of default."

For a second, her shoulders give way.

**EVELYN:** "Then that's done."

Mr. Graves slides the transfer slip toward her.

**MR. GRAVES:** "The sender line can still be corrected."

Evelyn looks at the paper. Then at Adrian Ashford's signature drying above hers.

**MR. GRAVES:** "Do you want your name on it?"

She hears rain in her head before she answers.

**EVELYN:** "No."

**MR. GRAVES:** "Miss Whitmore—"

**EVELYN:** "If it comes from me, they'll spit it back."

She folds the slip once.

**EVELYN:** "Just let it save them."

## Scene 2

**EXT. WHITMORE DEPARTMENT STORE - FRONT STEPS - DAY**

Suppliers crowd the entrance with invoices. Two reporters shout over each other. A florist's crate has been kicked over; white carnations lie smashed into the wet pavement.

Victor stands at the top step like anger can still hold the building up. Nathan is halfway down, arguing with a supplier. Margaret keeps one arm around Celeste, as if she might faint from the noise.

Across the street, Evelyn gets out of the cab with a small suitcase and stops under the awning.

The side door bangs open.

**ASSISTANT:** "Mr. Whitmore! The Ashford wire came through."

Everything stills.

**VICTOR:** "Say that again."

**ASSISTANT:** "We're covered. Payroll, suppliers, the bank note. All of it."

Nathan grabs the telegram from his hand.

**NATHAN:** "Who got it done?"

The assistant scans the line, breath shaking.

**ASSISTANT:** "It says... your daughter asked Ashford for emergency bridge funds."

Every head turns.

Not to the street.

To Celeste.

She blinks, like even she didn't expect it this fast.

Then her mouth trembles.

**CELESTE:** "I just... I couldn't watch us lose everything."

Margaret breaks first. She pulls Celeste in and kisses her hair.

**MARGARET:** "My sweet girl."

Victor's whole face changes. Softer than Evelyn has seen in years.

**VICTOR:** "You saved Whitmore."

Nathan turns toward the reporters, one hand already out like a wall.

**NATHAN:** "That's enough. You got your story."

**REPORTER:** "Miss Celeste, did Ashford ask for collateral?"

Celeste lowers her eyes.

**CELESTE:** "I didn't do it for attention."

The cameras love her for that.

Under the awning, Evelyn takes one step forward.

**EVELYN:** "Dad—"

Nathan doesn't even look.

**NATHAN:** "Not now, Evelyn."

## Scene 3

**EXT. WHITMORE DEPARTMENT STORE - CONTINUOUS**

Evelyn's fingers tighten around the handle of her suitcase.

She could cross now. Hold up the contract. Say the one ugly truth out loud.

Instead she sees it again:

Rain.

Rose on the ground.

Victor's hand across her face.

And Celeste crying into Margaret's coat while everyone chose her.

Back on the steps, Victor puts his hand on Celeste's shoulder and guides her inside first.

**VICTOR:** "Come in. You've done enough."

Margaret turns with her, still holding Celeste close.

**MARGARET:** "Get her some water."

Nathan pulls the door open for them.

No one opens it for Evelyn.

Celeste glances back once.

Only once.

Her eyes find Evelyn across the street.

**CELESTE** (softly, almost kind): "You should go home."

Evelyn lets out one small laugh that dies before it becomes a sound.

The store doors close on Celeste.

Evelyn stays where she is, outside the family she just bought another day for.

Above the block, the church clock begins to strike.

Nine hours until the Ashford car comes for the bride.
