# Episode 3: Grandmother's Brooch

> fresh first draft by narrowed writer input contract  
> purpose: `/episode`-only validation, no `/dialogue-polish`

## Scene 1

**INT. WHITMORE HOUSE - FRONT HALL - AFTERNOON**

Evelyn steps in with the same small suitcase. The hall is drowning in flowers.

Not for her.

Pink roses line the table under Grandmother Rose's portrait. A card in Nathan's hand reads: **For Celeste. For saving Whitmore.**

Victor looks up first.

**VICTOR:** "Why are you back?"

Evelyn keeps both hands on the suitcase handle.

**EVELYN:** "I came for one thing."

No one asks her to sit.

**EVELYN:** "Grandmother's brooch."

Margaret stills. Nathan closes the card. Cole turns all the way around.

**EVELYN:** "She told me once... when I was older, it would be mine."

Victor's face hardens at once.

**VICTOR:** "Rose is dead because of you."

The room goes flat.

**VICTOR:** "You don't touch what she left behind."

## Scene 2

**INT. WHITMORE PARLOR - MOMENTS LATER**

The brooch box sits open on the tea table.

Inside, the ruby rose catches the light like a drop of blood.

Celeste sits beside Margaret with a blanket over her knees, still wearing the soft, shaken look from the store steps.

Evelyn moves before she thinks.

Cole steps in front of the table.

**COLE:** "Don't."

Evelyn stops.

**NATHAN:** "After what happened today, this shouldn't even be a question."

**EVELYN:** "Nathan—"

**NATHAN:** "Celeste saved the store."

He says it like a fact already carved in stone.

**NATHAN:** "If anyone wears Grandmother's rose tonight, it's her."

Evelyn looks at Celeste.

**EVELYN:** "The money wasn't—"

Celeste draws back before the sentence can finish.

**CELESTE:** "Please. Not like this."

She shakes her head, voice thin and soft.

**CELESTE:** "I don't want her hurting herself over me again."

That does it.

Margaret reaches for Celeste's hand.

**MARGARET:** "You hear her? Even now she's thinking about you."

Victor lifts the brooch from the box.

For half a second, Evelyn thinks he might put it back.

He doesn't.

He pins it to Celeste's dress himself.

**VICTOR:** "This house knows who stood by it."

## Scene 3

**INT. WHITMORE PARLOR - CONTINUOUS**

The ruby rose gleams against Celeste's pale dress.

On her, it looks wrong.

Evelyn takes one breath. Then another.

**EVELYN:** "Father, the funds came from—"

Rain slams across her memory.

Rose falling.

Her own voice saying no, over and over, while no one looked at her.

The rest of the sentence dies in her throat.

Victor closes the empty box and presses it into her hands.

**VICTOR:** "Take that."

Evelyn looks down.

Blue velvet.

No brooch.

**VICTOR:** "It suits you better. Empty."

Celeste lowers her eyes, fingertips brushing the rose at her collar like she's afraid to keep it.

Like she's already keeping it.

The grandfather clock begins to strike.

Eight hours until the Ashford family comes for the bride.
