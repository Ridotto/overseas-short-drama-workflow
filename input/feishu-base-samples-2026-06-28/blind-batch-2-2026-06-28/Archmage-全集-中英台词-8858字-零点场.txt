一、基本信息

1. 作品名称
《金阳战姬 · Golden Sun Warrior》
对标剧：《绝世红颜》《我凭一把屠刀问鼎江湖》

2. 题材
西方奇幻/魔法史诗/血脉觉醒/寻亲认亲/家族恩怨/宿命对决

3. 卖点
屠龙少女寻亲、黄金太阳神血脉、家族打脸反转、血脉宿命相克、暗夜血族复仇、高能卡点爆燃

二、一句话梗概

屠龙强者劳拉自幼被弃，被大魔法师梅林收养并觉醒黄金太阳神血脉，下山寻亲时卷入凛冬城两大世仇纷争，凭实力逆转战局、认回家人，遭遇血脉克星仍坚守守护，最终揭穿冒牌师父阴谋，迎来禁术生死决战。
Dragon slayer Laura was abandoned as a child, then raised by the Archmage Merlin and awakened the Golden Sun God bloodline. When she descends the mountain to find her birth family, she gets pulled into Winterhold’s feud between two rival houses. With sheer power, she turns the battle around, reunites with her family, stands firm even against the one bloodline born to counter hers, and finally exposes a fake master’s scheme, triggering a forbidden life-or-death duel
三、剧情大纲

（1集）大魔法师梅林弟子劳拉斩杀黑龙，晋升大魔法师，携师父所赠家族徽章，下山寻找亲生父母。

（2–9集）劳拉救下史塔克家族少爷瑞卡，随其抵达凛冬城。史塔克夫妇认出徽章，知晓劳拉是当年因未觉醒魔法被弃的女儿，却因族规不敢相认。劳拉在水晶球测试中展露黄金太阳神血脉，震惊全城。兰斯特家族挑衅，戈尔使用绝招陨星坠欲灭史塔克众人，劳拉挺身而出，一拳轰碎陨石化作漫天玫瑰花瓣，气场炸裂，喝止恶行（1卡）

（10–20集）劳拉接连击败戈尔、加雷斯、雷蒙德，威震凛冬。兰斯特搬出终极靠山——暗夜冥龙血脉传人卢西恩，其血脉天生克制太阳神血脉，史塔克众人陷入绝境。卢西恩威压全场，卢西恩限时劝退，劳拉无视生死威胁，眼神坚定直言“我不走”，直面宿命克星，引爆全场（2卡）

（21–30集）卢西恩实为梅林好友，出手废掉兰斯特众人修为，劳拉正式认亲团聚。兰斯特余孽布莱尔怀恨，投靠暗夜血族莫拉克，修炼绝欲血咒。劳拉师兄艾文下山，告知师父曾经的师弟莫拉克已经出现。二人前往调查，救下索菲亚，得知冒牌梅林将举办英雄宴会。劳拉赴宴揭穿冒牌者正是布莱尔，二人定下生死决斗， 冒牌身份曝光，布莱尔禁术大成杀气暴涨，生死对决一触即发，高能季终卡点（3卡）
(Ep. 1) Laura, disciple of the Archmage Merlin, slays a black dragon and rises to Grand Mage herself. Carrying the family crest Merlin gave her, she leaves the mountain to find her birth parents.
(Eps. 2-9) Laura saves Rikar, the young heir of the Stark family, and follows him to Winterhold. Toren and Leanna Stark recognize her crest and realize she is the daughter they abandoned years ago for failing to awaken magic, but clan rules stop them from claiming her. During a crystal test, Laura reveals the Golden Sun God bloodline and shocks the entire city. When the Lanster family provokes the Starks and Gore casts his ultimate spell, Meteorfall, to wipe them out, Laura steps in, shatters the meteor with one punch, turns it into a sky of rose petals, and shuts down the attack in a spectacular power reveal. (Card 1)
(Eps. 10-20) Laura defeats Gore, Gareth, and Raymond one after another, shaking all of Winterhold. The Lansters bring out their ultimate backer, Lucien, heir to the Night Nether Dragon bloodline, a bloodline born to counter the Golden Sun God bloodline. The Starks are pushed into a dead end. Lucien’s pressure dominates the arena and he gives Laura a deadline to leave, but she ignores the threat of death, looks him straight in the eye, and says, “I’m not leaving,” facing her fated counter head-on and igniting the crowd. (Card 2)
(Eps. 21-30) Lucien is revealed to be Merlin’s friend. He strips the Lansters of their magic, and Laura finally reunites with her family. Blair, the surviving Lanster, swears revenge and joins Morak, a dark vampire, to train in the Desire-Severing Blood Curse. Laura’s senior brother Evan descends the mountain and tells her that Morak, Merlin’s former junior disciple, has appeared. The two investigate, save Sophia, and learn that a fake Merlin is hosting a hero banquet. Laura attends, exposes the impostor as Blair, and agrees to a life-or-death duel. With his fake identity revealed and his forbidden art complete, Blair’s killing intent explodes, setting up a high-stakes season finale clash. (Card 3)
