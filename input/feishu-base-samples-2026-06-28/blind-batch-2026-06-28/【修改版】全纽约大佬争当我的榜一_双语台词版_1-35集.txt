梗概：
为了拯救病重的弟弟，布鲁克林贫民窟女孩Ava在Twitch开启了直播。原本只想赚点微薄的医药费，却因绝美的素颜和破碎感，意外引发了华尔街巨鳄Liam、好莱坞顶流Chase、黑道教父Dante三大巨头的“榜一争夺战”。一夜之间，打赏破亿，震撼全网。平台一姐Chloe因嫉妒联合黑心前男友Eric，造谣Ava是出卖身体的“捞女”，并在全网直播的年度盛典上试图毁掉她。面对绝境，三大佬从线上降临现实，联手封杀反派、豪掷千金。Ava在顶级盛宠中完成蜕变，渣男恶女身败名裂，小主播最终成了整个纽约顶层圈层捧在手心的唯一女王。

To save her sick younger brother, Brooklyn slum girl Ava starts streaming on Twitch, hoping to earn a little money for his medical bills. But her raw beauty and fragile charm draw the attention of three powerful men: Wall Street tycoon Liam, Hollywood superstar Chase, and mafia godfather Dante. Overnight, their battle to become her top donor sends her tips past hundreds of millions and shocks the internet.

Jealous of Ava’s rise, former platform queen Chloe teams up with Ava’s greedy ex-boyfriend Eric to frame her as a gold-digging escort and destroy her at a live-streamed annual gala. But at Ava’s darkest moment, the three men step out of the screen and into reality, crushing her enemies and protecting her at any cost. In their overwhelming devotion, Ava rises from a bullied little streamer to the one woman New York’s most powerful men all cherish.

故事大纲：
Ava因弟弟的医药费被停，走投无路之下与黑心公会老板Eric签下苛刻合同，成为Twitch主播。第一天开播，她被平台一姐Chloe的粉丝网暴嘲笑，却意外吸引了刚刚结束财阀会议的Liam。Liam随手刷出的“世纪流星雨”特效打破平台纪录，不仅帮Ava解围，更引来了好莱坞巨星Chase和黑帮教父Dante的注意。三人互不相让，在直播间展开疯狂的财富厮杀，直接将Ava推上全球热搜。
眼红的Eric企图用合同逼Ava线下陪酒，Chloe则在网络上大肆造谣Ava的钱全靠“卖”换来。Ava在直播间随口感叹了一句“虚拟城堡真美”，第二天，Liam直接将长岛价值上亿的真实城堡地契送到了她合租屋的门前；Chase更是在时代广场买下所有大屏为她澄清；而Dante则派人在暗巷里给了Eric血的教训。
年度直播盛典上，Chloe和Eric布下死局，企图在全网面前曝光Ava的“伪善面目”，甚至暗中停掉了Ava弟弟医院的呼吸机。在Ava最崩溃、身败名裂的边缘（卡点），三架私人直升机空降现场，三大顶级大佬同时现身。
从这一刻起，局势彻底反转。Liam买下整个直播平台，让Eric背上百亿债务；Chase动用全球媒体封杀Chloe；Dante则让黑手党接管了医院，给了弟弟最好的治疗。得罪Ava的人全部生不如死，而Ava也在三人的极致宠爱下，摆脱了自卑，真正接纳了自己，成为了全纽约最耀眼、最无人敢惹的存在。

After Ava’s younger brother’s medical care is cut off, she is pushed into a corner and signs a brutal contract with Eric, a shady agency boss, becoming a Twitch streamer under his control. On her very first stream, Chloe’s fans flood in to mock and bully her. But unexpectedly, Ava catches the attention of Liam, who has just walked out of a high-stakes financial meeting.

With a casual gift of the “Century Meteor Shower” effect, Liam breaks the platform record, saves Ava from humiliation, and draws the attention of Hollywood superstar Chase and mafia godfather Dante. None of the three men is willing to back down. Their insane spending war in Ava’s livestream sends her straight to the top of the global trending list.

Driven by greed, Eric tries to use Ava’s contract to force her into offline drinking gigs, while Chloe spreads rumors online that Ava earns her money by “selling herself.” When Ava casually mentions during a stream that the virtual castle effect is beautiful, Liam sends the deed to a real castle on Long Island, worth over a hundred million dollars, to the door of her shared apartment the very next day. Chase buys out every screen in Times Square to clear her name. Dante sends his men to teach Eric a bloody lesson in a dark alley.

At the annual streaming gala, Chloe and Eric set a deadly trap. They try to expose Ava’s so-called “fake innocent act” in front of the entire internet, and even secretly have her brother’s ventilator shut off at the hospital. Just as Ava is pushed to the edge of a complete breakdown and public ruin, three private helicopters descend on the venue, and the three most powerful men appear at the same time.

From that moment on, everything turns around. Liam buys the entire streaming platform and buries Eric under billions in debt. Chase uses his global media power to blacklist Chloe everywhere. Dante has the mafia take over the hospital and gives Ava’s brother the best treatment possible. Everyone who hurt Ava is left worse than dead, while Ava, showered in the three men’s obsessive devotion, finally breaks free from her insecurity, learns to accept herself, and becomes the most dazzling, untouchable woman in all of New York.
