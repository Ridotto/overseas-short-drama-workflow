英文名：Alpha’s Fated Pregnant Mate
剧本信息
一、一句话概括
阿尔法狼人杰克逊意外与少女米娅缔结命定伴侣契约，两人历经陷害、误会与生死考验，最终携手对抗敌对狼族，守护孩子，彼此相爱。
Jackson Hale, an Alpha werewolf, accidentally forms a fated mate bond with Mia, a young student. After traps, misunderstandings, and life-or-death trials, they join forces against a rival wolf clan, protect their child, and finally fall in love.

二、故事梗概
月圆之夜，狼人阿尔法杰克逊受家族诅咒折磨，母亲安排为他挑选了数十名处女伴侣，但都不是他的命定之人。恰逢兼职宠物陪玩的少女米娅误入别墅，两人意外结合并定下狼人伴侣印记。米娅事后慌忙逃离，一个月后查出怀孕，流言也随之传开。
杰克逊在学校重逢米娅，起初误会她贪图钱财，得知真相后满心愧疚，开始全力护着她。米娅的父亲雷格嗜赌欠了一大笔外债，为还清赌债，他狠心把米娅卖到地下赌场。赌场老板沃恩和手下肆意欺负米娅，她身受重伤，肚子里的狼族孩子也险些保不住。杰克逊及时化狼赶来救下她，才得知米娅怀了自己的血脉。
敌对贝塔狼族洛德家族首领布雷，一直觊觎杰克逊的阿尔法狼王位置，还暗中派医生科勒暗算母子，杰克逊识破阴谋并斩杀敌人，向洛德首领布雷下了生死战书。布雷与妹妹卡瑞娜不甘落败，接连设计挑拨离间：卡瑞娜在校园当众诋毁米娅，雷格又被收买伪造流产单据，让两人产生巨大误会，米娅伤心选择离开。
月圆之夜生死大战来临，杰克逊受诅咒压制实力，对战布雷时节节败退陷入绝境。米娅得知全部真相后匆忙赶去相助，途中被卡瑞娜暗算，危急之下独自生下孩子。亲子间强大的血脉共鸣唤醒杰克逊全部力量，他火速赶到米娅身边，二人解除所有误会，并肩直面最终危机。

On the night of the full moon, Alpha werewolf Jackson is tortured by his family curse. His mother arranges dozens of virgin mates for him, but none of them are his fated mate. By chance, Mia, a young student working part-time as a pet sitter, enters the villa by mistake. The two accidentally bond and leave behind a werewolf mate mark. Mia panics and runs away. One month later, she finds out she is pregnant, and rumors begin to spread.

Jackson meets Mia again at school. At first, he mistakes her for someone after his money, but after learning the truth, he is filled with guilt and starts protecting her with everything he has. Mia’s father, Reg, is a gambling addict buried in debt. To pay it off, he cruelly sells Mia to an underground casino. Casino owner Vaughn and his men abuse Mia, leaving her badly injured and nearly costing her the werewolf child in her womb. Jackson arrives in wolf form just in time to save her, and only then discovers that Mia is carrying his child.

Blade Lord, leader of the rival Beta wolf clan, has long been after Jackson’s position as Alpha Wolf King. He secretly sends Dr. Koller to harm Mia and the baby. Jackson uncovers the plot, kills the enemy, and challenges Blade Lord to a death duel. Unwilling to lose, Blade and his sister Karina keep setting traps to drive Jackson and Mia apart. Karina publicly slanders Mia at school, while Reg is bribed into forging abortion records, creating a huge misunderstanding between them. Heartbroken, Mia chooses to leave.

When the full-moon death duel arrives, Jackson’s power is suppressed by the curse. He falls into a deadly disadvantage against Blade. After Mia learns the whole truth, she rushes to help him, only to be ambushed by Karina on the way. In the crisis, she gives birth alone. The powerful bloodline bond between father and child awakens Jackson’s full strength. He races to Mia’s side, clears up every misunderstanding with her, and together they face the final crisis.

三、核心爽点
1、宿命强制爱：暴躁狼人阿尔法男主在月圆失控时，感应到女主是命定伴侣，强行和女主一夜情缔结狼人契约，极致占有欲与宿命感拉满，符合海外观众对强制爱的情感向爽感。
2、霸总认亲：男主从误会女主不爱自己，前期虐感拉扯，后得知女主怀孕的是自己的狼人孩子血脉，通过血脉认亲，终于和女主解除误会相爱。
